# Beach Picnic Notes

Lessons and reminders for the next beach picnic.

## For next time

- Look after your belongings. People always lose stuff on the beach, so keep an eye on bags, phones and valuables.
- Clear up litter as you go, don't leave it to everyone else. This is imperative.
- Try to decide on the picnic location before the day. We can always walk over to the volleyball if needed.
- Set up far enough away from the volleyball so people don't get hit. Equally, the volleyball crew should not "play" near the picnic.
