## 📋 How to Join

1. **Mark yourself as going on the Meetup event** so Andre knows numbers: https://www.meetup.com/board-games-bcn/events/314741119
2. **(Optional) Join the Keep Calm pre-meet at 19:00** for a drink with the crew before we head in together. Exact spot will be shared in the **Social WhatsApp group on the day**.
3. **Or turn up at the venue** at 20:00 (or any time during the evening, the social runs until 23:30, sometimes later!)
4. **Pay on arrival** - 5€ for Keep Calm members, drink included. Mention Keep Calm at the door.
5. **No experience needed** - games are taught on the night, come solo or bring friends

---

## 🗓️ On the Night

The venue has **three areas** to choose from:
- 🎲 **Main board games room** - tables, drinks, and the BGB games library
- 🎤 **Karaoke lounge** - for when you need a break from Catan
- 🕹️ **Retro Super Nintendo room** - old-school gaming corner

Rough schedule:
- **19:00 to 20:00** - 🍻 **Keep Calm pre-meet** (optional) - meet the crew nearby for a drink so we can all head in together. Exact spot shared in the Social WhatsApp group on the day.
- **20:00** - doors open at Terraza del Ángel, grab a drink, settle in
- **20:15 onwards** - games start, Andre and the Board Games BCN crew help pair people up and teach games. Karaoke and Nintendo room run alongside.
- **23:30** - official wrap, though the night often runs a bit later

🎁 **Bonus:** attending this Saturday event gets you **free entry to Board Games BCN's Sunday event** too, if you fancy another round.

## 📍 Getting There

**Terraza del Ángel** is a rooftop terrace on Plaça de l'Àngel, right next to the Jaume I metro stop in the Gothic Quarter.

- Metro: **L4 (Yellow) - Jaume I** (less than 1 minute walk)
- Address: Plaça de l'Àngel 2, 08002 Barcelona
- It's a rooftop, so head inside and go up

## ℹ️ Good to Know

- **All levels welcome** - from never-played-a-board-game to Catan world champion
- **Come solo or bring friends** - easy to join a table, Andre helps match people to games
- **Languages** - international crowd, mostly English and Spanish (plus others)
- **Games provided** - no need to bring your own, Board Games BCN has a big library
- **Bring:** just yourself and 5€ (cash is safest, ask Andre on the night if card is accepted)
- **Photos likely** - by attending you accept being photographed or filmed for Board Games BCN's Instagram ([@boardgamesbcn](https://www.instagram.com/boardgamesbcn/)) and our channels. See the data protection notice below.
