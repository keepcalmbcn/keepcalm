# 🎲 Board Games - FAQ

## Where is it?
**Terraza del Ángel**, a rooftop terrace at **Plaça de l'Àngel 2**, right next to the **Jaume I** metro stop in the Gothic Quarter.

[Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/uxcq6dQZmxy3LnGf9)

## When is the next one?
**Saturday 23rd May 2026, 20:00 to 23:30** (sometimes runs later). Drop in any time during the evening. The full Board Games BCN event title is **"Terrace Board Game + Karaoke"**.

## Is there a Keep Calm pre-meet?
**Yes - 19:00 to 20:00**, optional. The plan is to meet up nearby for a drink so we can all head into the venue together. The exact spot will be shared in the **Social WhatsApp group on the day**. If you'd rather skip the pre-meet, just turn up at Terraza del Ángel from 20:00.

## What's at the venue?
Three areas to choose from:
- 🎲 **Main board games room** with the BGB games library
- 🎤 **Karaoke lounge**
- 🕹️ **Retro Super Nintendo room**

So if you fancy a break from games there's plenty else going on.

## What language is spoken?
**English and Spanish mostly**, plus other languages. It's an international crowd, very easy to join in whatever your level.

## Is there a bonus for going?
Yes - attending this Saturday event gets you **free entry to Board Games BCN's Sunday event** too.

## How much does it cost?
- **5€ Keep Calm Members price** (drink included)
- Normally 10€, so it's half price for our community

Mention Keep Calm at the door to get the members rate.

## How do I sign up?
**Mark yourself as going on the Board Games BCN Meetup event** so Andre knows numbers:
https://www.meetup.com/board-games-bcn/events/314741119

No DMs needed, just RSVP on Meetup and turn up.

## Do I need to know how to play board games?
**No.** All levels welcome. Andre and the Board Games BCN crew teach games on the night and help pair people up to tables.

## Do I need to bring a game?
No, Board Games BCN brings a big library of games. Just bring yourself.

## Can I come solo?
**Absolutely** - this is one of the easiest socials to come to on your own. You'll be paired up with a table and meet new people through the games.

## Can I bring a +1?
Yes, bring whoever you like. They'll just pay the standard entry on the door (or the members price if they're in the Keep Calm community).

## What if I'm running late?
No problem, drop in any time before 23:30. Just RSVP on Meetup so Andre has a rough headcount.

## Who runs it?
The night is hosted by **Andre** and the **[Board Games BCN](https://www.meetup.com/board-games-bcn/)** team. This is **their event**, the entry fee goes to them as the organiser. **Alona** coordinates with Andre on the Keep Calm side and handles the announcement. We're sharing it with the Keep Calm community because it's a great night out and they've offered a members discount.

## Will there be more board games nights?
The plan is to run one roughly **every couple of months**. Watch the **Social WhatsApp group** for announcements.

## How do I join the Social WhatsApp group?
Links are available at [keepcalm.fit](https://keepcalm.fit/).
