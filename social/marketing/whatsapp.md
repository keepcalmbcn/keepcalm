# WhatsApp Post - Keep Calm Social Events

---

🎳🎲🌊 *New to Barcelona? Come hang out with us!*

*Keep Calm Social* is part of Keep Calm - a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona. We do all the fun stuff: bowling, beach days, board games, dinners, day trips, and whatever else people fancy.

You don't need to play a sport to join. Just show up and meet people 😊

It's completely free to be part of the community and everything is volunteer-run. No agenda, no pressure - just a good excuse to get out and make friends in a new city.

👉 Find us at *keepcalm.fit* or search *Keep Calm Barcelona* on Instagram and Facebook.

Come say hi! 🙌

📋 By taking part you agree to our policies (safety, data, conduct, 18+): https://keepcalm.fit/policies
