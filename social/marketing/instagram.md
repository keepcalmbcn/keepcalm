Life in a new city is better with good people around you 🌞

Keep Calm Social is where we do everything outside of sport - bowling nights, beach days, board game evenings, dinners, day trips, markets, and whatever else the group comes up with. You don't need to play padel or volleyball to join us. If you want to get out, meet people, and actually enjoy Barcelona, you're in the right place.

It's free, volunteer-run, and has been bringing English speakers together in Barcelona for years. New to the city or just looking to expand your circle? Come along 🙌
