# Media Guide - Keep Calm Social Events

Photos and visuals that work well for promoting social events:

## Ideal Shot Types

- **Group candids** - people laughing, chatting, mid-activity. Candid beats posed every time.
- **Bowling** - action shots of someone bowling, group reactions, scoreboard moments
- **Board games / pub quizzes** - people leaning in around a table, game pieces close-up, winning moments
- **Pool and table tennis** - mid-play action, competitive faces, doubles pairs
- **Beach days** - group shots by the sea, picnic setups, people in the water
- **Dinners and drinks** - table full of food, raised glasses, good lighting
- **Markets and festivals** - colourful stalls, people exploring together, food and finds
- **Day trips** - landmark backdrops, travel moments, scenic group shots

## What Works Best

- Natural light where possible
- Mix of wide group shots and tighter social moments
- Diverse groups that reflect the community
- Smiles and genuine reactions over stiff poses
- Locations that say "Barcelona" (sea, architecture, outdoor terraces)

## What to Avoid

- Empty venues before people arrive
- Blurry or dark indoor shots
- Photos where people look uncomfortable or staged
- Anything that needs a face blur (always ask permission before posting)
