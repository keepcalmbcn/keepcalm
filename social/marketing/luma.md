# Luma Event Description - Keep Calm Social

---

**Keep Calm Social - Get Out and Meet People in Barcelona**

A casual social event for anyone who wants to meet new people and enjoy Barcelona with a friendly crowd: bowling, a beach day, board games, a group dinner, or a day trip. Open to everyone, not just the sporty crowd.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Ad hoc, announced per event
- 📍 Varies, see event details
- 💶 Free to join the community, individual events may have their own cost
- 👥 Everyone welcome, 18+

**How to join**

RSVP here, then find our WhatsApp groups and full schedule at keepcalm.fit/luma.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
