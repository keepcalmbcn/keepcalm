# Meetup.com Event Description - Keep Calm Social

---

**Keep Calm Social - Get Out and Meet People in Barcelona**

This is a casual social event for anyone who wants to meet new people and enjoy Barcelona with a friendly crowd. Whether it's bowling, a beach day, board games, a group dinner, or a day trip - we pick something fun and just go do it.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona. We've been running sports and social events for years, entirely organised by volunteers. The social group is open to everyone - you don't need to be into sport to come along.

*What to expect:* A relaxed, welcoming group of people. Some will be new to the city, some have been here for years. The atmosphere is friendly and there's no pressure to be social in any particular way - just show up and see how it goes.

*Cost:* Free to join the community. Individual events may have their own costs (e.g. bowling, entry fees) which will be listed on the event.

*How to join:* RSVP here and check the event details. For regular updates and more events, find our WhatsApp groups and full schedule at keepcalm.fit/meetup.

Everyone is welcome. See you there!
