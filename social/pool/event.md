## 🎱 How It Works

1. **Mark yourself as going in the WhatsApp event** - this is required so we have a headcount
2. **Turn up any time between 17:00 and 20:00** - there is no fixed start, just drop in when you can
3. **Grab a table when one frees up** - Billar 8 is a busy spot, so you may need to wait a little
4. **Grab a drink and play as tables open up** - no lists, no DMs, no pre-booking needed

---

## 🗓️ On the Night

- **17:00 - 20:00** - venue is open, first arrivals can grab a table drop in whenever suits you and play as tables free up
- **19:00 onwards ** - if you fancy a second event, [Keep Calm Board Games](../boardgames/README.md) or head out for drinks?

## 📍 Getting There

**Billar 8 Pool** is near **Plaça d'Espanya**, so it is easy to reach by metro.

- Metro: **L1 (red)** and **L3 (green)** - *Espanya* stop
- From the metro it is a short walk - use the Google Maps link above for the exact spot

---

## ℹ️ Good to Know

- **First one ever:** this is a trial run for Keep Calm Pool. If it goes well, we may make it a regular and explore other venues
- **Busy venue:** Billar 8 Pool is one of the busiest pool spots in Barcelona, so tables may not be free immediately - grab a drink and a table will free up before long
- **The tables:** the venue is fairly new, and a few tables sit close to walls - fine for a social game, just not ideal for ambitious trick shots
- **All levels welcome:** total beginners absolutely included, this is about meeting people and having fun
- **Bring:** just yourself and some cash or card for table time and drinks
- **Late arrivals:** no problem, it is a drop-in window - just bear in mind things wind down by 20:00
- **Photos likely** - see the data protection notice below
