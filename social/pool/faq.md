## When is the next one?
See the current [event.md](event.md) - date, time, and venue change each time depending on who's organising.

## How much does it cost?
**Pay as you go.** The venue charges for table time, which is split between the players on the table. Drinks are paid for at the venue too. Bring some cash or card.

## How do I join in?
1. **Mark yourself as going in the WhatsApp event** - this is required so we have a headcount.
2. **Turn up any time in the drop-in window** - no fixed start, just drop in when you can.

No DMs or pre-booking needed.

## Do I need to book a table?
No. Tables are first come at the venue - just grab one when it is free.

## What if there are no free tables when I arrive?
Popular venues can get busy, so this can happen. Grab a drink, chat to the other Keep Calm people around, and a table will free up before long.

## How many spots are there?
No cap - the more the merrier. A bigger group simply means we take more tables.

## Do I need to be good at pool?
Not at all. All skill levels are welcome, total beginners included. This is a social event first and foremost.

## Can I bring a +1?
Yes, bring whoever you like. Just ask them to mark themselves as going in the WhatsApp event so the headcount stays accurate.

## What if I can't make it after all?
No problem - there is nothing to cancel since nothing is pre-booked. Just don't worry about it.

## Who organises this?
It varies - whoever picks it up each time chooses the venue and runs the night. See [event.md](event.md) for who's organising the next one, and [README.md](README.md) for who's organised past ones.

## Will there be more pool nights?
Yes - pool is a regular Keep Calm fixture, with the venue changing depending on who's organising. Watch the **Social WhatsApp group** for announcements.

Links are available at [keepcalm.fit](https://keepcalm.fit/).
