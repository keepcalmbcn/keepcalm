# 🎱 Pool - FAQ

## Where is it?
**Billar 8 Pool**, near **Plaça d'Espanya** in Barcelona. Take the metro to *Espanya* on **L1 (red)** or **L3 (green)** - it is a short walk from there.

[Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/6oiUp48Qwfqtvy6t6)

## When is the next one?
**Saturday 23rd May 2026.** It is a drop-in window, so turn up any time between **17:00 and 20:00**.

## How much does it cost?
**Pay as you go.** The venue charges for table time, which is split between the players on the table. Drinks are paid for at the venue too. Bring some cash or card.

## How do I join in?
1. **Mark yourself as going in the WhatsApp event** - this is required so we have a headcount.
2. **Turn up any time between 17:00 and 20:00** - no fixed start, just drop in when you can.

No DMs or pre-booking needed.

## Do I need to book a table?
No. Tables are first come at the venue - just grab one when it is free.

## What if there are no free tables when I arrive?
Billar 8 Pool is one of the busiest pool spots in Barcelona, so this can happen. Grab a drink, chat to the other Keep Calm people around, and a table will free up before long.

## How many spots are there?
No cap - the more the merrier. A bigger group simply means we take more tables.

## Do I need to be good at pool?
Not at all. All skill levels are welcome, total beginners included. This is a social event first and foremost.

## What if I can only get there later?
That is fine, it is a drop-in window. Just bear in mind that things wind down by 20:00 and Dan heads off around 19:00.

## Can I bring a +1?
Yes, bring whoever you like. Just ask them to mark themselves as going in the WhatsApp event so the headcount stays accurate.

## What if I can't make it after all?
No problem - there is nothing to cancel since nothing is pre-booked. Just don't worry about it.

## Is there anything on after?
[Keep Calm Board Games](../boardgames/README.md) starts elsewhere around 20:00. It is a separate event, but you are very welcome to head there afterwards.

## Who organises this?
**Noelia** organises and runs the night at the venue. **Dan** helps with the announcement.

## Will there be more pool nights?
This first one is a trial run. If it goes well, pool could become a regular Keep Calm fixture, possibly at other venues too. Watch the **Social WhatsApp group** for announcements.

## How do I join the Social WhatsApp group?
Links are available at [keepcalm.fit](https://keepcalm.fit/).
