## 🎟️ How to Get a Voucher

1. **Mark yourself as going in the WhatsApp event** — this is required so we have a headcount
2. **Turn up at the venue** — vouchers are handed out on arrival
3. **First come, first served** — once they're gone, they're gone
4. **No DMs needed** — no pre-booking, just RSVP in the WhatsApp event and show up early

**Bowling Ilusiona** is inside the **Gran Vía 2** shopping centre in L'Hospitalet de Llobregat — easy to reach by metro or bus.
