# Annual Celebration - Notes for Next Year

Lessons learned from previous celebrations to factor into planning for next year.

## Layout

- Preplan the layout before the day. Make sure the picnic area is not in between the courts. People sitting in the picnic area were being hit by stray balls, which they found annoying. Keep the seating and food area clearly separated from where the games are played.

## Bringing people together and communication

- Find a better way to bring everyone together and to communicate with the whole group during the day. Options to try: a whistle to get everyone's attention, and/or a small group of admins spread across the beach who can pass on announcements.

## Speech

- Try to get people static long enough to do some kind of speech. This was skipped last time because everyone was spread out and busy. Tie it to a moment people already gather for (for example the cake and group photo) so we have everyone in one place.
