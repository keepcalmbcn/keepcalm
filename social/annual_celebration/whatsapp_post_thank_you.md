# WhatsApp Post - Annual Celebration Thank You (send to all groups)

**For sharing in all Keep Calm community channels after the 1st Birthday beach party**



---

💙 *THANK YOU, KEEP CALM* 💙

Good morning @all, What a day yesterday was! 🎂🏖️

Our 1st Birthday beach party was everything we hoped it would be and more - cakes, drinks, ice, food, games, sunshine, drama and a whole load of happy faces. We took loads of brilliant photos, and honestly the best part was just looking around and seeing the community we have all built together, in one place, having a wonderful time.

🖼️ 👉 PLEASE UPLOAD YOUR PICTURES: https://photos.app.goo.gl/e5A6mWpMiPTJjJTr6 👈 🖼️

None of it happens on its own, so a few heartfelt thank yous are in order.

🏐 *To the volleyball crew* - thank you for so generously making space for all of us and running games for the whole community to join in. You welcomed everyone, all levels, with open arms, and it was one of the highlights of the day. We are so grateful.

🤝 *To every single person who turned up* - padel players, volleyball crews, hikers, ping pong legends, tennis lot, runners, and everyone in between. You brought the food, the drinks, the good vibes, and the spirit that makes Keep Calm what it is. Thank you for being part of it.

🌟 *To our admins and organisers* - the quiet engine behind all of this. The people who run the polls, book the courts, welcome the newcomers, sort the kit, answer the questions, take the photos, and show up early and leave late, week after week. You give your time and energy freely, and you make this community feel like home for hundreds of people. We see it, we appreciate it, and we could not do this without you. Thank you. 🙏

---

🌱 *Looking ahead to year two*

One year in and we are just getting started. We would love to grow Keep Calm into even more sports and activities, while keeping the exact same warm, welcoming, no-egos vibe we have today.

To do that, we need more brilliant people helping to organise. 🙌

If you have ever thought "I'd love to help run this" - whether that's giving a hand with an existing activity, or starting something brand new you are passionate about - we want to hear from you. No experience needed, just enthusiasm and a bit of spare time. We will support you every step of the way.

*Drop a message to me or any of the organisers if you'd like to get involved.* 💬

Here's to a fantastic year two, and to all of you who make this community so special.

With love and gratitude,
The Keep Calm team 💙
