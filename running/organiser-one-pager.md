# Running Organiser - One-Pager

**The role in one sentence:** you plan the route and lead the run - the announcements, reminders, and RSVP events post themselves.

## Weekly rhythm

Runs are Wednesday evenings:

- **Before Monday**: have the route and meeting point decided so the week's posts describe the right run
- **Wednesday 18:00**: be at the meeting point, brief the group, lead the run
- **After the run**: stay for the coffee/social if you can - it's half the point

## Handled automatically (you do nothing)

- Announcement Monday, reminder Tuesday, day-of post Wednesday
- Post-run thank-you Wednesday evening
- Next week's RSVP event is created automatically Wednesday night

## Yours to do

- **Pick the route**: out-and-back or loop so all paces work, with distance options (see the [readme](readme.md))
- **Lead on the day**: brief everyone at the start, keep an eye on the back of the group, agree regroup points
- **Answer questions** in the group: route, pace groups, meeting point
- **Welcome newcomers**: pair them with someone at their pace, make the no-drop promise real
- **Safety calls**: shorten or reroute for heat, daylight, or conditions per the [group rules](rules.md)

## You decide without asking anyone

- Route, distance options, and meeting point
- Pace-group handling and regroup points on the day
- Weather and safety calls (runs are free to move or shorten)
- Strikes 1 and 2 for genuine repeat patterns (rare - runs are drop-in)
- Spending up to a normal session's cost (runs are free, so this is essentially nothing) ⚙️ CONFIRM

## Decide together with other organisers

- Changing the regular run day or adding a second weekly run
- Anything with a recurring cost
- Partnerships that touch running

## Escalate to the Community Lead only for

- Money beyond the above
- Bans and appeals
- Anything technical: events, automation, the website
