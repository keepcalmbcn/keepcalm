# 🏃 Run 1: La Carretera de les Aigües - FAQ

## Where do we meet?
**Outside Penitents metro station** (L3, green line). We gather there at 8:00am and walk up to the path together.

[Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Penitents+metro+station+Barcelona)

## When is it?
**Saturday 23rd May 2026, 8:00am.** Aim to arrive a few minutes early so we can set off on time.

## What's the route?
The **Carretera de les Aigües** - a wide, flat, car-free dirt path along the Collserola hillside with panoramic views over Barcelona and the sea. There's a short climb from Penitents up to the path, then it's flat and easy underfoot.

## How far will we run?
That's up to you. It's an out-and-back, so you turn around whenever you like. We'll loosely group around:
- **~5k** - turn around at roughly 2.5k
- **~10k** - turn around at roughly 5k
- **Half marathon (~21k)** - keep going further along the path

## I'm a walker, not a runner. Can I still come?
**Absolutely.** This event is framed as a walk *and* a run on purpose. Walk a comfortable stretch, enjoy the views, and turn back whenever suits you. You'll still be part of the group.

## How fit do I need to be?
Not very. The path itself is flat, so the only real effort is the short climb up from Penitents at the start. Pick a distance that suits you and go at your own pace.

## How much does it cost?
**Free.** Bring a little cash or card if you'd like to join the coffee after.

## Is there a poll?
Yes - vote in the **distance poll** in the Running WhatsApp group so the organisers know rough numbers for each distance. See [whatsapp-poll.md](whatsapp-poll.md).

## How do I sign up?
1. **Vote in the distance poll** in the Running WhatsApp group
2. **Mark yourself as going in the WhatsApp event** so we have a headcount

No DMs needed - just RSVP and turn up.

## What should I bring?
Trainers or running shoes, water, and your phone. May mornings are sunny, so a cap, sunglasses, and sun cream are worth packing. Bring a little cash or card for coffee after.

## What's the pace? Will I get left behind?
No. Because it's an out-and-back, faster runners and slower walkers naturally cross paths, so the group stays connected and no one is left alone. Faster runners are asked to circle back and check on everyone - see the [Running Group Rules](../rules.md).

## Is there coffee after?
Yes, for anyone who wants it. We'll regroup near Penitents afterwards and head somewhere for coffee. The exact spot is decided on the day.

## What if it rains?
Light rain, we'll likely still go. If heavy rain is forecast we may postpone, since the dirt path gets muddy. Watch the **Running WhatsApp group** on the morning for any update.

## Can I bring a +1?
Yes, bring whoever you like. It's free and open, just ask them to vote in the poll and mark themselves as going too.

## What if I'm running late or can't make it?
Let someone know in the **Running WhatsApp group** so we're not waiting around. If you said you were coming, please make the effort to show up - others may be planning their run around having company.

## Who organises this?
**Dan, Mitali and Nori** put the first run together. Dan handles the announcement, and all three will be there on the day.

## Will there be more runs?
That's the plan! This is **Run 1** and the start of Keep Calm Running as a regular thing. Watch the **Running WhatsApp group** for future announcements.
