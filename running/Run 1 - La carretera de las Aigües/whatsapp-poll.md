Posted a few days before the run, alongside the [announcement](whatsapp-post.md).

How far are you thinking of going on Saturday? It's an out-and-back, so you can turn around whenever you like. This just helps us loosely group people by pace and distance.

Pick the closest option:

- 🏃 ~5k (easy)
- 🏃‍♂️ ~10k
