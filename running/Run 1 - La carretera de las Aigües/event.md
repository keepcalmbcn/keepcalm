## 🏃 About the Route

The **Carretera de les Aigües** is one of Barcelona's best-loved spots for running and walking. It's a wide, mostly flat dirt path that runs along the Collserola hillside, completely car-free, with sweeping views over the whole city and out to the sea. Roughly 10k long end to end, it's an ideal first group route: flat, easy underfoot, and impossible to get lost on.

Because it's an out-and-back, you can do exactly the distance you want. Walkers can stroll a comfortable stretch and turn back, while anyone chasing the half marathon can push further along the path.

## ℹ️ Good to Know

- **Run or walk** - this one is framed as both, so don't be put off if you're not a runner
- **All paces welcome** - it's an out-and-back, so faster runners and walkers cross paths and no one gets left behind. See the [Running Group Rules](../rules.md) on respecting all paces.
- **Bring:** trainers or running shoes, water, and your phone. May mornings are sunny, so a cap, sunglasses, and sun cream are a good idea.
- **Coffee after** - bring a little cash or card if you'd like to join
- **Late or can't make it?** - let someone know in the Running WhatsApp group so we're not waiting around
- **You run at your own risk** - know your limits and pick a distance that suits you
- **Photos likely** - see the data protection notice below

## ⚠️ Safety & Disclaimer
