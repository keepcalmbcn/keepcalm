# Media Ideas for Keep Calm Running

Photos and visuals that work well for promoting the running group.

## Route Shots

- The seafront path along the Barceloneta and Poblenou coastline - especially early morning with soft light
- La Carretera de les Aigues - the mountain trail with city views stretching behind
- Ciutadella Park - relaxed, green, accessible, great for showing the social side
- Montjuic paths - dramatic city backdrop, good for varied terrain shots
- El Born and Gracia streets - urban running with character

## Group and People Shots

- Runners gathered at a meeting point before a run (relaxed, chatting, smiling)
- Mixed group mid-run showing a range of ages and abilities
- Post-run coffee or water stop - this captures the social element well
- Action shots at pace on scenic routes, especially with Barcelona in the background

## Mood and Atmosphere

- Early morning golden hour on the seafront
- Views from Carretera de les Aigues looking over the city
- Running gear laid out informally (shoes, water bottle, phone)
- Small group finishing a run together

## Practical Notes

- Avoid anything that looks staged or stock-photo-like
- Candid group shots perform better than posed ones
- Show the city as much as possible - Barcelona is a selling point
- Get consent before posting photos of identifiable people
