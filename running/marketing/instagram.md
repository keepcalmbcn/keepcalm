Running is better with people. 🏃

We've just launched Keep Calm Running - group runs around Barcelona for English speakers of all paces and levels. Seafront, parks, mountain trails, city streets. Routes change, the good company doesn't.

No club fees, no pressure, no minimum pace. Just show up, run as far as feels good, and enjoy the city from the best possible angle.

Part of the Keep Calm and Carry On community - free, volunteer-run, and open to everyone.

First run coming up on the Carretera de les Aigues. Details in the link in bio 👆
