# Meetup.com Event Description - Keep Calm Running

---

**English Speaking Running - All Levels**

https://keepcalm.fit/meetup/running

Join us for a group run as part of Keep Calm, the not-for-profit sports community for English speakers in Barcelona.

**What to expect**

A relaxed group run along one of Barcelona's best routes. We share the meeting point, time, and distance in advance so you know what you're signing up for. Runs vary in terrain and length - from flat seafront paths to hillside trails with city views.

**Pace and level**

All levels welcome. We don't run at one fixed pace - faster runners circle back and check in, and nobody gets left behind. If you're getting back into running or haven't run in a while, you're very welcome.

**Cost**

Free. Keep Calm is a not-for-profit community run entirely by volunteers.

**What to bring**

Water, comfortable running shoes, and your phone with emergency contacts accessible.

**How to join**

RSVP here on Meetup, then join our WhatsApp group at https://keepcalm.fit/meetup/running - this is where run announcements, meeting points, routes, and distances are shared, and you must be in the group before you can come to a run.

**About Keep Calm**

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona. Adults only (18+). Follow us on Instagram at instagram.com/keepcalmbarcelona and like us on Facebook at facebook.com/keepcalmbarcelona to stay up to date with everything we do.
