# Keep Calm Running - Group Rules

Welcome to Keep Calm Running! To keep things safe and enjoyable for everyone, please follow these guidelines.

> **These rules add to — but don't replace — our community-wide policies. Please read these first if you haven't already:**
> - [WhatsApp Rules](https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/whatsapp-rules.md) — group communication, unsolicited DMs, referral links, spam
> - [Community Conduct](https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/community-conduct.md) — respect, harassment, advertising, conflict resolution
> - [Event Rules](https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/event-rules.md) — commitment, cancellations, the strike system

## 1. Attendance

- If you say you're coming, make the effort to show up
- Others may plan their run around having company, so dropping out last minute is frustrating
- If your plans change, let the group know

## 2. Safety

- Stick to agreed routes and meeting points
- Let someone know if you need to stop or leave the group early
- Be visible - wear bright or reflective clothing for evening runs
- Bring your phone and keep emergency contacts accessible
- Stay hydrated, especially in summer
- You run at your own risk - know your limits

## 3. Respect All Paces

- Not everyone runs at the same speed, and that's fine
- Don't leave slower runners behind without checking they're OK
- If the group splits by pace, agree on a meeting point
- Faster runners - circle back and check on the group
