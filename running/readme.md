# Keep Calm Running 🏃

Welcome to Keep Calm Running! Group runs around Barcelona for English speakers, at every pace.

Part of the larger **Keep Calm** not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona, we're built on values of inclusivity, reliability, and collaboration. Everyone is welcome, and the community belongs to all of us.

## How It Works

- Runs are posted in the WhatsApp group with the meeting point, time, and route
- Many runs use a **distance poll** so organisers know rough numbers for each distance and can loosely group people by pace
- Most runs are **free** - just show up, no booking needed
- Routes are usually **out-and-back or loops**, so you can pick your own distance and turn around whenever you like
- Coffee or breakfast together after, for anyone who fancies it

## What to Expect

- A friendly, no-pressure group where every pace is genuinely welcome
- Runners and walkers alongside each other on routes that suit both
- A mix of scenic routes around the city and the hills above it
- People looking out for each other - we don't drop slower runners
- A social coffee at the end as much a part of the morning as the run

## Pick Your Distance

Our runs are built so nobody gets left behind:

- Routes are usually **out-and-back**, so you turn around at whatever distance suits you
- We loosely group around common distances (for example ~5k, ~10k, and half marathon)
- Walk it, jog it, or push the pace - it is your run
- Faster runners circle back so the group stays together

## Where We Run

Routes vary, but Barcelona offers some great running:

- **La Carretera de les Aigües** - the flat, car-free path along the Collserola hills with panoramic views over the city
- **The seafront and beaches** - flat, fast, and scenic
- **Parks and city loops** - Ciutadella, Montjuïc, and more

The exact route and meeting point are posted with each run.

## Cost

- **Free.** Group runs cost nothing - just turn up
- You only pay for your own coffee or breakfast afterwards if you join that
- No Keep Calm membership fee - everything is volunteer-run and not-for-profit

## What to Bring

- **Running shoes** and comfortable kit
- **Water**, especially in the warmer months
- **Your phone** with emergency contacts accessible
- **Bright or reflective clothing** for evening runs so you stay visible
- Sunscreen and a hat for sunny daytime runs

## Safety

- You run at your own risk - know your limits and your fitness honestly
- Stick to agreed routes and meeting points
- Tell someone if you need to stop or leave the group early
- Stay hydrated and be visible in low light
- Runs are not coached or guided - everyone takes part independently and looks after their own safety

## Getting Started

1. Join the Running WhatsApp group (links at https://keepcalm.fit/)
2. Watch for run announcements and vote in the distance poll if there is one
3. Turn up at the meeting point on time
4. Run or walk your distance, at your pace
5. Stick around for a coffee if you can
