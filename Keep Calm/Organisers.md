## Weekly Posting Schedule

### Saturday Morning
- **Padel poll** posted by Dan/Nadir (for next Sunday's games)

### Tuesday/Wednesday
- **Padel games** posted after poll closes

### Monday/Tuesday
- **Tennis poll/announcement** posted by Dan/Russ/Jeff (for upcoming Tuesday)

### Tuesday/Wednesday
- **Ping Pong reminder** posted by Jia (for Thursday)

### Thursday/Friday
- **Volleyball sessions** often announced by Chris/Mario/Nix (for Saturday or upcoming days)
