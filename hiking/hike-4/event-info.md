# 🥾 Keep Calm Coastal Hike & Beach
## Garraf → Sitges via GR-92 & Ermita de la Trinitat | Sunday 24th May 2026

---

| | |
|---|---|
| 📍 Route | Garraf → Sitges (GR-92 Camí de les Costes) |
| 📏 Distance | ~9 km (station to station) |
| ⏱️ Walking time | ~3 - 3.5 hours |
| 🟡 Difficulty | Easy-to-moderate - a few climbs (~300-400m total ascent), nothing technical |
| ✨ Highlights | Ermita de la Trinitat clifftop chapel, Campdàsens medieval farmhouse, Castellet ruins, Balcó de Sitges viewpoint, sea views over the Garraf massif, finish on Sitges beach |

[View route on Wikiloc](https://www.wikiloc.com/hiking-trails/garraf-gr92-ermita-de-la-trinitat-sitges-21988567)

> Note: this one is a proper coastal hill walk, not a flat beach stroll. It climbs up to the Ermita de la Trinitat and over a couple of headlands, so expect a bit more of a workout than the last hike.

---

## 🚉 Getting There (R2 Sud towards Vilanova)

Garraf is one stop before Sitges on the R2 Sud line, so we take the train down and walk back to Sitges.

| Stop | Meet | Train departs |
|---|---|---|
| Passeig de Gràcia | **08:25** | ~08:55 |
| Barcelona Sants | **08:30** | ~09:00 |
| Garraf | ~09:30 | (start of walk) |

- Meet at **Barcelona Sants** in the main hall near the ticket machines - look out for **Alona**, who's leading this hike, and the organisers
- Journey time: ~45 minutes from Sants
- Ticket: ~€4-5 one way (may be covered by your T-Mobilitat card)
- **Please be punctual - we can't hold the train!**
- Final train times confirmed the week before via the Rodalies app

## 🗓️ On the Day

**Morning - The Walk**
- Group photo at Garraf station (start of route)
- Climb up through the Garraf hills to the Ermita de la Trinitat (the scenic highlight - a white clifftop chapel with the sea behind it)
- Short snack and photo stop at the viewpoint
- Heads up: one short stretch passes the Vallcarca cement works - not the prettiest part, but it's brief

**Afternoon - Sitges**
- Arrive central Sitges ~13:30-14:30
- Swim at Sitges beach, shared lunch and snacks
- Drinks and food in Sitges - we'll find somewhere on the day

**Return trains from Sitges to Sants (~40 min journey)**

| Option | Depart Sitges | Arrive Sants |
|---|---|---|
| A | ~16:30 | ~17:10 |
| B | ~17:00 | ~17:40 |
| C | ~17:30 | ~18:10 |

*Confirm via the Rodalies app closer to the date*

## 🎒 What to Bring

- Hiking shoes (better here - the route is rockier and hillier than the last one) or sturdy trainers
- Water (1.5-2L, it gets hot in May)
- Sun cream and hat
- Light jacket or extra layer (it can get cold by the sea, especially after a swim)
- Swimwear and towel for the swim in Sitges
- Packed lunch
- Snacks to share (encouraged!)

## ℹ️ Good to Know

**Logistics**
- This hike is posted as a **WhatsApp Event** in the hiking group - tap **"Going"** so we have a headcount
- No sign-up form this time, just mark yourself as going on the event
- All updates come through the hiking WhatsApp group
- If it rains we'll postpone - keep an eye on the group

**On the trail**
- A few uphill sections - this is a bit more of a workout than the flat coastal walks
- Dogs welcome - keep them close on the climbs and rocky bits
- Stay with the group on the descents into Sitges

**General**
- Open to all, no cap on numbers
- Please check you're comfortable with ~9 km and some hills before signing up
- We're a friendly bunch - say hi to people along the way!
- **Alona** is leading this hike - say hi on the day
- Any questions? Drop a message in the group or DM Alona directly

**Disclaimer**
This is a free, voluntary walk on marked public trails. Everyone participates independently and is responsible for their own safety, decisions, and insurance. This is not a guided activity.
