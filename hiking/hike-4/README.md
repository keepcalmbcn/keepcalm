# Hike 4 - Garraf to Sitges (GR-92, Ermita de la Trinitat)

**Sunday 24th May 2026** | ~9 km | ~3-3.5 hours | easy-to-moderate (a few climbs)
**Organiser:** Alona (leading on the day)

A coastal hill walk along the GR-92 from Garraf up to the Ermita de la Trinitat clifftop chapel, then down into Sitges for a swim and drinks. No sign-up form - RSVP via a WhatsApp Event ("tap Going").

[View route on Wikiloc](https://www.wikiloc.com/hiking-trails/garraf-gr92-ermita-de-la-trinitat-sitges-21988567)
