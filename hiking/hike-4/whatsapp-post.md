🥾 KEEP CALM | COASTAL HIKE & BEACH - Garraf to Sitges | Sunday 24th May 🏖️

Hi @all we're back on the coast! Join us for a ~9km walk along the GR-92 from Garraf to Sitges, climbing up through the Garraf hills to the beautiful Ermita de la Trinitat before dropping down into Sitges.

⛪ Whitewashed clifftop chapel + medieval farmhouse
🌊 Big sea views over the Garraf massif
🏖️ Swim at Sitges beach to finish
🍉 Bring your own food and share with others!
🍻 Drinks in Sitges to round off the day
🟡 Easy-to-moderate | ~3-3.5 hours | a few climbs (more of a workout than last time)
🚉 Barcelona Sants 08:30 (or Passeig de Gràcia 08:25)
👋 Alona's leading this one - say hi at the station!
🆓 The hike itself is completely FREE!
