# WhatsApp Post - Keep Calm Hiking

---

🥾 *Keep Calm Hiking - join us for our next adventure!*

We do monthly hikes around Catalunya - coastal paths, mountain trails, forest routes. All reachable by train from Barcelona in under two hours.

*What to expect:*
- 10-20km through some seriously beautiful scenery
- Shared lunch tradition - everyone brings something to eat together
- Friendly, mixed group - all fitness levels welcome
- Cost: just the train fare (usually €6-14 return) 🚆

Part of Keep Calm and Carry On, a not-for-profit English-speaking community in Barcelona.

👉 Join the hiking WhatsApp group at *keepcalm.fit* to see the next hike announcement!

18+ only.
