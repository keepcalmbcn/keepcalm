🥾 *Fancy a day hiking in the Catalan countryside?* 🥾

We're a friendly, sociable group that gets out for a hike around once a month, and we'd love more people to join us! 🏔️

🗓️ *When:* Roughly monthly, usually a full day on a Sunday
📍 *Where:* Beautiful trails around Catalonia (coast, mountains, forests, medieval villages), reached by train from Barcelona
💶 *Cost:* Free! Just the train fare (usually €6-14 for the day)
🥾 *Distance:* Typically 10-20km, with route details shared in advance so you can pick the ones that suit you

✅ *All levels welcome* - the route difficulty is always shared so you can choose what's right for your fitness
✅ Bring water, solid shoes, layers and some food for our shared group lunch (a lovely tradition!)
✅ Great scenery, great company, often drinks or dinner together after

If you'd like to come along, or just have a question, *send me a message* and I'll get you involved! 🥾🏔️
