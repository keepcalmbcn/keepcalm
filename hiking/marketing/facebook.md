# Facebook Post - Keep Calm Hiking

---

If you've been meaning to actually get out and explore Catalunya properly, this might be the nudge you need.

Keep Calm Hiking is a monthly hiking group run by Keep Calm, a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona. We pick a route - coastal trails, mountain paths, forest tracks, medieval villages - and head out there together by train on a Sunday.

The hikes are proper full-day trips: typically 10-20km with 300-800m of elevation, taking around 4-6 hours. Routes vary but they're all within an hour or two of Barcelona by Rodalies, so there's no car needed.

One of the best parts is the shared lunch tradition. Everyone brings something to eat and you lay it all out together on a rock or in a clearing somewhere. It sounds simple but it's honestly one of the highlights.

Cost is basically nothing - just your train fare, usually around €6-14 return.

The group is friendly and mixed - some people are experienced hikers, others are just getting started. You don't need to be super fit, but routes are described in detail so you can judge for yourself before signing up.

We're a volunteer-run community with no commercial interest - just people who want to explore this part of Spain with good company.

Head to keepcalm.fit to find the hiking WhatsApp group and see what's coming up next. Open to adults (18+).
