# Luma Event Description - Keep Calm Hiking

---

**English Speaking Hiking - All Levels**

Full-day hikes through some of Catalunya's best scenery: coastal clifftop trails, mountain paths, and forest tracks, all reachable by Rodalies train in under two hours from Barcelona. Shared lunch is a highlight.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Monthly hikes, dates announced in the WhatsApp group
- 📍 Meeting point shared in the group, reached by train
- 💶 Free, just your train fare (~€6-14 return)
- 👥 Intermediate difficulty (10-20km), all levels welcome, 18+

**How to join**

RSVP here, then join our hiking WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/luma/hiking - hikes are announced there with the meeting point, time, and route. Sign-ups via a linked form, capacity 25-30.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
