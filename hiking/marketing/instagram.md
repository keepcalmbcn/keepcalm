# Instagram Caption - Keep Calm Hiking

---

Catalunya has some of the best hiking in Europe, and a lot of it is less than an hour from Barcelona by train. Coastal clifftops, mountain ridges, forest paths, ancient villages - we try to do all of it.

Keep Calm Hiking goes out once a month on a Sunday. Full day trip, shared lunch on the trail, good group of people. Cost is just the train fare.

Part of Keep Calm and Carry On - a not-for-profit English-speaking community in Barcelona, run entirely by volunteers.

Link in bio to join the hiking group and see what's coming up next. 🥾
