# Hiking Organiser - One-Pager

**The role in one sentence:** you plan and announce roughly one hike a month and make the safety calls - hiking has no automated posts, so the announcements are yours too.

## Monthly rhythm

Hikes are roughly monthly, usually Sundays that don't clash with major padel events:

- **2-3 weeks before**: pick the route, set the date, post the announcement with the sign-up form
- **The week of**: confirm numbers, watch the forecast, make the go/no-go call in advance
- **Hike day**: lead the group (or hand the front to a volunteer co-lead with the route), make real-time safety calls

## Handled automatically (you do nothing)

- Community-wide posts only: monthly announcements, group spotlights, inactivity reminders
- Nothing hike-specific is automated - announcements, reminders, and the sign-up form are yours

## Yours to do

- **Plan the route**: distance, elevation, and difficulty clearly stated (see [planning](planning.md))
- **Post the announcement**: date, meeting point, train plan, what to bring, capacity (25-30)
- **Manage sign-ups**: run the form, keep a waiting list, fill dropouts
- **Answer questions**: difficulty, kit, logistics - be honest with people about fitness per the [group rules](rules.md)
- **Weather and safety**: go/no-go in advance; on the trail, keep the group together and adjust as needed
- **Welcome newcomers**: check they know what they signed up for, pair them into the group
- **Apply strikes** for no-shows - spots are capacity-limited, so no-shows block others

## You decide without asking anyone

- Route, date, capacity, meeting point, and train plan
- Go/no-go weather calls and on-trail safety decisions
- Strikes 1 and 2, and waiting-list management
- Spending up to a hike's normal per-person cost (train fare territory) to solve a problem ⚙️ CONFIRM

## Decide together with other organisers

- Paid activities (guided routes, huts, transport hire) or anything beyond train-fare costs
- Multi-day trips
- Changing the monthly cadence
- Strike-3 removals

## Escalate to the Community Lead only for

- Money beyond the above
- Bans and appeals
- Anything technical: forms, automation, the website

## Handover

Stepping down? Follow the Stepping Down checklist in the [Organiser Guide](../keep-calm/governance/organiser-guide.md#stepping-down), and hand over the route ideas list.
