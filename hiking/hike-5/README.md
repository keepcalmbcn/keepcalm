# Hike 5 - 7 Gorges (7 Gorgs), Campdevànol

**Sunday 19th July 2026** | ~13 km round trip | 3-5 hours | easy-to-moderate (~200m elevation)
**Organised by:** Alona, Mitali and Prashant

A day trip out to the 7 Gorgs natural park near Campdevànol - seven waterfalls linked by a there-and-back trail, most of them swimmable. Trains don't currently reach the area, so the group chartered a private bus instead, departing from Fabra i Puig metro station.

[Route info](https://misrutasfavoritas.com/ruta-7-gorgs-campdevanol/)

## Notes for next time

- **Bus:** chartered through [Guin Bus](https://guinbus.com/), met at Fabra i Puig metro station. ~30 people went (bus plus one car).
- **Timing:** settled on an 8:30am departure (voted on in the group, to beat the midday heat), returning to Barcelona ~18:30-19:00. Round trip driving time from Fabra i Puig is roughly 1.5 hours each way.
- **Cost:** ~23.80€ per person for the bus, plus ~3€ each for the park entry/reservation.
- **Park reservation:** entry to the falls requires a booking in advance via the Campdevànol town hall site - https://reserves.campdevanol.cat/producte/torrent-de-la-cabana/. One person books for the whole group and gets individual QR codes to distribute - worth sorting well ahead of the day, not last minute.
- **Swimming:** all seven gorges/waterfalls are swimmable - make sure the announcement clearly says to bring swimwear and a towel.
- **Food/water:** no shops near the meeting point open early enough to rely on - remind people to bring their own food and water the night before.
