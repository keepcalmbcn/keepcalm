🥾 Keep Calm Mega Hike — Saturday 9th May 2026
Route: Vilanova i la Geltrú → Sitges via the GR-92 Camí de Ronda
Distance: ~8.5 km (~10 km station-to-station)
Walking time: ~3 hours
Difficulty: Beginner-friendly, almost entirely flat (max 36m elevation gain)
Highlights: Coastal cliffs, ~14 beaches and coves, Punta Grossa viewpoint, Cova del Gegant prehistoric cave, Civil War bunker, Atlàntida ruins

🗺️ Route map: https://es.wikiloc.com/rutas-senderismo/cami-de-ronda-gr-92-sitges-vilanova-i-la-geltru-33393692

🎒 What to bring

Trainers are fine; hiking shoes are more comfortable on rocky sections
Water (1.5–2L recommended for May sun)
Sun cream and hat
Light jacket or extra layer (coastal wind can be fresh, especially after a swim)
Swimwear and towel for the mid-hike dip
Packed lunch
Snacks to share (encouraged!)
