# 🥾 Keep Calm Coastal Hike & Beach
## Vilanova → Sitges via GR-92 Camí de Ronda | Saturday 9th May 2026

---

| | |
|---|---|
| 📍 Route | Vilanova i la Geltrú → Sitges |
| 📏 Distance | ~8.5 km (~10 km station-to-station) |
| ⏱️ Walking time | ~3 hours |
| 🟢 Difficulty | Beginner-friendly, almost entirely flat (max 36m elevation gain) |
| ✨ Highlights | Coastal cliffs, ~14 beaches & coves, Punta Grossa viewpoint, Cova del Gegant cave, Civil War bunker, Atlàntida ruins |

## 🎒 What to Bring

- Trainers (fine) or hiking shoes (better on rocky sections)
- Water (1.5-2L, it gets hot in May)
- Sun cream and hat
- Light jacket or extra layer (it can get cold by the sea, especially after a swim)
- Swimwear and towel for the mid-hike dip
- Packed lunch
- Snacks to share (encouraged!)

- RSVP by **Friday 8th May evening** so we have a headcount
