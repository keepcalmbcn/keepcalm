# Hike Weather Guide

A reference for checking and interpreting weather before any Keep Calm hike, based on experience planning the Vilanova–Sitges coastal hike (GR-92, May 2026).

---

## Weather Sources

Check all four sources and compare — they often disagree, especially for coastal routes.

| Source | Link | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| AEMET | [aemet.es – Sitges](https://www.aemet.es/es/eltiempo/prediccion/municipios/sitges-id08270) | Official Spanish met agency — most reliable overall |
| AEMET Hourly | [aemet.es – Sitges hourly](https://www.aemet.es/es/eltiempo/prediccion/municipios/horas/tabla/sitges-id08270) | Best for timing rain and wind by the hour |
| Meteoblue | [meteoblue.com – Sitges](https://www.meteoblue.com/en/weather/week/sitges_spain_6534273) | Good hourly breakdowns, tends to be optimistic |
| El Tiempo | [eltiempo.es – Sitges](https://www.eltiempo.es/sitges.html) | Popular but tends to overstate rain probability — weight least |
| Meteo.cat | [meteo.cat](https://www.meteo.cat/) | Catalan met service, good for local detail |

**When sources disagree, trust AEMET first, then Meteo.cat.**

El Tiempo in particular has a known tendency to overcall rain, especially on the Mediterranean coast — a 100% forecast from El Tiempo alone is not a reason to cancel.

---

## What to Check for Coastal Hikes

### Wind
Wind matters more than rain on exposed coastal paths like the GR-92.

- The clifftop sections between Vilanova and Sitges are **fully exposed to easterly and northeasterly winds**
- Gusts above **30 km/h** on clifftops make walking unpleasant and potentially unsafe
- Gusts above **40 km/h** are a strong reason to postpone
- Wind direction matters: **easterly (E) and northeasterly (NE)** blow directly into walkers on this route

### Rain
- Light drizzle is manageable — it is not a reason to cancel on its own
- Check the **timing** of rain against the hike schedule — morning rain that clears by 10am is very different to rain arriving at 2pm
- For the Vilanova–Sitges route, rain arriving after 2pm mainly affects the Sitges social at the end, not the walk itself
- Look for rainfall amounts: under 1mm per hour is light, 4–5mm per hour is heavy

### Temperature
- May hikes: 16–22°C is comfortable. Below 14°C with wind and rain becomes a welfare concern.
- Factor in the **sea breeze and post-swim chill** — it can feel significantly colder after a swim stop

## Hike 3 – Vilanova to Sitges (16 May 2026) – Decision Log

Postponed due to weather. Here's what the forecast showed the day before:

| Source | Conditions | Rain | Wind (peak) |
|--------|-----------|------|-------------|
| AEMET | Cloudy, rain from 14:00 | 25–35% morning, 100% from 14:00 | 22 km/h E, gusts 40 km/h at 12:00 |
| El Tiempo | Overcast, rain all day | 100% | 26 km/h, gusts 39 km/h |
| Meteoblue | Clear | 0% | 4–22 km/h NE |
| Meteo.cat | Heavy cloud, dry | ~0% daytime | 9–17 km/h |

**Decision:** Postponed. The AEMET hourly forecast showed gusts of 40 km/h during the beach stop and the clifftop walk, with heavy rain arriving at exactly 15:00 — when the group would be waiting for the train in Sitges. Even setting El Tiempo aside, the wind alone on the exposed clifftops was the deciding factor.

**Lesson:** Check AEMET hourly (not just the daily summary) — the daily view only shows 6-hour blocks, whereas the hourly table gives a much clearer picture of timing.

---

## Decision Timeline

| When | Action |
|------|--------|
| 5–7 days before | First check — look for any major red flags (storms, named weather events) |
| 2–3 days before | Main assessment — compare all sources, check wind and rain timing against the hike schedule |
| Day before (evening) | Final call — send WhatsApp update to the group either way |
| Morning of hike | Last-minute check if borderline — Barcelona coastal weather can shift overnight |

---

## Communication

- Send a WhatsApp message to the hiking group with the decision and the reasons
- If postponing, run a poll for the new date at the same time
- Keep the tone positive — one postponement is not a big deal
