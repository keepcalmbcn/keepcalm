🥾 KEEP CALM | COASTAL HIKE & BEACH — Vilanova to Sitges 🏖️
Saturday 9th May 2026

A beginner-friendly coastal walk along the GR-92 Camí de Ronda from Vilanova i la Geltrú to Sitges, with a mid-hike swim stop and drinks in Sitges afterwards.

📍 ROUTE
• Vilanova i la Geltrú → Sitges via GR-92 Camí de Ronda
• Distance: ~8.5 km (~10 km station-to-station)
• Walking time: ~3 hours
• Difficulty: Beginner-friendly, almost entirely flat (max 36m elevation gain)
• Highlights: Coastal cliffs, ~14 beaches & coves, Punta Grossa viewpoint, Cova del Gegant cave, Civil War bunker, Atlàntida ruins
• Route map: https://es.wikiloc.com/rutas-senderismo/cami-de-ronda-gr-92-sitges-vilanova-i-la-geltru-33393692

🚉 GETTING THERE — R2 Sud towards Vilanova
• Passeig de Gràcia — meet 08:25 (train ~08:55)
• Barcelona Sants — meet 08:30 (train ~09:00) — main hall near the ticket machines
• Vilanova i la Geltrú — meet 09:40 at the station exit
• Journey time: ~1 hour from Sants
• Ticket: ~€8 one way (may be covered by T-Mobilitat card)
• Please be punctual — we can't hold the train!
• Final train times confirmed the week before via the Rodalies app

🗓️ ON THE DAY
• Group photo at Far de Sant Cristòfol lighthouse (start of route)
• ~1 hour beach stop mid-route for a swim and snacks
• Arrive central Sitges ~14:30–15:00
• Drinks and food in Sitges — we'll find somewhere on the day

🚆 RETURN TRAINS — Sitges to Sants (~40 min journey)
• Option A: depart ~16:30 → arrive ~17:10
• Option B: depart ~17:00 → arrive ~17:40
• Option C: depart ~17:30 → arrive ~18:10
Confirm via the Rodalies app closer to the date.

🎒 WHAT TO BRING
• Trainers (fine) or hiking shoes (better on rocky sections)
• Water — 1.5–2L recommended for May sun
• Sun cream and hat
• Light jacket or extra layer (coastal wind can be fresh after a swim)
• Swimwear and towel for the mid-hike dip
• Packed lunch
• Snacks to share (encouraged!)
