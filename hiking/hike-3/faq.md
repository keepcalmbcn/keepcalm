# FAQ - Coastal Hike & Beach (Vilanova to Sitges)

Quick answers to the questions we've been asked most. Anything not covered here? Drop a message in the hiking WhatsApp group: https://keepcalm.fit

---

**Can I bring my dog?**
Yes! Dogs are very welcome. Please keep your dog on a lead for the whole route though - several sections run right alongside an active railway line, and with a big group of us walking together a lead keeps your dog safe and comfortable around everyone.

**Can I bring my boyfriend, husband, partner or a friend?**
Absolutely - everyone's welcome. Bring whoever you'd like along, the more the merrier. If they haven't signed up via the form yet, it helps us with the headcount if they do.

**Is it just walking, or is it a proper technical hike?**
It's just walking. This is a very non-technical, beginner-friendly hike - almost entirely flat (max 36m elevation gain) along a clear coastal path. If you're comfortable with a roughly 3-hour walk, you'll be absolutely fine.

**How much does it cost?**
The hike itself is completely free. The only cost is your train ticket - around €8 one way (it may be covered by your T-Mobilitat card).

**Do I need to be fit?**
No special fitness needed. It's about 8.5 km of walking over roughly 3 hours, on flat ground, with a long beach stop in the middle to rest and swim. Just check you're comfortable with that distance before the day.

**Do I have to swim?**
Not at all. The mid-hike beach stop is there for anyone who fancies a dip, but you're just as welcome to relax on the beach with snacks. Bring swimwear and a towel if you'd like to join in.

**Can I leave early or head back on my own?**
Yes. Return trains from Sitges run regularly and take around 40 minutes back to Sants, so you're free to head back earlier or stay on later.

**What happens if the weather is bad?**
We'd postpone and announce it in the hiking WhatsApp group, so please make sure you've joined. Good news for this one though - the forecast for Saturday is looking sunny!
