ON THE DAY
------------------------------------------------------------

We'll be walking the GR-92 Cami de Ronda from Vilanova to Sitges - about 8.5 km of stunning coastal scenery with clifftop paths, coves, sea caves, and historic landmarks along the way.

Halfway through (maybe closer to the end) we'll stop at one of the beaches for a swim, snacks, and some volleyball - so look forward to that!

We expect to arrive in Sitges around 14:30-15:00, where we'll find somewhere for drinks and food before heading back.

We expect to be ready to leave Sitges between 15:00 and 16:00, though of course you're welcome to head back earlier or stay on later - return trains run regularly and take around 40 minutes back to Sants.

*Rough timings;*
09:00 - Train leaves Sants
10:00 - Arrive Vilanova, ~10-15 mins to gather and set off
10:15 - Start walking
12:00 - Beach stop (rough timing)
13:00 - Back to walking
14:15 - Arrive/Explore Sitges
15:00 - Train to leave Sitges

------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT TO BRING
------------------------------------------------------------
Water (1.5-2L - it gets warm in May)
Sun cream and hat
Trainers or hiking shoes (both are fine, hiking shoes are better on rocky sections)
Swimwear and a towel for the mid-hike swim
A packed lunch
A snack to share at the beach stop
A light jacket or extra layer (it can feel cool by the sea, especially after a swim)
