# Keep Calm Hiking - Group Description

The following is used as the WhatsApp group description.

---

Keep Calm Hiking - part of the Keep Calm and Carry On community for English speakers in Barcelona.

Monthly hikes around Catalonia, usually on Sundays. Routes selected from Komoot, ranging from coastal trails to mountain paths. Full day trips with a shared lunch tradition - everyone brings food to share!

Typical hike: 10-20km, 300-800m elevation, 4-6 hours. Check each announcement for details and assess your fitness level honestly.

Capacity: 25-30 people per hike.

Cost: Free! Just the train fare (usually 3-7 euros each way).

Bring: Water, food to share, solid hiking shoes, layers, rain jacket, sunscreen.
