# Tuesday Tennis - Sign-Up Announcement

This is the WhatsApp message that opens the weekly sign-up form. It goes out (and is pinned for ~4 days) on **Wednesday 18:00** for the session **two Tuesdays away** (~13 days out). Sign-ups stay open until the form closes the following Monday at 22:00.

> Note: the Wednesday / Friday / Monday posts are sent automatically by a scheduled job (kept in our private ops repo). This file is the reference copy of the announcement - keep it in sync with the live template.

🗂 ORGANISATION & RULES

Sign-up closes:
Monday around 22:00. An organiser then builds the matchups during the week, and you'll be posted into a court with your slot and booker before the session.

⚠️ Cancellation policy:
You are part of a 4-person court, so late drop-outs affect three other people. Let us know as early as possible and help find a replacement. Repeated late cancellations may result in removal from the group.
