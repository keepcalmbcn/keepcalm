# Meetup.com Event Description - Keep Calm Tennis

---

**English Speaking Tennis - All Levels**

https://keepcalm.fit/meetup/tennis

Join us for weekly doubles tennis, organised by Keep Calm, a not-for-profit sports community for English speakers in Barcelona.

**What to expect**

90 minutes of doubles tennis (2v2) in a friendly, relaxed atmosphere. We travel to the venue together as a group, so no one has to figure out the journey alone.

**Skill level**

All levels from complete beginners to intermediates are welcome. We use four levels (Beginner, Improver, Intermediate, Advanced) to organise courts when numbers allow, so games feel balanced and enjoyable for everyone.

**Cost**

Around €8-10 per person for 90 minutes. Payment is handled through Playtomic - you'll need a free account set up before joining.

**What to bring**

Your own racket, water, and sports shoes. Tennis balls are coordinated among players.

**How to join**

RSVP here on Meetup, then join our WhatsApp group at https://keepcalm.fit/meetup/tennis - this is where session day, time, and venue are shared, and you must be in the group before you can come to a session. Events are posted in advance; mark yourself as Going and drop your level in the comments, and you'll be tagged in the court booking post once it's confirmed.

**About Keep Calm**

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona. Adults only (18+).
