# Meetup.com Event Description - Keep Calm Tennis

---

Join us for Tuesday Tennis - a weekly doubles session organised by Keep Calm and Carry On, a not-for-profit sports community for English speakers in Barcelona.

**What to expect**

90 minutes of doubles tennis (2v2) in a friendly, relaxed atmosphere. We play at Up Cornellà, a well-maintained indoor/outdoor venue accessible by train from Plaça Espanya. We meet at Plaça Espanya and travel together - no one has to figure out the journey alone.

**Skill level**

All levels from complete beginners to intermediates are welcome. We use four levels (Beginner, Improver, Intermediate, Advanced) to organise courts when numbers allow, so games feel balanced and enjoyable for everyone.

**Cost**

Around €8-10 per person for 90 minutes. Payment is handled through Playtomic - you'll need a free account set up before joining.

**How to join**

Events are posted two weeks in advance. Mark yourself as Going and drop your level in the comments. You'll be tagged in the court booking post once it's confirmed.

**Bring**

Your own racket, water, and sports shoes. Tennis balls are coordinated among players.

Keep Calm is volunteer-run and not-for-profit. Come for the tennis, stay for the people.
