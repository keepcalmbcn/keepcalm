# Luma Event Description - Keep Calm Tennis

---

**English Speaking Tennis - All Levels**

Weekly doubles tennis (2v2) in a friendly, relaxed atmosphere. We travel to the venue together as a group, so no one has to figure out the journey alone.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Weekly session, day and time in the WhatsApp group
- 📍 Barcelona tennis venue, travel together
- 💶 ~€8-10 per person, via Playtomic
- 👥 All levels (Beginner to Advanced), 18+

**How to join**

RSVP here, then join our tennis WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/luma/tennis - session day, time, and venue are shared there. Mark yourself as Going and drop your level in the comments.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
