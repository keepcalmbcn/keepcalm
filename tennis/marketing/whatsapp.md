# WhatsApp Post - Keep Calm Tennis

---

🎾 *Playing tennis in Barcelona?* Come join us!

*Keep Calm Tennis* is a friendly weekly doubles group for English speakers in Barcelona. We play every *Tuesday evening* at Up Cornellà - 90 minutes, all levels from beginner to intermediate.

No partner needed, no pressure, just good tennis and good people. Cost is around *€8-10 per person*.

We're part of *Keep Calm and Carry On* - a not-for-profit sports community run by volunteers. The vibe is inclusive and relaxed.

👉 Find us at *keepcalm.fit* or search *Keep Calm Barcelona* on Instagram/Facebook.

Drop a message if you want to know more! 🙌

📋 By taking part you agree to our policies (safety, data, conduct, 18+): https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/policies/README.md
