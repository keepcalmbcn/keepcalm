🎾 *Fancy a game of tennis?* 🎾

We're a friendly, sociable group that plays tennis every Tuesday evening, and we'd love more people to join us! 🙌

🕢 *When:* Tuesday evenings (around 19:30-20:30, 90 mins)
📍 *Where:* Courts at Up Cornellà - we meet at Plaça Espanya and travel together
💶 *Cost:* Around €8-10 per person
🎾 *Format:* Friendly doubles (2v2) - social and inclusive

✅ *All levels welcome* - from beginners learning the basics to advanced players. We organise courts by level so games are fun and balanced
✅ Bring your own racket (and tennis balls, not padel ones!)
✅ Relaxed, social atmosphere, great way to meet people

If you'd like to come along, or just have a question, *send me a message* and I'll get you involved! 🎾
