# Media Guide - Keep Calm Tennis

Photos and visuals that work well for promoting the tennis activity.

## Action Shots
- Players mid-rally on an outdoor or indoor clay court
- Doubles play - 4 people on court captures the social format well
- A good serve or backhand in motion (movement adds energy)
- Players at the net after a point - laughing or chatting

## Group and Social Moments
- The group meeting at Plaça Espanya before heading to the venue
- Post-game group photo with rackets
- Players sitting courtside with water, catching their breath and chatting
- Mixed ability groups playing together - shows all levels are welcome

## Venue Shots
- The Up Cornellà courts (exterior and playing surface)
- The train/commute to the venue - adds a Barcelona feel
- Evening lighting on court if shooting the later 20:30 slot

## Details and Props
- Tennis balls on a court surface
- Rackets stacked or leaned against a fence
- Playtomic app on a phone (for how-to content)

## Style Notes
- Candid and natural beats posed and stiff
- Evening light is flattering and common given the 19:30/20:30 slots
- Show diversity of ages and backgrounds - this is an inclusive community
- Barcelona outdoor settings (even just a street or plaza) reinforce the location
