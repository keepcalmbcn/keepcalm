Tuesday evenings on court with this lot. 🎾

Keep Calm Tennis is our weekly doubles group for English speakers in Barcelona - beginner to intermediate, all welcome, no partner needed. We meet at Plaça Espanya and head out to Up Cornellà together. Around €8-10 a session and 90 minutes of proper tennis.

Part of Keep Calm and Carry On - a not-for-profit sports community run by volunteers. The sport gets you out of the house. The people keep you coming back.
