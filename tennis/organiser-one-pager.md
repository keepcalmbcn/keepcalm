# Tennis Organiser - One-Pager

**The role in one sentence:** you are the human layer of Tuesday Tennis - the systems post and draft the matchups, you review them, answer people, and make the judgment calls.

## Weekly rhythm

Sessions run Tuesday evenings. Your real touchpoints:

- **Wednesday morning**: review and sign off the matchups draft when it reaches you
- **During the week**: post the confirmed court assignments (players get tagged), confirm bookers
- **Tuesday evening**: be reachable around session time for day-of issues

## Handled automatically (you do nothing)

- Next session's sign-up form is created automatically overnight Tuesday into Wednesday
- The matchups draft (courts by level, with bookers) is generated Wednesday morning and sent to the reviewer for sign-off
- Sign-up announcement Wednesday evening, reminder Friday evening
- Combined final call and post-session thank-you on Tuesday night

## Yours to do

- **Review the matchups**: levels balanced, every court has a booker, no known conflicts, then sign off
- **Answer questions** in the group: levels, Playtomic, the Plaça Espanya meet-up, venues
- **Day-of calls**: weather, no-shows, last-minute replacements
- **Welcome newcomers**: point them at the [readme](readme.md), help them pick an honest level
- **Apply strikes** for no-shows and late cancellations per the [group rules](rules.md) (tennis asks for 5 days notice)

## You decide without asking anyone

- Player assignments, level moves, and session format
- Booker assignments
- Strikes 1 and 2, and replacements for dropouts
- Timing tweaks within the usual 19:00-22:00 window
- Weather calls (same principle as padel: games on unless the venue cancels or the whole court agrees)
- Spending up to a session's normal per-player cost (about 10 euros) to solve a problem ⚙️ CONFIRM

## Decide together with other organisers

- Changing venue or fees, adding or dropping a session
- Strike-3 removals
- Partnerships that touch tennis

## Escalate to the Community Lead only for

- Money beyond the above
- Bans and appeals
- Venue contracts
- Anything technical: forms, automation, the website
