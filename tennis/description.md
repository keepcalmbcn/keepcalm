# Keep Calm Tennis - Group Description

The following is used as the WhatsApp group description.

---

Keep Calm Tennis - part of the Keep Calm and Carry On community for English speakers in Barcelona.

We play every Tuesday evening at 19:30 or 20:30 at Barcelona Pro Tennis Academy (Up Cornellà). We meet at Plaça Espanya ~45 mins before to travel together. Games are organised through an event posted every Wednesday (2 weeks in advance) — tap Going to sign up. Courts are booked via Playtomic. Typically doubles (2v2) format, 90 min sessions.

All levels welcome — currently beginner to intermediate range.

Cost: Around €8–10 per person (90 min session).

Bring your own racket. Tennis balls coordinated among players.
