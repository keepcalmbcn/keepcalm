# Keep Calm Tennis - Group Description

The following is used as the WhatsApp group description.

---

Keep Calm Tennis - part of the Keep Calm and Carry On community for English speakers in Barcelona.

We play every Tuesday from 18:00 at Up Padel & Tennis Cornella. Games are organised through a weekly poll, with courts booked via Playtomic. Typically doubles (2v2) format.

All levels welcome - we match players by ability where possible.

Cost: Around 8 euros per person (90 min session).

Bring your own racket. Tennis balls coordinated among players.
