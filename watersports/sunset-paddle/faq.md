# 🏄 Sunset Paddleboarding - FAQ

## Where is it?
**Paddle Surf Brasil BCN** - see [their website](https://paddlesurfbrasilbcn.netlify.app/). Meet **in front of the venue**, exact street address/directions to be confirmed and added here.

## When is it?
**Saturday 18th July 2026.** Meet at **19:00** in front of the venue, out on the paddleboards from **19:30**.

## How much does it cost?
**15 euros per person** - a reduced group rate Mitali arranged (the standard rate is 25 euros, so it's a 10 euro discount per person).

## How many spots are there?
**25 spots**, first come first served once payment is confirmed. If it fills up, ask to go on the waiting list in case of drop-outs.

## How do I get a spot?
1. **Mark yourself as going in the WhatsApp event** so we have a headcount.
2. **Use the payment link** posted in the Watersports group alongside the announcement, and send your 15 euros. Paying is what actually secures your spot, an RSVP alone doesn't guarantee a place since only 25 spots exist.

## Do I need paddleboarding experience?
No. This is a guided, beginner-friendly experience, and all abilities are welcome.

## Do I need to be a strong swimmer?
A reasonable level of swimming confidence is expected, but let the organiser know if you're not a confident swimmer, most venues can still accommodate you safely with the right equipment.

## What should I bring?
Swimwear (worn under your clothes is easiest), a towel, sunscreen, water, and something warm and dry for after the sun goes down.

## What's provided?
The board, paddle, and safety equipment such as a life vest.

## What should I wear?
Swimwear, with something you don't mind getting a bit wet or salty over it if you like. Barefoot or watersport-friendly footwear, nothing that will slip off in the water.

## Is it one person per board?
Yes, this is solo stand-up paddleboarding, not tandem, unless the venue tells us otherwise on the day.

## How long is the session?
The board time itself is arranged with the venue, check with Mitali or the group announcement for the exact duration. Plan for the full evening from 19:00 (meeting) to sunset and a bit after.

## What happens if the weather is bad?
Safety comes first, if wind or conditions make paddleboarding unsafe, the venue and Mitali will make the call and let the group know as early as possible. See the [group rules](../rules.md) for how weather/safety calls work.

## What if I pay and then can't make it?
Since this is a paid, capacity-limited booking (not a free drop-in like pool or bowling), please read the [group rules](../rules.md) on cancelling with notice, and let Mitali know as soon as you can so your spot can go to someone on the waiting list.

## Is there somewhere to leave my bag, phone, or valuables?
Check with the venue on the day, most paddleboarding operators have a bag storage area, but don't bring anything you'd be devastated to lose or that isn't water-resistant.

## Can I bring a +1?
Yes, if spots are available, they'll need to sign up and pay the same way.

## Is there a minimum age?
Keep Calm is 18+ for all events, no exceptions, regardless of any lower age allowed by the venue itself.

## Who organises this?
**Mitali** arranged the reduced rate and is running this event. See [readme.md](../readme.md) for the Watersports group overview.

## Will there be more watersports events?
This is the first one. If there's demand, more paddleboarding sessions or other water activities may follow, watch the **Watersports WhatsApp group** for announcements.

## How do I join the Watersports WhatsApp group?
Links are available at [keepcalm.fit](https://keepcalm.fit/).
