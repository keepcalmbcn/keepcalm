## 🎟️ How to Get a Spot

1. **Mark yourself as going in the WhatsApp event** - this is required so we have a headcount
2. **Watch the Watersports group for the payment link** - it goes up alongside the announcement
3. **Pay your 15 euros through that link** - this is what actually guarantees your spot, not the RSVP alone
4. **First come, first served** - once 25 people have paid, the list is closed and further interest goes on a waiting list in case of drop-outs

## 📍 Getting There

**Paddle Surf Brasil BCN** - see [their website](https://paddlesurfbrasilbcn.netlify.app/) for the experience itself. Meet **in front of the venue** at 19:00 - exact street address/directions to be confirmed with Mitali and added here.

---

## ℹ️ Good to Know

- **All abilities welcome** - no paddleboarding experience needed, this is a guided beginner-friendly experience
- **Bring**: swimwear, a towel, sunscreen, water, and something warm for after sunset
- **Swimming**: a reasonable level of swimming confidence is expected - let the organiser know if you're not a confident swimmer, most venues can still accommodate this safely
- **Life vests**: worn as instructed by the venue
- **Getting on the water**: meet at 19:00, briefing and kit fitting, then out on the boards at 19:30
- **Cancellations**: since this is a paid, capacity-limited booking, please read the [group rules](../rules.md) on cancelling with notice
- **Photos likely** - see the data protection notice below
