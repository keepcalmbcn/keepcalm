# Keep Calm Watersports 🏄

Welcome to Keep Calm Watersports! Ad-hoc water-based experiences for English speakers in Barcelona, stand-up paddleboarding, and whatever else the group fancies trying next.

Part of the larger **Keep Calm** not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona, we're built on values of inclusivity, reliability, and collaboration. Everyone is welcome, and the community belongs to all of us.

## How It Works

- **Ad-hoc, not weekly** - events run whenever an organiser has arranged access to a venue, activity, or discounted rate
- **Limited spots are common** - most watersports venues cap group size, so sign up promptly and pay upfront where asked
- **Venue and activity vary each time** - stand-up paddleboarding to start, with kayaking, sailing, or other water activities possible if there's interest
