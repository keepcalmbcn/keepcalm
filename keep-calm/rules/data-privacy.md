# Data & Privacy

This document explains what personal data Keep Calm collects, how we use it, and your rights. We aim to be transparent and collect only what's necessary to run the community.

---

## Our Approach

Keep Calm is a volunteer-run community, not a commercial business. We:
- Collect minimal data needed to organise activities
- Don't sell or share your data with third parties
- Don't use your data for marketing beyond Keep Calm activities
- Try to be transparent about what we collect and why

---

## What Data We Collect

### When You Join WhatsApp Groups

**Automatically visible:**
- Phone number (visible to group members)
- WhatsApp display name
- Profile photo (if public)

**Note:** This is controlled by WhatsApp, not Keep Calm. Adjust your WhatsApp privacy settings to control what others see.

### When You Participate

**We may record:**
- Your name
- Which sessions you attend
- Your skill level (self-reported or observed)
- Strike/violation history
- Payment status for paid activities

**Organisers may note:**
- Reliability (for booker assignments)
- Preferences (times, venues)
- Any issues or feedback

### When You Use Playtomic (Padel/Tennis)

**Playtomic collects (their platform, their privacy policy):**
- Email address
- Phone number
- Payment details
- Playing history and skill score
- Profile information

**We may access:**
- Your Playtomic username/profile
- Booking confirmations you share
- Your skill score (public on Playtomic)

### When You Fill Out Forms

**Google Forms or similar may collect:**
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Skill level
- Availability preferences
- Any other fields on the form

### Photos and Videos

**At events:**
- Photos may be taken at sessions
- You may appear in group photos
- Photos may be shared on social media

See the Photography section below.

## How We Use Your Data

### Organising Activities

- Assigning you to games/sessions
- Contacting you about bookings
- Matching skill levels
- Tracking attendance

### Community Management

- Applying the strike system
- Identifying reliable members for roles
- Following up on issues
- Welcoming new members

### Communication

- WhatsApp group announcements
- Direct messages about bookings
- Event invitations
- Community updates

### We Don't

- Sell your data
- Share with third parties for marketing
- Use for purposes unrelated to Keep Calm
- Keep data longer than necessary

---

## Where Data Is Stored

### WhatsApp

- Group messages and member lists
- Controlled by Meta's privacy policy
- We can't export or fully control this data

### Google (Forms, Sheets, Drive)

- Form responses
- Organiser spreadsheets
- Shared documents
- Subject to Google's privacy policy

### Playtomic

- Booking and payment data
- Player profiles and scores
- Controlled by Playtomic's privacy policy

### Organisers' Devices

- Some notes/records may be on personal phones
- Not ideal, but reality of volunteer organisation
- We're working toward centralised, secure storage

---

## Data Retention

### How Long We Keep Data

| Data Type | Retention |
|-----------|-----------|
| WhatsApp messages | As long as you're in the group |
| Attendance records | Duration of your membership + 1 year |
| Strike records | Duration of your membership |
| Form responses | Until no longer needed (typically 1 year) |
| Payment records | As required for financial records |
| Photos | Indefinitely unless you request removal |

### When You Leave

If you leave Keep Calm:
- Your WhatsApp data is removed when you leave groups
- We'll delete organiser records within reasonable time
- Some records may be kept for community continuity
- Photos on social media remain unless you request removal

## Photography

### At Events

**General approach:**
- Group photos are common and expected
- We try to capture the community positively
- Photos may be used for social media

**Your rights:**
- You can ask not to be photographed
- You can step out of group photos
- You can ask to be excluded from specific photos
- Tell the photographer or organiser

**If you're taking photos:**
- Be respectful of others
- Ask before taking close-up/individual shots
- Don't photograph people who've asked not to be
- See [Community-Conduct.md](community-conduct.md)

### Social Media Use

**Photos may be posted on:**
- Keep Calm Instagram
- Keep Calm Facebook
- Other Keep Calm channels

**Before posting:**
- We aim to select flattering, positive images
- We won't deliberately post embarrassing photos
- We may tag individuals (you can untag yourself)

**Requesting removal:**
- Contact the Social Media Coordinator or an organiser
- We'll remove photos within reasonable time
- Be specific about which photos

### Photography Consent

By participating in events, you implicitly consent to:
- Being in group photos
- Photos being shared on Keep Calm social media

If you don't consent:
- Tell the organiser at the start of the event
- Position yourself away from cameras
- Remind photographers if needed

For special events, we may request explicit consent via sign-up forms.

## Your Rights (GDPR)

As we operate in the EU, you have rights under GDPR:

### Right to Access

You can ask what data we hold about you.

**How to request:**
- Contact an organiser or core team member
- We'll respond within 30 days
- We'll provide what we can access

### Right to Rectification

If data about you is incorrect, you can ask us to fix it.

**Examples:**
- Wrong skill level recorded
- Incorrect contact details
- Mistaken strike record

### Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can ask us to delete your data.

**What we can delete:**
- Organiser records and notes
- Form responses
- Spreadsheet entries

**What we can't delete:**
- WhatsApp messages (you control this by leaving groups)
- Playtomic data (contact Playtomic)
- Publicly posted photos (but we'll try to remove)

### Right to Object

You can object to certain uses of your data.

**Examples:**
- Don't include me in social media posts
- Don't share my number with bookers

### How to Exercise Rights

Contact any organiser or core team member. We're volunteers, so:
- Be patientâ€”we'll respond as quickly as we can
- Be specific about what you're requesting
- Understand we may have technical limitations

## Data Security

### What We Do

- Limit access to organiser records
- Use platform security features where available
- Don't share data unnecessarily
- Delete data when no longer needed

### Limitations

As a volunteer organisation:
- We don't have enterprise security systems
- Some data is on personal devices
- We rely on platform security (WhatsApp, Google, etc.)
- Human error is possible

### If There's a Breach

If we become aware of unauthorised data access:
- We'll assess the scope
- We'll notify affected individuals if significant
- We'll take steps to prevent recurrence
- We'll be transparent about what happened

## Organisers and Data Access

### Who Has Access

- **Sport Leads**: Data for their sport
- **Core Team**: Community-wide data
- **Bookers**: Names of players on their court
- **Session Hosts**: Attendance for their session

### Organiser Responsibilities

Organisers should:
- Only access data needed for their role
- Not share data outside Keep Calm
- Delete data when no longer an organiser
- Report any concerns about data security
