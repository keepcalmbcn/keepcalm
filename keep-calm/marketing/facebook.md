# Facebook Post - Keep Calm (All Sports)

For sharing in Barcelona expat groups. Plain paragraphs, no headers.

---

Just moved to Barcelona and looking to meet people? Come and join Keep Calm and Carry On.

We are a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona, built around sport and social connection. In just over a year we have grown to more than 2,000 members, and there is something on almost every day of the week.

Padel on Sundays, tennis on Tuesdays, ping pong on Thursdays, beach volleyball through the week, monthly hikes in the Catalan countryside, plus running, football, yoga, ski trips in winter, and plenty of social events where everyone mixes. Whatever you are into, there is a way in.

The most important thing to know is that all levels are genuinely welcome. Loads of our members turned up having never played the sport before, or not having played in years. You do not need a partner, a team, or any kit to get started.

Everything is run by volunteers and nothing is for profit. Most activities are free or just a few euros to cover courts. There is no membership and no commitment - you just show up to whatever you fancy.

If moving to a new city has felt a bit lonely, this is exactly what we are here for. Come and find your people.

Join the WhatsApp groups for your sports at https://keepcalm.fit/ or follow us on Instagram @keepcalmbarcelona. See you out there!
