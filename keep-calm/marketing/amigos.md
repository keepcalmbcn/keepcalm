## Community description (800 character limit)

Keep Calm is Barcelona's not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers who want to stay active, meet people, and belong. We bring padel, football, tennis, volleyball, ping pong, badminton, basketball, hiking, running, climbing, yoga and pilates, languages, ski trips, and social events together under one roof, all levels welcome, from complete beginners to seasoned players. With thousands of members and something happening most days of the week, it is an easy way to find your people in a new city. Everything is run by volunteers who care about community over competition, not profit: inclusive, reliable, and collaborative. New to Barcelona? Come find your people. Join us, pick an activity, RSVP in the WhatsApp group, and show up. Adults only (18+).
