# Post-Event Recap Template (generic)

A reusable thank-you / recap message to send in the WhatsApp group after any Keep Calm session or event. Copy it, swap the `[PLACEHOLDERS]`, and trim anything that does not apply. Uses WhatsApp formatting (`*bold*`).

Keep the tone warm and inclusive. The photo call-to-action is always a friendly invitation, never a requirement.

---

Hey everyone! 👋

What a [SESSION / GAME / NIGHT / DAY]! Huge thank you to everyone who came along to [EVENT NAME] today - it was exactly what this community is all about. 🙌

🙏 *THANK YOU* to [ORGANISER / HELPERS / HOST NAMES] for making it happen so smoothly. None of this works without people stepping up like that!

📸 *SHARE YOUR SHOTS*
Took any photos or videos today? We'd love to feature them! Share them on Instagram, tag *@keepcalmbarcelona* and use *#keepcalmbarcelona* so we can repost and celebrate our community. No pressure at all - just a lovely way to help show off what we do. Our session photographer will share theirs too, but the more the merrier! 💙

💬 *FEEDBACK*
We'd love to hear how it went for you - what you enjoyed and anything we could do better:
👉 [FEEDBACK FORM LINK]

🔜 *WHAT'S NEXT?*
[NEXT SESSION DETAILS / SIGN-UP LINK]

🌟 *ABOUT KEEP CALM*
Keep Calm is a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona. [ACTIVITY] is just one of the things we do - we also run volleyball, ping pong, tennis, badminton, football, hiking, running and social events.
• 🌐 https://keepcalm.fit/
• 📸 https://www.instagram.com/keepcalmbarcelona/
• 📘 https://www.facebook.com/keepcalmbarcelona
• 📋 Our policies (safety, data, conduct, 18+): https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/policies/README.md

Thanks again for being part of today. See you again soon! 💪
