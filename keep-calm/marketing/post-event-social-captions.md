# Post-Event Social Captions (Instagram & Facebook)

Ready-to-paste captions to put under the photo you take after each activity. There is **one caption per sport**, written to work when you post on Instagram and let it cross-post to Facebook automatically. Each one thanks everyone for the great time and points people to that sport's WhatsApp group or web page.

## How to use these

1. Pick the sport you photographed.
2. Copy the caption and post it under the picture on Instagram (it will cross-post to Facebook).
3. Tag the people in the photo, and tag any partner venue/club where relevant.
4. Swap in the day/venue if you want to make it specific (e.g. "this Friday up on Montjuïc").

A few conventions to keep it consistent:

- Always tag **@keepcalmbarcelona** so we can reshare, and keep the tone warm and inclusive - never pressure.
- Each sport links straight to its WhatsApp group join page: `keepcalm.fit/whatsapp/<activity>`. It's clickable on Facebook and readable on Instagram, plus there's a "link in bio" nudge for Instagram.
- **Keep it to five hashtags max** - Instagram only allows five, so each caption below uses exactly five.

Handles: Instagram **@keepcalmbarcelona** | Facebook **facebook.com/keepcalmbarcelona** | Website **https://keepcalm.fit/**
