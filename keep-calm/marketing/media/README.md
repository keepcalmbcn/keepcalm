# Media - Keep Calm (All Sports)

Visuals for general community marketing that represents Keep Calm as a whole, rather than any single sport.

## What works well

- **Variety collages / grids** - one image showing multiple sports at once (padel, volleyball, hiking, social) to communicate the breadth of the community
- **Big group shots** - the annual beach party, a packed Sunday padel session, a full hike group. These sell scale and belonging better than any single action photo
- **Candid social moments** - people laughing together post-game, drinks after a session, the welcome of a newcomer. The community is the product, not the sport
- **Faces and diversity** - a genuine mix of people, ages, and nationalities. This is a community for everyone and the visuals should show it
- **The Barcelona backdrop** - beach, city skyline, Catalan countryside. Tie the community to the lifestyle of living here

## What to avoid

- Overly polished or stock-looking images - authenticity matters more than production value
- Shots that look like only one sport or one clique, when the message is breadth and inclusion
- Anything posted without the consent of recognisable people in it (see the data privacy rules)

## Formats to keep on hand

- Square (1:1) for Instagram feed and Facebook
- Portrait (4:5 or 9:16) for Instagram stories and reels
- Landscape (16:9) for Meetup and website banners
