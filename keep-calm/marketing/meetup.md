# Meetup Description - Keep Calm (All Sports)

A general group/community description for Meetup.com.

---

**Keep Calm - Barcelona's community for English speakers**

We are a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona who want to stay active, make friends, and feel at home in the city. In just over a year we have grown to more than 2,000 members, all brought together by sport and good company.

**What we do**

There is something on almost every day of the week:

- Padel (Sundays)
- Tennis (Tuesdays)
- Ping pong (Thursdays)
- Beach volleyball (throughout the week)
- Monthly hikes in the Catalan countryside
- Running, football, yoga, and ski trips in winter
- Regular social events where members from all sports meet and mix

**Who it is for**

Everyone. All skill levels are genuinely welcome, from complete beginners to seasoned players. You do not need a partner, a team, or your own equipment to join in.

**Cost**

Most activities are free or just a few euros to cover court hire. There is no membership fee. Everything is run by volunteers and nothing is for profit.

**How to join**

Browse our events, RSVP to anything that takes your interest, and just turn up. To stay in the loop, join the WhatsApp groups for your sports at https://keepcalm.fit/meetup and follow us on Instagram @keepcalmbarcelona.

New to Barcelona? Come and find your people.
