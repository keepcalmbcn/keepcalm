# Luma Event Description - Keep Calm (All Sports)

A general group/community description for Luma.

---

**Keep Calm - Barcelona's community for English speakers**

Not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona, 2,000+ members and growing, all brought together by sport and good company. All skill levels genuinely welcome, no partner or team needed.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Something on almost every day: padel, tennis, ping pong, beach volleyball, hiking, running, football, yoga
- 📍 Venues across Barcelona, per activity
- 💶 Free or a few euros for court/venue hire, no membership fee
- 👥 All levels welcome, 18+

**How to join**

RSVP to whatever takes your interest, then join the WhatsApp groups for your sports at keepcalm.fit/luma.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
