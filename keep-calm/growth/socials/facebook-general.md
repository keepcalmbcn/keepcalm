English speaking sports & activities in Barcelona for all abilities 😎  

Hey - just moved to Barcelona? Or been here a while but still feel like you haven’t found your people?

https://keepcalm.fit/ is a not-for-profit sports community for English speakers in Barcelona - no fees, no memberships, no one making money. Just a group of people who wanted an easy way to stay active and actually make real friends in the city.

What’s on each week (all social, all levels welcome):

🏃 Mondays - Running (5k / 10k)
🎾 Tuesdays - Tennis
😴 Wednesdays - Rest
🏓 Thursdays - Ping pong
⚽ Fridays - Football
🏐 Saturdays - Volleyball
🎾 Sundays - Padel
🥾 Monthly - Hiking trips

There’s also loads happening ad hoc - especially volleyball, which somehow turns into a near-daily thing.

You don’t need to be good. Seriously. Most people are just there to have fun, meet people, and try something new.

What makes Keep Calm different:
🙌 Actually not-for-profit - no fees, no hidden costs
📲 Super simple - join WhatsApp, vote in a poll, show up
🤝 Community-first - we collaborate with other groups
❤️ Volunteer-run - built by people who care

The sport is just the excuse.

The real goal is helping people find their circle here - whether that’s someone to grab a drink with after football, join a weekend hike, or just not feel alone in a new city.

If that sounds like something you need - come try one event this week. No pressure.
