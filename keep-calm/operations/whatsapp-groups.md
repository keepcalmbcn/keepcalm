# WhatsApp Group Descriptions

Copy-paste descriptions for each Keep Calm WhatsApp group. Each includes a link to the WhatsApp rules and any sport-specific rules on GitHub.

Each description below sits in a code block, so you can copy it cleanly (use the copy button on GitHub) and paste it straight into the WhatsApp group description field, with no `>` characters or formatting to clean up.

## Internal & Governance Groups

These are invite-only / internal groups, not open to the general community.

### Keep Calm Core (General)
```
🌟 Keep Calm Core 🌟

An invite-only group for the heart of the Keep Calm community - members who've been active for 6+ months and/or who regularly attend or host events.

This is a space to connect across all our activities, share ideas, and help shape what Keep Calm becomes.

Welcome, and thanks for being part of the core! 🤗

📋 WhatsApp rules: https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/whatsapp-rules.md
```

### Keep Calm Helpers
```
🙌 Keep Calm Helpers 🙌

The internal group for our brilliant volunteers - the people who give their time to make Keep Calm better.

That includes content creators (photos, video, design), folks who turn up early to help run padel sessions, event helpers, and anyone pitching in behind the scenes.

You don't need to organise a whole activity to make a difference. Every hand counts. 🤗

📋 WhatsApp rules: https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/whatsapp-rules.md
```

### Keep Calm Organisers
```
📣 Keep Calm Organisers 📣

The internal group for anyone organising a Keep Calm activity - padel, hiking, tennis, social, and everything else.

Use it to share what you're planning, ask for a hand, coordinate venues, dates and equipment, and support each other.

Organising should be fun, not lonely. We've got you. 💪

📋 WhatsApp rules: https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/whatsapp-rules.md
📖 Organiser guide: https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/governance/organiser-guide.md
```

### Keep Calm Moderators
```
🛠️ Keep Calm Moderators 🛠️

The internal group for Keep Calm WhatsApp group moderators.

Here we coordinate the running of our groups - rules, moderation, membership clear-outs, and sorting out any issues that come up across the community.

Keep it constructive and keep it kind. 🙏

📋 WhatsApp rules: https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/whatsapp-rules.md
```

### Keep Calm Governance
```
🏛️ Keep Calm Governance 🏛️

The internal group for the bigger-picture side of Keep Calm.

This is where we discuss how the community is run, the future Keep Calm association, community-wide policy, and the decisions that shape our direction.

Thoughtful input welcome. 🧭

📋 WhatsApp rules: https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/whatsapp-rules.md
📜 Decision-making: https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/governance/decision-making.md
```
