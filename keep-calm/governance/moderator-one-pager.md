# Moderator - One-Pager

**The role in one sentence:** you keep the WhatsApp groups friendly and usable - welcoming new faces, steering chat to the right place, and acting on rule breaches so members don't have to put up with them.

## Rhythm

There is no fixed schedule - it's little and often:

- **Daily-ish**: skim your groups, welcome anyone new, tidy anything off track
- **When flagged**: respond to reports (screenshots of unsolicited DMs, spam, harassment) quickly - being responsive is most of the job
- **Ongoing**: keep pinned messages current with the group's actual routine

## Handled automatically (you do nothing)

- Session announcements, reminders, polls, and thank-yous in the sport groups
- Monthly community announcements, weekly group spotlights, and the daily group-discovery post
- Inactivity reminders and rotation

## Yours to do

- **Welcome new members** and point them at the pinned info and the group's readme
- **Redirect off-topic chat** to the social group or private messages, per the [WhatsApp Rules](../rules/whatsapp-rules.md)
- **Act on rule breaches**: referral links, event promotion, spam, negativity spirals - a friendly steer first, firmer if repeated
- **Handle unsolicited-DM reports seriously and fast**: thank the reporter, gather screenshots, act per the WhatsApp Rules - this is the one we never let slide
- **Keep pins current** so "check the pinned message" is always good advice

## You decide without asking anyone

- Deleting spam and steering conversations
- Friendly warnings and formal first warnings
- Strikes 1 and 2 for WhatsApp rule breaches
- Routine welcome and housekeeping messages

## Decide together with other organisers

- Strike-3 removals
- Changes to the WhatsApp Rules themselves
- New groups, merging groups, or changing what a group is for

## Escalate to the Community Lead only for

- Bans and appeals
- Serious cases (harassment, threats, sexual content) that may need immediate removal
- Anything technical: bots, automation, group settings beyond admin basics

## Handover

Stepping down? Follow the Stepping Down checklist in the [Organiser Guide](organiser-guide.md#stepping-down), and make sure another moderator has admin rights in every group you cover.
