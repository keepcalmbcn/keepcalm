# Content Editor - One-Pager

**The role in one sentence:** you own everything members and newcomers read that isn't a session post - Instagram, blog review, group descriptions, and website copy - in one consistent Keep Calm voice.

## Rhythm

- **Weekly-ish**: an Instagram post or two when there are good photos (session recaps, event promos)
- **When asked**: review blog drafts and website copy before they go live
- **Periodically**: sweep the WhatsApp group descriptions so they match how each group actually runs

## Handled automatically (you do nothing)

- All scheduled WhatsApp posting: announcements, reminders, polls, thank-yous, monthly community posts, group spotlights, and the daily group-discovery nudge
- You are not the session announcer - if a sport's posts look wrong, flag it rather than posting manually over the top

## Yours to do

- **Instagram**: captions and stories from event photos (check consent norms in [Photography & Media Consent](../policies/photography-media-consent.md))
- **Blog and website copy review**: tone, accuracy, and privacy before publishing
- **Group descriptions**: keep every WhatsApp group's description short, current, and pointing at the right links
- **Marketing copy upkeep**: the per-sport `marketing/` folders follow the [marketing folder template](../marketing/marketing-folder-template.md) - keep them in sync when facts change

## House style (non-negotiable)

- No em dashes, ever - hyphens, commas, or a new sentence
- Warm, not-for-profit, volunteer-run "we" voice, never "I"
- Instagram: maximum 5 hashtags
- All events and groups are 18+ - say it where relevant
- Safety/data/media notices stay short: link keepcalm.fit/policies rather than restating policy
- WhatsApp group links are always keepcalm.fit/whatsapp/(group) short links, never raw invite links
- No member names or personal data without explicit consent

## You decide without asking anyone

- Publishing Instagram posts and stories that follow the house style
- Copy edits to group descriptions and existing website text
- Which photos to use, within the media-consent policy

## Decide together with other organisers

- New pages or sections on the website
- Campaigns that commit organisers' time (a posting series, a push for a new sport)
- Anything announcing a policy or schedule change

## Escalate to the Community Lead only for

- Anything spending money (paid promotion, tools)
- Takedown requests and consent disputes
- Anything technical: the website build, automation, new platforms

## Handover

Stepping down? Follow the Stepping Down checklist in the [Organiser Guide](organiser-guide.md#stepping-down), and hand over account access securely.
