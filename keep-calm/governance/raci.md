# Who Does What (RACI)

A quick-reference map of who is responsible for what across Keep Calm. We use roles, never names - people change, roles stay. See [Role Descriptions](role-descriptions.md) for what each role involves and [Decision Making](decision-making.md) for how decisions get made.

This matrix describes where we are going, not only where we are today. Rows marked `(target)` are currently handled by the Community Lead but belong to a volunteer role - the matrix doubles as our delegation plan.

## The legend, in plain language

- **R - Responsible**: does the work. Exactly one R per row, so it's never "someone else's job".
- **A - Accountable**: makes sure it happens and signs off. Often the same person as R.
- **C - Consulted**: asked for input before it happens.
- **I - Informed**: told after it happens.

**Automation** is a column because a lot of our posting runs on scheduled systems. Where Automation is R, no human posts anything - the humans review, answer, and decide.

## How to read your column

- **Sport Lead**: your week is the "Weekly sessions" block plus strikes and welcoming. Your one-pager (`<sport>/organiser-one-pager.md`) turns your column into a checklist.
- **Booker**: one row, done well - book promptly, share the link, chase payment. See [Role Descriptions](role-descriptions.md#booker).
- **Moderator**: the groups' front door and referee. See the [Moderator One-Pager](moderator-one-pager.md).
- **Community Admin**: paperwork, appeals, and the membership machinery. See the [Community Admin One-Pager](community-admin-one-pager.md).
- **Content Editor**: everything members read that isn't a session post. See the [Content Editor One-Pager](content-editor-one-pager.md).
- **Community Lead**: everything nobody else owns yet, plus money, partnerships, removals, and the technical platform. The goal of this document is to shrink this column over time.
