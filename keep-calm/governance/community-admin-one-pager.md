# Community Admin - One-Pager

**The role in one sentence:** you run Keep Calm's paperwork and process - forms, permissions, association admin, and appeals - so organisers can focus on sessions and the Community Lead isn't the bottleneck.

## Rhythm

Mostly asynchronous, driven by requests rather than a weekly cycle:

- **When asked**: process permission requests, form tweaks, and paperwork within a few days
- **When an appeal lands**: pick it up promptly - people appealing a strike are anxious, speed is kindness
- **Periodically**: check the association paperwork calendar (renewals, filings) so nothing lapses
- **Periodically**: cross-check the Core roster against attendance records for the 6-month activity bar (see [Staying Core](../core/README.md#staying-core-the-ongoing-activity-bar))

## Handled automatically (you do nothing)

- All scheduled posting: announcements, polls, reminders, spotlights
- Inactivity reminders and rotation run on their own - you only get involved when a member queries their status

## Yours to do

- **Appeals**: hear the member's side as the uninvolved party, review with the organisers, communicate the final call, per [Decision Making](decision-making.md#appeals)
- **Association paperwork**: registrations, renewals, and filings (the working documents live in our private admin space, not this public repo)
- **Forms and permissions**: field requests for new sign-up forms or changes, define what's needed, and pass the technical build to the Community Lead
- **Membership queries**: inactivity, rejoining, and "why was I removed" questions
- **Core activity checks**: cross-reference the Core roster against attendance records, send a friendly private heads-up around month 4-5 if someone's coming up on 6 months with nothing recorded, and quietly lapse anyone who doesn't follow up - see [Staying Core](../core/README.md#staying-core-the-ongoing-activity-bar)
- **Records**: keep decisions and appeals outcomes written down where organisers can find them

## You decide without asking anyone

- Appeal logistics: who hears it, timelines, how the outcome is communicated
- Routine paperwork and renewals at their normal cost ⚙️ CONFIRM
- Answering membership-status queries per existing policy

## Decide together with other organisers

- Appeal outcomes (you run the process; the decision is shared)
- Policy changes surfaced by an appeal or a paperwork requirement
- Anything that changes what data we hold about members

## Escalate to the Community Lead only for

- Money beyond routine paperwork costs
- Legal questions and anything binding the association
- Bans (as opposed to appeals of strikes)
- Anything technical: form tooling, automation, the website

## Handover

Stepping down? Follow the Stepping Down checklist in the [Organiser Guide](organiser-guide.md#stepping-down), and walk your successor through the paperwork calendar and where records live.
