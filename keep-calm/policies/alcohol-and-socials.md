# Alcohol & Social Events

> **Policy** · Version 1.0 · Last updated 2026-06-27 · Part of the [Keep Calm policies](README.md).

Many Keep Calm events involve a drink, from post-game beers to dinners and parties. We want these to stay fun, friendly, and safe.

---

## The basics

- Keep Calm is an [adults-only (18+) community](safeguarding-age-policy.md).
- Drinking is always optional. Plenty of members do not drink, and socials are just as much for them.
- You are responsible for your own drinking and your own way home.

## Drink responsibly

- Know your limits and pace yourself.
- Never feel pressured to drink, and never pressure anyone else.
- Look out for each other. If someone has had too much or seems unwell or vulnerable, help them or tell an organiser.
- Do not drive after drinking. Plan a safe way home.

## Conduct still applies

Drinking is not an excuse for behaviour that breaches [Community Conduct](community-conduct.md). Harassment, aggression, or making others feel unsafe is taken just as seriously at a social as at a session, and can lead to removal from the community.

## Drugs

Illegal drug use is not part of Keep Calm events. Anyone using or supplying illegal drugs at an event may be removed.

## Venues

At bars, restaurants, and clubs, follow the venue's rules and respect their staff. Any tab or minimum spend is between you and the venue. See [Payments, Refunds & Cancellation](payments-refunds-cancellation.md).

## Getting home

Think about your route home before the night gets late, especially if public transport stops running. Share a taxi with others where you can, and let someone know you got home safely.
