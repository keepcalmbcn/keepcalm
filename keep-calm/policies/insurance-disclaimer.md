# Insurance Disclaimer

> **Policy** · Version 1.0 · Last updated 2026-06-27 · Part of the [Keep Calm policies](README.md).

A plain statement of our insurance position. This expands the insurance section of [Safety & Liability](safety-liability.md).

---

## Keep Calm does not provide insurance

We are a volunteer-run, not-for-profit community, not an insured organisation. We do not have:

- Public liability insurance
- Personal accident insurance for participants
- Equipment insurance
- Medical or travel cover for trips and hikes

When you take part in a Keep Calm activity, you are not covered by any Keep Calm insurance policy, because none exists.

## What this means for you

You participate at your own risk. If you are injured, or your belongings are lost, damaged, or stolen, Keep Calm and its organisers are not liable and cannot compensate you. See [Safety & Liability](safety-liability.md) for the full liability position.

## Cover you may want to arrange

This is your decision, but you may wish to consider:

- Personal accident or sports injury insurance
- Travel insurance for trips and hikes
- Private health insurance

And to check whether you already have cover through:

- An existing policy or your employer
- A sports federation or gym membership
- Public healthcare access in Spain (Tarjeta Sanitaria)

## Venues

Venues have their own insurance and liability policies, which are between you and the venue. Keep Calm is not responsible for venue conditions or cover.

## If we register as an association

If Keep Calm formalises as an asociación in future, that may bring insurance options. This document will be updated if that changes.
