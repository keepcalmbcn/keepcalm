# Meetup.com Event Description - Keep Calm Volleyball

---

**Saturday Beach Volleyball - All Levels Welcome**

Join us for a relaxed afternoon of beach volleyball on the Barcelona coast! We play every Saturday from 14:00 to 18:00 at the beaches around Barceloneta - Nova Icaria, Somorrostro, and Bogatell are regular spots.

**What to expect**

A friendly, social session with rotating teams so everyone gets plenty of game time. We mix skill levels across teams and keep things fun rather than competitive. Beginners are very welcome - experienced players are happy to help you get the hang of it.

**Skill level:** All levels, complete beginners included
**Cost:** Free - we use public beach nets
**Equipment:** Net and balls provided by the organiser
**What to bring:** Water, sunscreen, and comfortable clothes for the beach

**About us**

We're part of Keep Calm and Carry On - a not-for-profit volunteer-run sports community for English speakers in Barcelona. There are no membership fees. Games also happen during the week when members post ad-hoc sessions in the WhatsApp group.

**How to join**

RSVP here and head to keepcalm.fit for the volleyball WhatsApp group link, where all games are coordinated and last-minute updates are posted.
