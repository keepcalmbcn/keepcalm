# Meetup.com Event Description - Keep Calm Volleyball

---

**English Speaking Volleyball - All Levels**

https://keepcalm.fit/meetup/volleyball

Join us for beach volleyball on the Barcelona coast! We play at public beach nets, with a regular weekly session plus ad-hoc games posted whenever members fancy a game.

**What to expect**

A friendly, social session with rotating teams so everyone gets plenty of game time. We mix skill levels across teams and keep things fun rather than competitive. Beginners are very welcome - experienced players are happy to help you get the hang of it.

**Skill level**

All levels, complete beginners included. We mix ability across teams and rotate regularly so nobody is stuck on the sidelines.

**Cost**

Free. We use public beach nets, so there's no venue fee and no Keep Calm membership fee.

**What to bring**

Water, sunscreen, and comfortable clothes for the beach. Net and balls are provided by the organiser, but bring your own if you have one.

**How to join**

RSVP here on Meetup, then join our WhatsApp group at https://keepcalm.fit/meetup/volleyball - this is where all games are coordinated and last-minute updates are posted, and you must be in the group before you can come to a session. Day, time, and beach location are shared there, so read the pinned info or ask the group if you're not sure.

**About Keep Calm**

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona. Adults only (18+). Follow us on Instagram at instagram.com/keepcalmbarcelona and like us on Facebook at facebook.com/keepcalmbarcelona to stay up to date with everything we do.
