# Luma Event Description - Keep Calm Volleyball

---

**English Speaking Volleyball - All Levels**

Beach volleyball on the Barcelona coast at public beach nets, with a regular weekly session plus ad hoc games. Rotating teams so everyone gets plenty of game time.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Weekly plus ad hoc games, posted in the group
- 📍 Barcelona beach nets
- 💶 Free
- 👥 All levels, complete beginners included, 18+

**How to join**

RSVP here, then join our volleyball WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/luma/volleyball - day, time, and beach location are shared there.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
