# Facebook Post - Keep Calm Volleyball

---

If you've been looking for a reason to get out and meet people in Barcelona, beach volleyball might be exactly it.

We're Keep Calm Volleyball - part of Keep Calm and Carry On, a not-for-profit sports community for English speakers in Barcelona. Every Saturday afternoon we're out on the beach near Barceloneta playing volleyball from 14:00 to 18:00. It's free to join, we bring the net and balls, and all levels are genuinely welcome. Whether you played in school and want to get back into it, or you've never touched a volleyball in your life, you'll fit right in.

The format is relaxed - teams rotate so everyone gets equal game time and you're not stuck on the sideline. After the game there's usually a casual group heading for drinks nearby, which is honestly half the fun.

We also play ad-hoc games during the week when people are up for it. Someone posts in the group, others join, simple as that.

It's a great way to stay active, enjoy the Barcelona sunshine, and meet people from all over the world who are living here. No sign-up fees, no membership - just show up.

Head to keepcalm.fit to find the link to our volleyball WhatsApp group. See you on the beach! 🏐☀️
