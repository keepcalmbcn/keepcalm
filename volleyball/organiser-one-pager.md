# Volleyball Organiser - One-Pager

**The role in one sentence:** you make beach volleyball happen - the daily poll posts itself, you bring the net, set the meeting point, and keep the games friendly and mixed.

## Weekly rhythm

Games run throughout the week as members propose them, with regular Saturday sessions as the anchor:

- **Early afternoon daily**: the start-time poll goes out on its own - glance at the votes and confirm the plan if a game is forming
- **Saturday**: the regular session - arrive early enough to claim a net and set up
- **Anytime**: nudge spontaneous games along when someone posts "who's in?"

## Handled automatically (you do nothing)

- The daily start-time poll, posted early afternoon (it skips days where someone already made a poll)
- Community-wide posts: monthly announcements, group spotlights, inactivity reminders

## Yours to do

- **Confirm games**: turn poll votes into a concrete plan - beach, net, meeting time
- **Bring the kit**: net and balls, and coordinate who covers it when you can't
- **Run the session**: mix teams, rotate players in, keep it beginner-friendly per the [group rules](rules.md)
- **Answer questions** in the group: locations, format, what to bring
- **Welcome newcomers**: introduce them, get them into a rotation quickly
- **Day-of calls**: weather and wind, moving beaches if nets are taken
- **Apply strikes** only for real patterns (volleyball is drop-in, so be generous)

## You decide without asking anyone

- Which beach, what time, and the format on the day
- Team mixing and rotations
- Strikes 1 and 2 for genuine repeat offenders
- Weather calls (cancelled only if conditions are dangerous or unplayable)
- Spending up to a normal session's cost, e.g. a replacement ball; occasional indoor sessions at the usual small per-player venue fee ⚙️ CONFIRM

## Decide together with other organisers

- A regular indoor winter venue or anything with a recurring fee
- Adding or dropping the regular Saturday session
- Partnerships that touch volleyball

## Escalate to the Community Lead only for

- Money beyond the above
- Bans and appeals
- Anything technical: polls, automation, the website

## Handover

Stepping down? Follow the Stepping Down checklist in the [Organiser Guide](../keep-calm/governance/organiser-guide.md#stepping-down), and make sure the net and balls find a new home.
