# Keep Calm Women's Volleyball - Group Description

The following is used as the WhatsApp group description.

---

Keep Calm Women's Volleyball - part of the Keep Calm community for English speakers in Barcelona.

🏐👯‍♀️ A women-only beach volleyball group for players of all levels. A relaxed, supportive space to learn, play, and have fun on the sand, whether you have never touched a volleyball or you played all through school.

Why a women's group? Lots of women told us they wanted somewhere to start out and play together first, so we made it. It runs alongside all our mixed volleyball, not instead of it - you are very welcome at both.

Watch this group for session announcements with the beach, day, time, and meeting point. We rotate teams so everyone gets plenty of game time.

Cost: Free! We use public beach nets.

Open to adults only (18+).
