# Keep Calm Women's Volleyball - FAQ

Common questions about the women's volleyball group.

---

**Is this really for all levels?**

Yes. Complete beginners, rusty returners, and experienced players are all welcome. The whole point is a supportive space where you can start out or keep playing without pressure.

**I have never played volleyball. Can I still come?**

Absolutely. The beach format is forgiving, there are usually other beginners alongside you, and more experienced players are happy to help. Nobody is keeping score of your mishits.

**Do I need to bring anything?**

Just water, sunscreen, a hat, and comfortable beach sportswear. The organiser usually brings the net and balls. Most people play barefoot.

**How much does it cost?**

It is free when we use the public beach nets. Occasionally an indoor club session may have a small venue fee, which is always communicated in advance. There is no membership fee.

**How do I sign up?**

Watch the WhatsApp group. Sessions are announced with the beach, day, time, and meeting point. Just say you are coming so the organiser knows the numbers.

**Why is there a women-only group? Does this mean I can't play in the mixed sessions?**

Not at all. The women's group exists because many members asked for a friendly, women-only place to start out and play together. It runs alongside all our mixed volleyball - the Saturday beach sessions and the weekday games - and you are equally welcome at those.

**Is it safe? I don't want to turn up somewhere alone not knowing anyone.**

That is exactly why this group exists. Sessions have a clear meeting point and a named organiser, everyone is there to make friends, and you will never be the only newcomer. Bring a friend if it helps you feel more comfortable.

**Can I bring a friend?**

Please do. Friends are the easiest way to feel at home on your first session, and bringing one is very welcome.

**What if I sign up and then can't make it?**

Let the group know as early as possible so the organiser has accurate numbers. See the [Event Rules](../../keep-calm/rules/event-rules.md).

**Who runs it?**

Volunteer organisers from the community. Keep Calm is not-for-profit, so nobody is making money - it is all run by members for members.

**I have an idea or want to help organise. Who do I talk to?**

Post in the WhatsApp group or message an organiser directly. We are always glad of more hands, and women stepping up to host is exactly how this group grows.
