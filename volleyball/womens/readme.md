# Keep Calm Women's Volleyball 🏐👯‍♀️

Part of the **Keep Calm** not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona.

A women-only beach volleyball group for players of all levels. A relaxed, supportive space to learn, play, and have fun on the sand, whether you have never touched a volleyball or you played all through school.

---

## Why a Women's Group?

A lot of women in the community told us the same thing: they wanted a friendly, low-pressure place to start playing volleyball alongside other women, before or as well as joining the mixed beach sessions. So we set one up.

This group runs **alongside** all our other volleyball, not instead of it. Everyone here is just as welcome at the regular Saturday beach sessions and the ad-hoc games during the week. It is simply one more door into the sport.

---

## How It Works

- Sessions are posted in the WhatsApp group with the beach, day, time, and meeting point
- Anyone can propose a game - just say where and when and see who is in
- We rotate teams so everyone gets plenty of time on the sand
- The organiser usually brings a net and balls

---

## Levels

All levels are genuinely welcome:

- **Never played?** Perfect. The beach format is forgiving and there are usually other beginners alongside you.
- **A bit rusty?** Come and knock the cobwebs off in a supportive group.
- **Experienced?** You are very welcome, and newer players love learning from you.

We mix skill levels across teams so nobody is stuck on the sideline.

---

## Cost

- **Free** when we use the public beach nets
- Occasionally for an indoor session at a club there may be a small venue fee, always communicated in advance
- No membership fee - Keep Calm is volunteer-run and not-for-profit

---

## What to Bring

- Comfortable beach sportswear
- Water (there is often no shade)
- Sunscreen and a hat - the Barcelona sun is no joke
- A good attitude and a willingness to laugh off the early mishits

Most people play barefoot, but sand socks are nice if you have them.
