# Instagram Playbook - Finding Women for Women's Volleyball

A practical guide to using Instagram to reach women for Keep Calm Women's Volleyball. It pairs with [strategy.md](strategy.md) (the why) and [instagram.md](instagram.md) (the caption to post).

Instagram does two jobs: **getting found** by women already searching, and **going out to find them** where they already are. Do both.

The honest priority order: Reels for reach > Collab posts with women's/club accounts > genuine outbound engagement > story interaction tools > optional paid boost. Cold DMs last.

---

## 1. Make yourself findable (inbound)

- **Reels are the reach engine.** The feed barely travels to non-followers; Reels do. Post short, real clips: a beginner landing a clean dig, laughter at the net, golden-hour volleyball on the sand. The algorithm shows these to people with similar interests nearby, which is exactly the target.
- **Geotag everything.** Tag the actual beach (Nova Icaria, Bogatell, Somorrostro). Women browsing location pages and "near me" will surface you.
- **Hashtag stack in three layers** (discovery, not vanity):
  - Local: `#barcelona #barcelonaexpat #womenofbarcelona #expatwomenbarcelona`
  - Sport: `#beachvolleyball #womensvolleyball #volleyballgirls #barcelonabeach`
  - Intent: `#beginnervolleyball #meetpeoplebarcelona #barcelonacommunity`
- **Bio + link.** Bio should name "English-speaking sports community, women's padel & volleyball" with keepcalm.fit obvious. People who discover a Reel check the bio next.

## 2. Go and find them (outbound) - highest yield, most skipped

Engage from the Keep Calm account on accounts where Barcelona women already are:

- Women-in-Barcelona / expat-women communities, language exchanges, brunch and run clubs
- Beach volleyball and beach-lifestyle accounts
- Other women's sport groups in the city

How, without being spammy:

- Thoughtful comments and story reactions get noticed far more than cold DMs. When a woman clicks back and sees a warm, real women's group, that is the conversion.
- Look at the *followers* of those accounts. A follow plus a like or comment is a soft, non-creepy way onto someone's radar without a DM.

## 3. Partner accounts (the biggest single lever)

- **Collab posts:** co-author one post or Reel with a partner account (a women's community, a beach/sport account). It appears on both feeds to both audiences under one post. The cleanest way to borrow someone else's women audience.
- **Story shout-out swaps:** you mention them, they mention you. Low effort, high trust transfer.
- Beaches and lifestyle accounts want content and footfall, so pitch the win-win.

## 4. Story tools that pull people in

- **Polls / question stickers:** "Thinking of trying volleyball but nervous? 👀" - low-commitment taps that signal interest to the algorithm and open a DM conversation.
- **Location + hashtag stickers** on every story for extra discovery surface.
- **Countdown sticker** to the next women's session so people can tap "remind me."

## 5. DMs - carefully

DMs convert but read as creepy if cold. Keep them warm, group-branded, never one-to-one salesy. Best as a *reply* to someone who engaged first: "saw you liked the volleyball Reel - we've got a women's beginner session Saturday if you fancy it 🏐". Keep it non-pushy in line with our WhatsApp and conduct rules on unsolicited DMs.

## 6. If there is any budget

A small **geo-targeted boost** (women, 18+, Barcelona, interests: volleyball / beach / expat) on your best-performing Reel is cheap and laser-targeted. Even 20-30 euros on one good clip can outperform weeks of organic.

---

## Weekly rhythm (lightweight)

- 1 Reel from a real session (the reach driver)
- 2-3 stories around each session (before: countdown; after: a warm candid + "next one is...")
- 1 outbound engagement session: 20 minutes commenting and reacting on partner and community accounts
- Monthly: 1 Collab post or shout-out swap with a women's account

## What to measure

- Reel reach and saves (saves signal real intent)
- Profile visits and link taps after posts
- New women joining the WhatsApp group
- The real test: first-timers coming back for a second session

## Reminders

- Keep external / partner cross-posts neutral, not heavily Keep Calm branded where the host expects it.
- Warm, plain, human. Women-only and all-levels-welcome must be unmistakable.
- No em dashes anywhere (community style rule).
