# Luma Event Description - Keep Calm Women's Volleyball

---

**Women-only beach volleyball for all levels - relaxed, supportive, and beginner-friendly**

A friendly, women-only beach volleyball session on the Barcelona beaches, alongside our mixed sessions rather than instead of them. Teams rotate so everyone plays.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Sessions posted with the beach, day, and time
- 📍 Barcelona beach, meeting point shared in the group
- 💶 Free, public beach nets
- 👥 Women only, all levels genuinely welcome, 18+

**How to join**

Join our women's volleyball WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/luma to get notified when the next session is announced.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
