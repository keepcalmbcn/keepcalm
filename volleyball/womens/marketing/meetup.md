# Meetup.com Event Description - Keep Calm Women's Volleyball

---

**Women-only beach volleyball for all levels - relaxed, supportive, and beginner-friendly**

Join Keep Calm Women's Volleyball for a friendly session on the Barcelona beaches. It's a women-only group for players of all levels, a relaxed and supportive space to learn, play, and have fun on the sand, whether you've never touched a volleyball or you played all through school.

**Why a women's group?**

A lot of women in the community told us they wanted a friendly women-only place to start playing volleyball, before or as well as joining the mixed beach sessions. So we set one up. It runs alongside all our other volleyball, not instead of them - you're very welcome at both.

**What to expect**

- All levels genuinely welcome, with beginners usually playing alongside you
- A no-pressure, social atmosphere with teams rotating so everyone plays
- A clear meeting point and a named organiser, so it's easy to turn up even if you don't know anyone
- Sessions posted with the beach, day, time, and meeting point

**Cost**

Free - we use the public beach nets. No membership fee.

**What to bring**

Water, sunscreen, a hat, and comfortable beach sportswear. The organiser brings the net and balls. Most people play barefoot.

**How to join**

Bring a friend if it helps you feel at home. Join our women's volleyball WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/meetup to get notified when the next session is announced.

**About Keep Calm**

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona. Adults only (18+).
