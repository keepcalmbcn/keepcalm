# Instagram Caption - Keep Calm Women's Volleyball

---

Beach volleyball, sunshine, and a group of women who just want to have fun on the sand 🏐👯‍♀️

Keep Calm Women's Volleyball is a women-only group for players of all levels - never touched a volleyball or played all through school, you're equally welcome. It's a relaxed, supportive space to learn, play, and meet people, with no pressure and no scorekeeping on your first dig.

Lots of women asked us for a friendly place to start out together, so we made one. It runs alongside all our mixed beach sessions, not instead of them.

And it's free - we play on the public nets. Just bring water, sunscreen, and good vibes.

Part of Keep Calm - a not-for-profit English-speaking community in Barcelona, run entirely by volunteers.

Link in bio to join the women's volleyball group and see what's coming up next. ☀️
