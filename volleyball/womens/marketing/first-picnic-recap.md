# Recap - First Women's Volleyball & Picnic (10 July 2026)

Outcome note for the event listed in [first-picnic-meetup-event.md](first-picnic-meetup-event.md). Kept for the record and as a reference for future recap posts.

**What happened:** Keep Calm Women's Volleyball ran its very first session, a beach volleyball and picnic evening at Somorrostro Beach, organised by Jade. Over twenty women from all over the world came together to play, with drinks and snacks shared on the sand afterwards. By all accounts a big success and a strong first proof point for the group.
