# Meetup.com Event Listing - Women's Volleyball & Picnic (10 July 2026)

One-off Meetup event for the group's first ever meetup, not the evergreen group listing (see [meetup.md](meetup.md) for that).

**Heads up:** this is a same-day event (Friday 10 July, 19:00). If you're posting this to Meetup today, people will only see it a few hours before it starts, so treat this as short notice / for latecomers rather than the main promo push.

---

**Title**: English-Speaking Women's Beach Volleyball & Picnic - Somorrostro

**Date**: Friday 10 July 2026
**Time**: 19:00
**Location**: Somorrostro Beach, Barcelona

---

**Description**

Join us for the very first Keep Calm Women's Volleyball meetup: an easy, social evening of beach volleyball and a picnic on the sand.

This is a women-only group for players of all levels, so come whether you've never touched a volleyball or you played all through school. Two courts will be set up, teams rotate so everyone gets plenty of time playing, and nobody is keeping score of the mishits.

Afterwards (or alongside, if you'd rather sit this one out) we'll share snacks and drinks together as the sun goes down. Bring a little something to share if you can.

**What to expect**

- All levels genuinely welcome, with beginners playing alongside more experienced players
- A relaxed, social atmosphere, part volleyball, part picnic
- Two nets/courts so more people can play at once
- A clear meeting point on the beach, look out for the Keep Calm sign

**Cost**

Free. We use the public beach nets, no membership fee, no booking needed.

**What to bring**

Water, sunscreen, comfortable beach sportswear, and a snack or drink to share if you can. A picnic blanket or something to sit on is handy too. Most people play barefoot.

**Come solo or bring a friend**

Everyone's welcome to bring a +1. You won't be the only newcomer.

**About Keep Calm**

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona. Adults only (18+). Full policies at keepcalm.fit/policies.

For last-minute updates on exact meeting point, join the WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/meetup.

Follow us on Instagram at instagram.com/keepcalmbarcelona and like us on Facebook at facebook.com/keepcalmbarcelona to stay up to date with everything we do.
