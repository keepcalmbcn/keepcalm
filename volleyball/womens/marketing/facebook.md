# Facebook Post - Keep Calm Women's Volleyball

---

If you've been wanting to try beach volleyball but wanted a friendly, low-pressure place to start, this might be exactly what you've been looking for.

Keep Calm Women's Volleyball is a women-only group run by Keep Calm, a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona. It's a relaxed, supportive space to learn, play, and have fun on the sand, whether you've never touched a volleyball or you played all through school.

We set it up because a lot of women in the community told us the same thing: they wanted somewhere to start playing alongside other women, before or as well as jumping into the mixed beach sessions. So the group runs alongside all our other volleyball, not instead of it. You're just as welcome at both.

All levels are genuinely welcome. Never played before? Perfect, the beach game is forgiving and you'll usually have other beginners right there with you. Already play? You're very welcome too, and newer players love learning from you. We rotate teams so nobody is stuck on the sideline.

You don't need any gear or experience to start. The organiser brings the net and balls. Just bring water, sunscreen, a hat, and comfortable beach clothes. Most people play barefoot.

And it's free - we use the public beach nets. There's no membership fee, the whole community is volunteer-run and not-for-profit.

Nervous about turning up alone? Don't be - there's always a clear meeting point and a named organiser, everyone is there to make friends, and you're welcome to bring a friend along.

Head to keepcalm.fit to find the women's volleyball WhatsApp group and see what's coming up next. Open to adults (18+).
