# WhatsApp Post - Keep Calm Women's Volleyball

---

🏐👯‍♀️ *Keep Calm Women's Volleyball - come and play with us!*

A women-only beach volleyball group for players of all levels. A relaxed, supportive space to learn, play, and have fun on the sand, whether you have never touched a volleyball or you played all through school.

*What to expect:*
- All levels genuinely welcome - lots of beginners alongside you
- A friendly, no-pressure place to start out and meet people
- Teams rotate so everyone gets plenty of game time
- A clear meeting point and organiser, so it's easy even if you come alone
- Cost: free - we use the public beach nets

Lots of women told us they wanted a friendly women-only place to start playing, so we set one up. It runs *alongside* all our mixed beach sessions, not instead of them.

Part of Keep Calm, a not-for-profit English-speaking community in Barcelona.

👉 Join the women's volleyball WhatsApp group at *keepcalm.fit* to see the next session!
