## How It Works

**Monthly hikes** organized by **Jan**:
- **Frequency**: Approximately once per month
- **Day**: Usually Sundays (scheduled to avoid conflicts with major Padel events)
- **Duration**: Full day trips
- **Capacity**: 25-30 people per hike

## What to Expect

### Typical Hike Day
- **Morning meeting**: Early start (often 8:00-9:00am) to maximize daylight
- **Train journey**: 1-2 hours to the hiking location
- **The hike**: 4-6 hours depending on route
- **Shared lunch**: Bring food to share with the group!
- **Return journey**: Late afternoon/evening back to Barcelona
- **Optional**: Drinks or dinner together after

### Routes
- Selected from **Komoot** hiking app
- Varies in difficulty and scenery
- Routes clearly described in event announcements
- **Disclaimer**: Hike at your own responsibility - assess your fitness level before joining

## Tips for a Great Hike

- **Arrive on time** - trains don't wait!
- **Charge your phone** - useful for photos and navigation
- **Break in new shoes** beforehand - blisters ruin hikes
- **Pack light** - you'll carry it all day
- **Bring enough water** - better too much than too little
- **Tell someone** where you're going
- **Check route beforehand** - know what you're getting into

## Safety

- **You hike at your own risk** - assess your abilities honestly
- Stay with the group
- Tell Jan if you're struggling
- Bring basic first aid items
- Have emergency contacts accessible
- Follow Jan's guidance and instructions
