# Friday Football Launch Posts

Two WhatsApp posts to launch the new weekly Friday Football session at Satalia. The first match is **Friday 26 June 2026, 18:00**.

- **Post 1 (teaser)** goes out **this week** to build interest and excitement.
- **Post 2 (early sign-up)** goes out **next week**, giving the community first access to the sign-up link.

## Post 1 - Teaser (post this week)

---

⚽ *Big news, Keep Calm... weekly FOOTBALL is here!* ⚽

We're launching a brand new **weekly Friday football session**, and our very first match is *next* Friday, *26 June at 18:00* 🙌

Here's why you'll want to be there:

📍 *Satalia, up on Montjuïc* - honestly one of the most beautiful pitches in the city, with stunning views over Barcelona as the sun goes down
👫 *Mixed and for everyone* - ladies and gents, all levels, total beginners genuinely welcome. This is social football, not a league
🤝 *We've teamed up with Vaya Sports* - they sort the bibs, the balls, an on-site organiser, and the payment, so you just turn up and play
🍻 *Post-match drinks* - we'll head to a nearby bar afterwards for a relaxed drink and a chat

Teams are mixed on the day so everyone gets a proper game, and nobody is expected to be a pro 😄

This is our *first ever* weekly football event and we'd love to see a big turnout - come to play, come to watch, or just come for the views and the drinks. Everyone's welcome!

👀 *Sign-up opens next week* right here in this group. Keep your boots by the door and your Friday free... full details and the link are coming soon!

Who's in? Drop a ⚽ if you're keen 👇
