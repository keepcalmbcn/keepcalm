# Luma Event Description - Keep Calm Friday Football

---

**English Speaking Football - All Levels**

Friendly, amateur 8v8 football on a stunning pitch up on Montjuïc. Mixed teams, all levels, beginners and improvers especially welcome, run in partnership with Vaya Sports. Drinks after.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Every Friday, 18:00 kickoff (arrive 17:50)
- 📍 Satalia, Sants-Montjuïc (Carrer de Margarit, 23)
- 💶 A few euros per player, via the Vaya Sports app
- 👥 Mixed teams, all levels, 18+

**How to join**

RSVP here, then join our football WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/luma/football - that is where the weekly Vaya sign-up link and pitch confirmation are shared. Spots are limited by pitch size, so confirm early.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
