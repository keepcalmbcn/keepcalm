⚽ Casual football for English speakers in Barcelona - all levels welcome.

Keep Calm Football is part of our not-for-profit sports community. No league, no pressure, just friendly games at local pitches around the city. Whether you're a regular player or haven't touched a ball since school, you're welcome here.

Sessions are posted in a WhatsApp group - confirm your spot, show up, play. Costs are split equally between players.

Adults (18+). Find the link in bio to join the group and see what else we run.
