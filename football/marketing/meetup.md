## Full description

Paste this into the main description box.

> **English Speaking Football - All Levels**
>
> https://keepcalm.fit/meetup/football
>
> Friendly, amateur football for English speakers in Barcelona, on one of the most scenic pitches in the city.
>
> This is **social football, not a league.** Teams are mixed up on the day so everyone gets a proper game, and the result matters far less than turning up and having a good time.
>
> **Who it's for**
> Everyone. This is amateur football aimed at **beginners and improvers** - no experience needed, and you don't need to bring a team or any kit. We play **mixed teams (ladies and gents together)** and we'd especially love **more women to get involved**, so if you've been thinking about it, this is your sign. 🧡
>
> **What to expect**
> - ⚽ 8v8, mixed teams, all levels
> - 👕 Bring a black or dark shirt, plus a lighter one if you have it, so teams can be split easily
> - 👟 Footwear for the surface (boots or astros), water, and shin pads if you like them
> - 💶 Pitch fees are split between players (just a few euros each), paid through the Vaya Sports app
> - 🍻 We head to a nearby bar afterwards for drinks and a chat - come for the football, the social, or both
>
> **Run in partnership with Vaya Sports**
> We've teamed up with Vaya Sports, who book the pitch, provide an on-site organiser, and supply the bibs and balls. You sign up and pay your share through the Vaya app - the link is shared each week in our WhatsApp group.
>
> **How to join**
> RSVP here on Meetup, then join our WhatsApp group at https://keepcalm.fit/meetup/football - this is where the weekly Vaya sign-up link, pitch confirmation, day, time, and venue are shared, and you must be in the group before you can come to a session. Spots are limited by pitch size, so confirm early and only if you can commit.
>
> **About Keep Calm**
> Keep Calm is a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona, with more than 2,000 members and something on almost every day of the week - padel, tennis, ping pong, beach volleyball, badminton, hiking, running and social events. Everything is run by volunteers and nothing is for profit. Follow us on Instagram at instagram.com/keepcalmbarcelona and like us on Facebook at facebook.com/keepcalmbarcelona to stay up to date with everything we do.

>
> By joining this event, you agree that your personal data will be collected, stored, and processed by Keep Calm solely for the purpose of organising and managing this event.

## How to get there (add to description or "How to find us")

- **Metro:** L3 (green line) to Poble Sec or Paral·lel, then a short walk up towards Montjuïc
- Lots of members travel from across the city - ask in the WhatsApp group if you want to share the trip

## What to bring (Meetup has a dedicated field if you want it separate)

- Black or dark shirt (and a lighter one if you have it)
- Football boots or astro trainers, suitable for the pitch surface
- Water
- Shin pads (recommended)
- A good attitude

## Cost

- A few euros per player, depending on pitch and numbers
- Paid through the Vaya Sports app, not on Meetup
- No Keep Calm membership fee - volunteer-run and not-for-profit

## Attendee settings (suggested)

- Set an **attendee limit** to match the pitch (e.g. 16 for 8v8) so the event doesn't oversell
- Turn on a **waitlist** for when it fills
- Consider a guest limit of 0-1 so numbers stay predictable
