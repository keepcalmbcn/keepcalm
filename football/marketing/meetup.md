# Meetup.com Event Description - Keep Calm Football

---

Casual football (soccer) for English speakers in Barcelona. Part of the Keep Calm and Carry On community - a not-for-profit, volunteer-run sports and social network for the city's English-speaking community.

**What to expect**

Friendly, social football at local pitches around Barcelona. A mix of casual kickabouts and more organised matches depending on numbers. Teams are mixed up so everyone gets involved. This is not a league - it's a good-natured game with good people.

**Skill level**

All levels genuinely welcome. Some players are experienced, some are picking it up again after years away. There's no pressure to be any particular standard - just bring a good attitude.

**Cost**

Pitch fees are split equally between everyone who attends. The exact cost is posted with each session announcement.

**What to bring**

Appropriate footwear for the surface (boots or astros), water, and shin pads if you want them.

**How to join**

Join the Keep Calm Football WhatsApp group via keepcalm.fit/meetup - sessions are announced there with the time, location, and cost. Confirm your spot and show up ready to play.

Adults only (18+).
