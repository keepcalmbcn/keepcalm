# Media Guide - Keep Calm Football

Photos and visuals that work well for promoting the football sessions.

## Best Shots

- **Action on the pitch** - people mid-game, not posed. Movement, the ball in play, tackles (friendly ones)
- **Group shots after the game** - sweaty, smiling, relaxed. Authenticity over polish
- **The pitch itself** - especially at golden hour or with Barcelona architecture or hills in the background
- **Warm-up and kickabout moments** - standing around chatting, stretching, juggling
- **Mixed ability in action** - beginners alongside more experienced players, showing it's genuinely all levels
- **Close-ups of boots, ball, kit** - good filler content between event posts

## What to Avoid

- Overly posed or staged photos
- Shots where faces are unclear (ask permission before posting anyone clearly identifiable)
- Empty pitches with no people

## Format Notes

- Landscape works well for Facebook and Meetup
- Square or portrait for Instagram
- Short video clips (15-30 seconds) of in-game moments perform well on Instagram Reels
- Bright, natural light preferred - avoid heavy filters
