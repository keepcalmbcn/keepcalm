# Football Organiser - One-Pager

**The role in one sentence:** you are Keep Calm's face for Friday Football - Vaya Sports runs the pitch-side logistics and the posts run themselves, you keep the community side warm and working.

## Weekly rhythm

Games are Friday 18:00 at Satalia, in partnership with Vaya Sports:

- **Through the week**: share the week's Vaya sign-up link when it's available, answer questions
- **Friday**: be reachable around kick-off; ideally play, or make sure a regular is the point of contact
- **After the game**: steer people to the bar - the social half of the session

## Handled automatically (you do nothing)

- Reminder Monday, day-of post Friday
- Post-match thank-you Friday evening

## What Vaya Sports handles (you also do nothing)

- Pitch booking, payment collection through their app
- Bibs, balls, and an on-site organiser

## Yours to do

- **Share the sign-up link** each week and nudge when numbers are short
- **Answer questions** in the group: kit (dark shirt), footwear, how Vaya sign-up works (see the [FAQ](friday-session/friday-football-faq.md))
- **Welcome newcomers**: especially beginners and women - it's mixed, social football by design
- **Watch the vibe on the pitch**: reckless play or arguments are yours to calm per the [group rules](rules.md)
- **Liaise with Vaya**: flag issues (numbers, pitch, timing) and relay changes to the group
- **Apply strikes** for no-shows that leave the game short

## You decide without asking anyone

- Team mixing and on-the-day format tweaks with the Vaya organiser
- Strikes 1 and 2, and chasing replacements
- Weather calls in coordination with Vaya

## Decide together with other organisers

- Changing pitch, day, or the session's price point
- Strike-3 removals
- Changes to the Vaya Sports arrangement itself

## Escalate to the Community Lead only for

- Money beyond the above
- Bans and appeals
- Partnership contract changes
- Anything technical: automation, the website

## Handover

Stepping down? Follow the Stepping Down checklist in the [Organiser Guide](../keep-calm/governance/organiser-guide.md#stepping-down), and introduce your replacement to the Vaya contact.
