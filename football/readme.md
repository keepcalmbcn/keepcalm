# Keep Calm Football

Welcome to Keep Calm Football! Casual football (soccer) for English speakers in Barcelona.

Part of the larger **Keep Calm and Carry On** not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona, we're built on values of inclusivity, reliability, and collaboration. Everyone is welcome, and the community belongs to all of us.

## Quick Links

- [Group Description](description.md) - what this group is about
- [Group Rules](rules.md) - how we keep things running smoothly
- [General WhatsApp Rules](../keep-calm/rules/whatsapp-rules.md) - applies to all Keep Calm groups

## How It Works

- Sessions are posted in the WhatsApp group with times, location, and cost
- If you want to play, respond to confirm your spot
- Show up on time and ready to play
- Mix of casual kickabouts and more organised matches

## What to Expect

- All skill levels welcome
- Local pitches around Barcelona
- Friendly atmosphere - this is social football, not a league
- Teams mixed up so everyone gets a game

## Cost

Depends on the pitch. Any costs will be shared equally among players and posted with the session announcement.

## Getting Started

1. Join the Football WhatsApp group (links at https://keepcalm.fit/)
2. Watch for session announcements
3. Confirm your spot
4. Show up and play!

## What to Bring

- Appropriate footwear (boots, astros, depending on the surface)
- Water
- Shin pads if you want them
- A good attitude

## Questions?

- Post in the Football WhatsApp group
- Contact an organiser via the group
