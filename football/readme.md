## Our Regular Session: Friday Football at Satalia

Our weekly game runs **every Friday at 18:00** at **Satalia** (Sants-Montjuïc), in partnership with **Vaya Sports**:

- **Mixed, 8v8, all levels** - ladies and gents welcome, inclusive and social, beginners genuinely welcome. This is social football, not a league
- We've **teamed up with Vaya Sports**, who supply the bibs and balls, provide an on-site organiser, and collect the payment through their app
- You **sign up and pay through Vaya** - the link is shared in the WhatsApp group each week
- A stunning pitch up on Montjuïc with **views over the city**, perfect for a Friday evening
- We head to a **nearby bar afterwards** for 30 minutes to an hour of drinks and a chat - come for the football, the social, or both
- Bring a **black or dark shirt**, the right footwear, and arrive **10 minutes before kickoff**

Full details, including the seasonal pitch schedule and travel directions, are in the [Friday Football FAQ](friday-session/friday-football-faq.md) and [Venue](friday-session/friday-football-venues.md) pages.

## How It Works

- Sessions are posted in the WhatsApp group with the time, pitch, and cost
- If you want to play, respond to the poll or message to confirm your spot
- Spots are limited by pitch size, so confirm early and only if you can commit
- Show up on time and ready to play
- Teams are mixed on the day so everyone gets a fair game

## What to Expect

- A mix of casual kickabouts and more organised small-sided matches
- All skill levels playing together - this is social football, not a league
- Indoor or outdoor pitches (fútbol sala or 7-a-side) at venues around Barcelona
- A friendly, welcoming atmosphere where the result is far less important than the game
- A drink or a bite together after some sessions

## Format

Most sessions are small-sided games on a hired pitch:

- **5-a-side / fútbol sala** - fast, indoor or hard-court, smaller teams
- **7-a-side** - on a larger outdoor pitch when numbers and bookings allow
- Teams are balanced on the day and rotated so games stay fun and even
- We play rolling matches or short games, mixing up sides through the session

The exact format depends on the pitch booked and how many people sign up, and is posted with each session.

## Cost

Football pitches in Barcelona are hired by the hour, and the cost is split equally among the players who turn up.

- Typically a few euros each, depending on the venue and numbers
- The exact amount is posted with each session announcement
- Pay your share promptly - if you confirm and then drop out late, you may still owe your share, since the pitch is booked and paid for either way
- No Keep Calm membership fee - everything is volunteer-run and not-for-profit

## What to Bring

- **Footwear** appropriate to the surface - astros or trainers for hard courts and fútbol sala, boots for grass or 3G. Check the surface in the announcement if unsure
- **Water** to stay hydrated
- **Shin pads** if you like to wear them (recommended)
- **A light and a dark top** if you have them, so teams can be split easily
- A good attitude

## New to the Group?

You are very welcome, whatever your level. This is social football, so nobody is expecting professionals:

- Turn up, introduce yourself, and someone will sort you into a team
- Let people know if you are rusty or new and the games will be kept friendly
- The main thing is showing up and having a good time

## Safety and Fair Play

- Keep tackles fair and sensible - no reckless or aggressive play
- Warm up before you start
- All levels share the pitch, so be patient and inclusive
- If you pick up a knock, sit one out rather than pushing through
- Sort any disagreements over a call quickly and get on with the game

## Getting Started

1. Join the Football WhatsApp group (links at https://keepcalm.fit/)
2. Watch for session announcements
3. Confirm your spot via the poll or message
4. Bring the right footwear and water
5. Show up on time and play!
