# Friday Football - Venue

Our regular Friday session is run in partnership with **Vaya Sports**, who book the pitch, provide an on-site organiser, and supply bibs and balls. Below is the venue we play at and the pitch arrangement.

---

## Primary Venue: Satalia

This is our home base for Friday Football.

- **Venue**: Satalia (Sants-Montjuïc)
- **Address**: Carrer de Margarit, 23, Sants-Montjuïc, 08004 Barcelona - [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/?q=Carrer+de+Margarit+23+Barcelona)
- **Day and time**: Friday, **18:00** kickoff
- **Format**: 8v8, all levels
- **On-site organiser**: Julian Ricardo Lozano (Vaya)
- **Booking and payment**: Through the Vaya app - sign up for the game and pay your share there
- **Provided on the day**: Bibs and balls (Vaya supplies these)

### Pitch arrangement

The pitch we play on changes through the season depending on Vaya's bookings:

| Period | Pitch |
|--------|-------|
| Opening session (Fri 26 Jun 2026) | Half of the F11 pitch |
| June / July | F8 pitch |
| August | F7 pitch |

The pitch is always confirmed in the Vaya game listing and in the WhatsApp group, so check there before each session. (F11 / F8 / F7 refer to the pitch size, i.e. the number of players a full team would use. Our games are 8v8 on whichever pitch is booked.)

### The pitch and the views

Satalia is one of the most scenic places to play in the city. The pitch sits up on the Montjuïc side of Poble Sec, looking out over the rooftops of Barcelona, so you're playing with a proper view as the sun goes down on a Friday evening. It's worth coming for the setting alone, and it makes a great spot to bring friends along even if they only want to watch.

### Getting there

Satalia sits on the Montjuïc side of Poble Sec.

- **Metro**: L3 (green line) to Poble Sec or Paral·lel, then a short walk
- Plenty of members come from across the city - post in the group if you want to travel together

### Post-match drinks

The game is only half the fun. After we finish, we head to a nearby bar for a relaxed **30 minutes to an hour** of drinks and a chat. It's the social heart of the session, a chance to meet people properly, and everyone is welcome to join, whether you played or just came down to watch.

---

## Why Vaya?

Running the Friday session through Vaya means we get a reliably booked pitch, an on-site organiser to coordinate teams, and bibs and balls provided, without a Keep Calm volunteer having to chase a pitch booking every week. We just funnel the community to the game, and Vaya handles the logistics on the day.
