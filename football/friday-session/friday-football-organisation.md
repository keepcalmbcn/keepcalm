# Friday Football Organisation

The Friday session runs in partnership with **Vaya Sports**. Vaya books the pitch at Satalia, provides an on-site organiser, and supplies bibs and balls. The Keep Calm organiser's job is to keep the community informed and to make sure each week's game fills up.

---

## How the Partnership Works

- **Vaya** books the pitch, sets the price, runs the game on the day, and provides bibs and balls.
- **Keep Calm** promotes the game to the community, shares the sign-up link in the WhatsApp group, and keeps an eye on numbers.
- Players **sign up and pay through the Vaya app** - Keep Calm does not handle bookings or money for the Friday session.

This is a different model from our other sports (e.g. tennis, where a Keep Calm booker reserves courts on Playtomic). Here Vaya owns the logistics and we drive attendance.

---

## Weekly Timeline

### Early in the week - Confirm and get the link
- Vaya confirms the Friday game (pitch, time, price).
- The Vaya organiser sends the **game link** to the Keep Calm organiser.

### Mid-week - Post in the group
- Post the Vaya game link in the **Keep Calm Football WhatsApp group**.
- Include the key details: Friday 18:00, Satalia, 8v8, all levels, and a reminder to bring a dark shirt and arrive 10 minutes early.
- Pin the message so latecomers can find it.

### Through the week - Keep it filled
- Keep an eye on the sign-up count in the Vaya app.
- If numbers are low, give it a nudge in the group.
- If someone needs to drop out, remind them to **remove themselves from the Vaya game** so the spot reopens.

### Friday - Match day
- Send a reminder in the group on the morning of the game.
- Players arrive at Satalia at least **10 minutes before** the 18:00 kickoff.
- The **Vaya on-site organiser** sorts teams, hands out bibs, and runs the game.
- After the game: post a recap or group photo in the WhatsApp group - good for recruitment - and encourage people to sign up for the next week. Invite people to share their own photos/videos and tag @keepcalmbarcelona (#keepcalmbarcelona) on Instagram so we can repost.

## Notes for the Keep Calm Organiser

- The Vaya organiser is the **point of contact for anything on the day** (teams, pitch, kit). Direct player questions about the game itself to them.
- Keep Calm's [community rules](../rules.md) still apply on top of Vaya's game etiquette - conduct, the strike system for repeated no-shows, and the 18+ requirement.
- If the partnership details change (price, pitch, time, organiser), update [Venue](friday-football-venues.md) and the [FAQ](friday-football-faq.md) so the docs stay accurate.
