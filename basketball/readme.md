# Keep Calm Basketball 🏀

Welcome to Keep Calm Basketball! Pick-up hoops around Barcelona for English speakers, all levels welcome.

Part of the larger **Keep Calm** not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona, we're built on values of inclusivity, reliability, and collaboration. Everyone is welcome, and the community belongs to all of us.

## How It Works

Basketball is organised on an **ad-hoc basis** by members in the WhatsApp group, with a regular weekly game as the anchor:

- **Regular weekly game**: A set weeknight game that runs every week (see below)
- **Spontaneous games**: Someone posts "Anyone up for a game today?" and others join
- **Flexible format**: Usually 3 on 3 half court, but we adapt to numbers, 1v1, 2v2, or a shooting/practice session if it's quiet
- **Just show up**: Games are free, no booking needed

## Regular Weekly Game

The group voted for a regular weekly slot:

- **When**: Wednesdays around 19:00 (time can flex, watch the group)
- **Where**: Courts near Sants station (see locations below)
- **Format**: 3 on 3 half court, adjusted to numbers on the day
- **Bring a ball if you have one** - see the note below

## Where We Play

We mostly play at the public courts around **Sants**:

- **Parc de l'Espanya Industrial** (next to Sants station) - our usual spot: https://maps.app.goo.gl/rFX7qzTg17GjFLSp9
- There are a couple of courts in the area, so if one is busy or there's a fiesta set up, we move to the nearest free basket

Courts are public, free, and first-come first-served, so we sometimes share or wait for a basket to free up.

## Bring a Ball 🏀

This is the big one for pick-up basketball: **games only happen if someone brings a ball!**

- If you have a ball, bring it, the more the merrier
- Before heading down, it's worth checking in the group that someone's bringing one
- No ball, no game, so don't rely on someone else having it

## What to Bring

- **A ball** (if you have one!)
- **Water** - stay hydrated, especially in summer
- **Comfortable sports clothing and court shoes**
- **Good vibes** - most important!

## Skill Levels

**All levels welcome!**

- Complete beginners learning the basics
- Casual players just here for fun and a run around
- More experienced players

We've had plenty of "haven't played in years" and "I'm a beginner but learning fast" players show up and have a great time. It's social, not a tournament, so don't let skill level put you off.

## Cost

**FREE!** We use public courts.

## Organisers

Check the WhatsApp group for current organisers. They post the weekly game, set up events you can RSVP to, and keep things coordinated.

## Getting Involved

### If You're New
1. Join the Basketball WhatsApp group (links at https://keepcalm.fit/)
2. Introduce yourself in the group
3. Watch for game announcements and RSVP to events
4. Show up to a session - everyone is welcoming!
5. Don't worry about skill level - we're here to have fun

### Organising Your Own Game
No game scheduled and fancy a hoop?
1. Post in the WhatsApp group: "Basketball at [court] at [time] - who's in?"
2. Confirm who's bringing a ball
3. Share the location pin
4. Show up and play!

## Questions?

- Post in the Basketball WhatsApp group
- Contact an organiser via the WhatsApp group
- Check group announcements for upcoming games
