# Keep Calm Basketball - Group Rules

Welcome to Keep Calm Basketball! To keep things fun and fair for everyone, please follow these guidelines.

> **These rules add to - but don't replace - our community-wide policies. Please read these first if you haven't already:**
> - [WhatsApp Rules](https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/whatsapp-rules.md) - group communication, unsolicited DMs, referral links, spam
> - [Community Conduct](https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/policies/community-conduct.md) - respect, harassment, advertising, conflict resolution
> - [Event Rules](https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/event-rules.md) - commitment, cancellations, the strike system
> - [Safety & Liability](https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/policies/safety-liability.md) - participation at your own risk, the 18+ age requirement
>
> **You must be 18 or over to take part. Keep Calm is an adults-only community.**

## 1. Attendance

- If you say you're coming, make the effort to show up
- If your plans change, let the group know so others can plan the numbers
- Pick-up games live or die on numbers, dropping out silently leaves people standing around a court

## 2. Bring a Ball

- Games only happen if someone brings a ball, so don't assume someone else has it
- Check in the group before heading down that a ball is sorted
- If you have your own ball, bring it along - the more the better

## 3. Fair Play

- Mix up teams so everyone gets to play
- Balance skill levels across teams
- Be patient with beginners and help them learn
- Keep it friendly - this is a social game, not a tournament
- Call your own fouls honestly and keep it respectful

## 4. Court Etiquette

- Courts are public and shared - respect other players
- If a court is busy, share baskets or wait your turn
- Take your rubbish with you when you leave
- Keep noise reasonable, especially in residential areas

## 5. Safety

- Stay hydrated, especially in summer
- Warm up before playing to avoid injuries
- Wear proper court shoes to protect your ankles and knees
- If you're feeling unwell or picking up a knock, sit one out

## 6. Communication

- If someone posts a game, try to respond so they know the numbers
- Share the location pin so newcomers can find the court
- Keep chat friendly and welcoming - remember there are always new members finding their feet
