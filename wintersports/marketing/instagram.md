Barcelona to the Pyrenees in two hours. That's it. That's the pitch. ⛷️🏔️

Keep Calm & Ski/Snowboard On is part of our not-for-profit community for English speakers in the city. Every winter we organise group trips to Grandvalira in Andorra and resorts in the Pyrenees - and all levels are welcome, whether you've never clipped in before or you've been doing this for years.

It's self-organised, social, and genuinely fun. Join the group, find your people, and make the most of having world-class skiing basically on your doorstep.

Link in bio to join. See you on the slopes. 🎿
