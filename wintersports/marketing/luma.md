# Luma Event Description - Keep Calm Ski/Snowboard

---

**Keep Calm Ski/Snowboard Trip - Pyrenees or Andorra**

Self-organised group ski and snowboard trips within two to three hours of Barcelona. You book your own transport and accommodation; we help coordinate carpooling and meeting points on the slopes.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Winter season, trips proposed in the WhatsApp group
- 📍 Grandvalira (Andorra), Masella, La Molina, and others
- 💶 Lift pass ~€50-80/day, rental ~€30-40/day
- 👥 All levels welcome, 18+

**How to join**

Join the Keep Calm Ski/Snowboard WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/luma - that is where trips are proposed and coordinated.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
