# Keep Calm Climbing - Group Rules

Welcome to Keep Calm Climbing! To keep our sessions safe and welcoming for everyone, please follow these guidelines.

> **These rules add to - but don't replace - our community-wide policies. Please read these first if you haven't already:**
> - [WhatsApp Rules](https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/whatsapp-rules.md) - group communication, unsolicited DMs, referral links, spam
> - [Community Conduct](https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/policies/community-conduct.md) - respect, harassment, advertising, conflict resolution
> - [Event Rules](https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/event-rules.md) - commitment, cancellations, the strike system
> - [Safety & Liability](https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/policies/safety-liability.md) - participation at your own risk, the 18+ age requirement
>
> **You must be 18 or over to take part. Keep Calm is an adults-only community.**

## 1. Commitment

- If you say you're coming, try to be there
- Some sessions have limited spots or group bookings, so a no-show can cost the group
- If your plans change, let the group know as early as you can

## 2. Safety First

- Climb within your limits and warm up properly before you start
- For bouldering, learn how to fall and land safely - ask an experienced member if you're not sure
- Never belay on ropes unless you know how to, and always double-check knots, harnesses, and devices
- Be aware of the area below boulder problems so you don't land on or under someone
- You climb at your own risk and are responsible for assessing your own ability

## 3. Follow Gym Rules

- Every gym has its own rules - read the signs and follow staff instructions
- Pay your own entry directly to the gym
- Respect the venue, the staff, and other climbers using the walls

## 4. Look After Newcomers

- We get a lot of first-timers, so be patient and welcoming
- Offer to show beginners the basics: reading routes, falling safely, gym etiquette
- Never pressure anyone to climb above their comfort level

## 5. Share the Wall

- Don't sit under a problem someone is about to climb
- Take turns on popular routes
- Keep bags, shoes, and chalk out of the fall zone

## 6. Outdoor Climbing

- Outdoor sessions carry real risk and are only for those with the right experience and gear
- Only join an outdoor trip if you are properly equipped or explicitly partnered with someone experienced
- Follow the trip organiser's guidance at all times

## 7. Be Sound

- Cheer people on and celebrate the small wins
- Help tidy up rental gear and shared chalk
- Stick around for a drink after if you can - the social side is half the fun
