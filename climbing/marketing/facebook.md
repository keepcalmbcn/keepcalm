# Facebook Post - Keep Calm Climbing

---

If you've been meaning to try climbing, or you already climb and just want some people to do it with, this might be for you.

Keep Calm Climbing is an indoor bouldering and climbing group run by Keep Calm, a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona. We pick a gym, post the day and time, and meet up there to climb together.

Most sessions are bouldering, which is the easiest way in. There are no ropes, no partner needed, and no experience required. The walls are low with thick mats below, and the routes (called "problems") are colour-graded from easy to hard so you pick your own level. If you're new, someone in the group will happily show you how it all works.

We rotate between gyms around the city - Sharma Climbing BCN, Climbat La Foixarda, and others - and occasionally head out to the rock around Catalonia for those with the experience and gear.

One of the nice things about climbing is how social it is. There's a lot of resting and chatting between problems, cheering each other on, and drinks or food together after some sessions.

Cost is just the gym entry, which you pay directly. Indoor day passes are usually around €10-16 depending on the venue, with shoe rental a few euros on top if you need it. There's no Keep Calm membership fee.

The group is friendly and mixed - some people are experienced climbers, others have never touched a wall. Everyone is welcome.

We're a volunteer-run community with no commercial interest, just people who want to climb with good company.

Head to keepcalm.fit to find the climbing WhatsApp group and see what's coming up next. Open to adults (18+).
