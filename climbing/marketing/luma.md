# Luma Event Description - Keep Calm Climbing

---

**English Speaking Climbing - All Levels**

Indoor bouldering (ropes when the venue and group suit it) at Barcelona's climbing gyms, rotating venues, plus occasional trips to the rock outside the city. No experience or partner needed.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Sessions posted in the WhatsApp group
- 📍 Rotating Barcelona climbing gyms
- 💶 Gym entry paid direct, ~€10-16, shoe rental extra
- 👥 All levels, mixed ability, 18+

**How to join**

RSVP here, then join our climbing WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/luma/climbing - that is where the gym, day, time, and entry cost are shared for each session.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
