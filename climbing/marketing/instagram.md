# Instagram Caption - Keep Calm Climbing

---

Barcelona has a brilliant spread of climbing gyms, and we try to climb at all of them. Indoor bouldering, rope walls, and the occasional trip to the rock outside the city.

Keep Calm Climbing meets up at gyms like Sharma Climbing BCN and Climbat La Foixarda. Mostly bouldering, which needs no ropes, no partner, and no experience. All levels welcome, and someone will always show newcomers the basics. Lots of resting and chatting between problems, drinks after.

Part of Keep Calm - a not-for-profit English-speaking community in Barcelona, run entirely by volunteers.
