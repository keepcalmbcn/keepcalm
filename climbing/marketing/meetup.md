# Meetup.com Event Description - Keep Calm Climbing

---

**Indoor bouldering and climbing meetups around Barcelona**

Join Keep Calm Climbing for a session at one of Barcelona's climbing gyms. Most sessions are indoor bouldering, with rope climbing when the venue and group suit it, plus occasional trips to the rock outside the city. We rotate between gyms like Sharma Climbing BCN and Climbat La Foixarda depending on what the group fancies.

**What to expect**

- Mostly indoor bouldering - no ropes, no partner, no experience needed
- Friendly, mixed-ability group - all levels welcome
- Newcomers shown the basics by people who have climbed before
- Walls are low with thick mats below, routes colour-graded from easy to hard
- Plenty of resting and chatting between problems, with drinks after some sessions

**Cost**

You pay the gym entry directly. Indoor day passes are typically €10-16 depending on the venue, with shoe rental a few euros on top if you need it. No Keep Calm membership fee.

**What to bring**

Comfortable, stretchy clothing you can move in, water, and climbing shoes if you own them (otherwise rent at the gym). For rope sessions, bring a harness and belay device if you have them, or rent at the desk and let an experienced member know you're new to ropes.

**How to join**

Sessions are posted in the climbing WhatsApp group with the gym, day, time, and entry cost. Respond to confirm so we know numbers, then turn up at the gym and look for the group. Join our climbing WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/meetup to get notified when the next session is announced.

**About Keep Calm**

Keep Calm and Carry On is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona. Adults only (18+).
