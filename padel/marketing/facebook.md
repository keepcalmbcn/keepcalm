# Facebook Post - Padel

Suitable for sharing in groups like "Barcelona Expats", "English Speakers in Barcelona", "Barcelona International Community", etc.

---

If you've been looking for a regular social sport in Barcelona, this might be exactly what you need.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona, and every Sunday we run padel sessions for all levels - from people who have never held a racket to more experienced players. The whole thing is run by volunteers who just want to make it easy for people to get out, be active, and meet others.

Here's how it works: a poll opens each Thursday and closes Sunday. Vote for the time slots you can make, and we organise your game during the week. Everyone who votes gets a guaranteed spot - that's a real commitment we make to every member. Courts are at venues around Barcelona and Cornella, and the cost is around €5-8 per person.

After the games most Sundays there's a casual post-game meetup at a local bar - good food, cold drinks, and the kind of easy conversation that happens when you've just played sport together.

It's a genuinely welcoming group. People come on their own, at all skill levels, from all over the world. A lot of members have said it's one of the best things they found when they moved to Barcelona.

If you're curious, head to keepcalm.fit or search for Keep Calm Barcelona on Instagram and Facebook. Happy to answer questions in the comments too 👇
