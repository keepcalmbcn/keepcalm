# Meetup.com Event Description - Sunday Padel

---

Join us for weekly Sunday padel - open to all levels, organised by Keep Calm and Carry On, a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona.

**What to expect**

We run multiple courts each Sunday across different time slots, organised by skill level. Whether it's your first time picking up a racket or you're a seasoned player, there's a group for you. Levels follow Playtomic scoring: Beginner (first time), Improver (0-1), Intermediate (1-2), and Advanced (2+).

**How it works**

A sign-up form opens each Thursday. Vote for the time slots you're available, and we'll organise your court during the week. Everyone who votes gets a guaranteed spot - that's our commitment to every member.

**Cost**

Around €5-8 per person for 1 to 1.5 hours. Racket rental is available at the venue for €4 if you need one.

**Venues**

Padel Delfos, Up Padel & Tennis Cornella, and Aurial Padel Cornella.

**After the game**

Most Sundays we meet up at a nearby bar for drinks and food from around 20:00. It's a great way to wind down and get to know people.

**How to join**

Visit keepcalm.fit/meetup or follow us on Instagram (@keepcalmbarcelona) for joining instructions. Adults only (18+).
