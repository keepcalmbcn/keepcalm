# Meetup.com Event Description - Sunday Padel

---

**English Speaking Padel - All Levels**

https://keepcalm.fit/meetup/padel

Join us for padel - open to all levels, organised by Keep Calm, a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona.

**What to expect**

We run multiple courts across different time slots, organised by skill level. Whether it's your first time picking up a racket or you're a seasoned player, there's a group for you. Levels follow Playtomic scoring: Beginner (first time), Improver (0-1), Intermediate (1-2), and Advanced (2+).

**How it works**

A sign-up form opens regularly in the WhatsApp group. Vote for the time slots you're available, and we'll organise your court during the week. Everyone who votes gets a guaranteed spot - that's our commitment to every member.

**Cost**

Around €5-8 per person for 1 to 1.5 hours. Racket rental is available at the venue for €4 if you need one.

**After the game**

Many of us meet up at a nearby bar for drinks and food afterwards. It's a great way to wind down and get to know people.

**How to join**

RSVP here on Meetup, then join our WhatsApp group at https://keepcalm.fit/meetup/padel - this is where the sign-up form and all organisation happens, and you must be in the group before you can come to a session. Day, time, and venue are shared there, so read the pinned info or ask the group if you're not sure.

**About Keep Calm**

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona. Adults only (18+). Follow us on Instagram at instagram.com/keepcalmbarcelona and like us on Facebook at facebook.com/keepcalmbarcelona to stay up to date with everything we do.
