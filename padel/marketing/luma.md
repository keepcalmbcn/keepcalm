# Luma Event Description - Sunday Padel

---

**English Speaking Padel - All Levels**

Padel for all levels, organised by Keep Calm. Multiple courts across time slots, organised by skill level (Playtomic scoring), with drinks after at a nearby bar.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Sundays, sign-up poll opens in the WhatsApp group
- 📍 Barcelona padel courts (rotating)
- 💶 ~€5-8 per person, racket rental €4
- 👥 All levels (Beginner to Advanced), 18+

**How to join**

RSVP here, then join our padel WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/luma/padel - that is where the sign-up form and all organisation happens.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
