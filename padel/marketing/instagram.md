Sunday padel with people who get it. Whether you've never touched a racket or you're a regular on the courts, there's a game for you.

Every week we run sessions for all levels across Barcelona - beginner, improver, intermediate, advanced. Everyone who votes in our poll gets a guaranteed spot. After the games, most Sundays end with drinks and food at a local bar.

Not-for-profit, volunteer-run, and genuinely open to everyone. If you're looking for a way to be active and actually meet people in Barcelona, this is a good place to start.
