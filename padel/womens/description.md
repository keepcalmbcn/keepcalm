# Keep Calm Women's Padel - Group Description

The following is used as the WhatsApp group description.

---

Keep Calm Women's Padel - part of the Keep Calm community for English speakers in Barcelona.

👸🏽 A women-only padel group for players of all levels. A relaxed, supportive space to learn, play, and improve without any pressure, whether you have never picked up a racket or you play every week.

Why a women's group? Lots of women told us they wanted somewhere to start out and play together first, so we made it. It runs alongside all our mixed padel sessions, not instead of them - you are very welcome at both.

Watch this group for session announcements including the court, day, time, and cost. Sign-ups are usually via a poll or Playtomic link pinned in the group.

Open to adults only (18+).
