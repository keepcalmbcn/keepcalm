# Keep Calm Women's Padel - Playtomic Outreach Message

A short, warm message for reaching out to women padel players on Playtomic and inviting them to the Women's Padel group.

---

Hi! 👋

I'm part of Keep Calm, a community of friends in Barcelona, English speakers who play sport and bring people together. 💛

We have a women's padel group for all levels (beginners very welcome!), super relaxed and supportive. We do ad hoc ladies games, ladies training, and ladies americanas.

There's no money in this for anyone, we're not-for-profit and fully volunteer-run, just friends doing it from the heart. No membership fee, you only chip in for the court (~€8-12).

Would love to have you - join via this link or reach out to me!

https://keepcalm.fit/womans-padel
