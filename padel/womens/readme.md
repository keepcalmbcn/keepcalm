# Keep Calm Women's Padel 👸🏽

Part of the **Keep Calm** not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona.

A women-only padel group for players of all levels. A relaxed, supportive space to learn, play, and improve, whether you have never held a racket or you play every week.

---

## Why a Women's Group?

A lot of women in the community told us the same thing: they wanted a friendly place to start playing padel alongside other women before, or as well as, jumping into the mixed sessions. So we set one up.

This group runs **alongside** all our other padel sessions, not instead of them. Everyone here is just as welcome at Sunday Padel, the americanas, the level groups, and the beginner sessions. It is simply one more door into the sport.

---

## How It Works

- Sessions are posted in the WhatsApp group with the court, day, time, and cost
- Sign-ups are usually via a poll or a Playtomic link pinned in the group
- Spaces are limited by court size, so sign up early and only if you can commit
- Turn up at the club a few minutes before your court time

---

## Levels

All levels are genuinely welcome:

- **Never played?** Perfect. This is one of the easiest places to start, and there are usually other beginners alongside you.
- **Improver or intermediate?** Come and get regular games in a supportive group.
- **Experienced?** You are very welcome, and beginners love learning from you.

If you are brand new to padel entirely, you may also enjoy our [Absolute Beginners](../absolute-beginners/) sessions, which are built around first-timers.

---

## Cost

- Typically around €8-12 per person to cover court hire, depending on the club
- Exact cost is posted with each session
- No membership fee - Keep Calm is volunteer-run and not-for-profit

---

## What to Bring

- Comfortable sportswear and clean trainers (court shoes ideal)
- A racket if you own one - if not, say so in the group, as spares can usually be sorted
- Water
- A good attitude and a willingness to laugh off the early mishaps
