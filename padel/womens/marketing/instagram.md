# Instagram Caption - Keep Calm Women's Padel

---

Padel is one of the easiest sports to fall in love with, and one of the friendliest places to start is right here. Keep Calm Women's Padel is a women-only group for players of all levels - never held a racket or play every week, you're equally welcome.

It's a relaxed, supportive space to learn, play, and improve with no pressure. Lots of women asked us for a friendly place to start out, so we made one. It runs alongside all our mixed padel sessions, not instead of them.

No racket? You can borrow one. Cost is just court hire, around €8-12 per person.

Part of Keep Calm - a not-for-profit English-speaking community in Barcelona, run entirely by volunteers.
