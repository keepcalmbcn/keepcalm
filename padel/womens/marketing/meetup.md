# Meetup.com Event Description - Keep Calm Women's Padel

---

**Women-only padel for all levels - relaxed, supportive, and beginner-friendly**

Join Keep Calm Women's Padel for a friendly session at a Barcelona club. It's a women-only group for players of all levels, a relaxed and supportive space to learn, play, and improve, whether you've never held a racket or you play every week.

**Why a women's group?**

A lot of women in the community told us they wanted a friendly women-only place to start playing padel, before or as well as joining the mixed sessions. So we set one up. It runs alongside all our other padel sessions, not instead of them - you're very welcome at both.

**What to expect**

- All levels genuinely welcome, with beginners usually playing alongside you
- A no-pressure, supportive atmosphere
- Sessions posted with the court, day, time, and cost

**Cost**

Just court hire, typically around €8-12 per person depending on the club. No membership fee.

**What to bring**

Comfortable sportswear, clean trainers (court shoes ideal), and water. Bring a racket if you own one. If you don't, say so in the group and spares can usually be sorted.

**How to join**

Sign-ups are usually via a poll or a Playtomic link pinned in the group. Spaces are limited by court size, so sign up early. Join our women's padel WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit to get notified when the next session is announced.

**About Keep Calm**

Keep Calm and Carry On is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona. Adults only (18+).
