# WhatsApp Post - Keep Calm Women's Padel

---

👸🏽 *Keep Calm Women's Padel - come and play with us!*

A women-only padel group for players of all levels. A relaxed, supportive space to learn, play, and improve, whether you have never held a racket or you play every week.

*What to expect:*
- All levels genuinely welcome - lots of beginners alongside you
- A friendly, no-pressure place to start out and improve
- Rackets can be borrowed if you do not have one
- Cost: around €8-12 per person for court hire

Lots of women told us they wanted a friendly women-only place to start playing, so we set one up. It runs *alongside* all our mixed padel sessions, not instead of them.

Part of Keep Calm, a not-for-profit English-speaking community in Barcelona.

👉 Join the women's padel WhatsApp group at *keepcalm.fit* to see the next session!

18+ only.
