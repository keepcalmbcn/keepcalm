# Luma Event Description - Keep Calm Women's Padel

---

**Women-only padel for all levels - relaxed, supportive, and beginner-friendly**

A friendly, women-only padel session at a Barcelona club, alongside our other padel sessions rather than instead of them. A relaxed, supportive space to learn, play, and improve.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Sessions posted with the court, day, and time
- 📍 A Barcelona padel club
- 💶 ~€8-12 court hire, no membership fee
- 👥 Women only, all levels genuinely welcome, 18+

**How to join**

Sign-ups are usually via a poll or Playtomic link pinned in the group. Join our women's padel WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/luma to get notified when the next session is announced.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
