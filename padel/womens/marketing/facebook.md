# Facebook Post - Keep Calm Women's Padel

---

If you've been curious about padel but wanted a friendly, low-pressure place to start, this might be exactly what you've been looking for.

Keep Calm Women's Padel is a women-only group run by Keep Calm, a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona. It's a relaxed, supportive space to learn, play, and improve, whether you've never held a racket or you already play every week.

We set it up because a lot of women in the community told us the same thing: they wanted somewhere to start playing alongside other women, before or as well as jumping into the mixed sessions. So the group runs alongside all our other padel, not instead of it. You're just as welcome at both.

All levels are genuinely welcome. Never played before? Perfect, you'll usually have other beginners right there with you. Already play regularly? You're very welcome too, and newer players love learning from you.

You don't need your own gear to start. If you don't have a racket, just say so in the group and spares can usually be sorted. Bring comfortable sportswear, clean trainers, and some water.

Cost is just court hire, typically around €8-12 per person depending on the club. There's no membership fee, the whole community is volunteer-run and not-for-profit.

Head to keepcalm.fit to find the women's padel WhatsApp group and see what's coming up next. Open to adults (18+).
