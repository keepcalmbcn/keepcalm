# Keep Calm Women's Padel - FAQ

Common questions about the women's padel group.

---

**Is this really for all levels?**

Yes. Complete beginners, improvers, and experienced players are all welcome. The whole point is a supportive space where you can start out or keep playing without pressure.

**I have never played padel. Can I still come?**

Absolutely - lots of members started with their very first ever game in this group. There are usually other beginners alongside you, and more experienced players are happy to help. If you want a session built entirely around first-timers, also look at our [Absolute Beginners](../absolute-beginners/) sessions.

**Do I need my own racket?**

No. If you do not have one, just say so in the group and a spare can usually be arranged. If you have your own, bring it.

**What should I wear?**

Comfortable sportswear and clean trainers. Proper court shoes are ideal but not essential to start.

**How much does it cost?**

Usually around €8-12 per person to cover court hire, depending on the club. The exact cost is posted with each session. There is no membership fee.

**How do I sign up?**

Watch the WhatsApp group. Sessions are announced with a poll or a Playtomic link to sign up. Spaces are limited by court size, so sign up early.

**Why is there a women-only group? Does this mean I can't play in the mixed sessions?**

Not at all. The women's group exists because many members asked for a friendly, women-only place to start out and play together. It runs alongside all our mixed padel sessions, and you are equally welcome at those - Sunday Padel, the americanas, the level groups, and the beginner sessions.

**What if I sign up and then can't make it?**

Let the group know as early as possible so someone on the waiting list can take your place. Spaces are limited, so reliability matters. See the [Event Rules](../../keep-calm/rules/event-rules.md).

**Who runs it?**

Volunteer organisers from the community. Keep Calm is not-for-profit, so nobody is making money - it is all run by members for members.

**I have an idea or want to help organise. Who do I talk to?**

Post in the WhatsApp group or message an organiser directly. We are always glad of more hands.
