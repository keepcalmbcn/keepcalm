🎾 HOW IT WORKS (the short version)
* Starting pairs will be posted here before the event in the morning - find your name, find your court! 👇
* No fixed pairs - partners and opponents rotate every round
* Rounds are 15 minutes - YOU keep track of your own games during each round 📝
* When you hear the whistle 📣 the round is over - rotate (Move down numerically if you win, up if you lose)
* Golden point at deuce (40-40) (no advantage!)
* Win your round and you move towards Court 1 - that's where the Queen will be crowned 👑
* Court numbers are on the pictures below - they're NOT in walking order, so check the layout!
