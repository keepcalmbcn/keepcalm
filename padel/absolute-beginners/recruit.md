Hey everyone! we are organising an *absolute beginner padel session on 19th April* and looking for *leaders* and *helpers* to make it happen.

*Leaders* guide a group of 4 beginners through their first session on court — teaching the basics and keeping it fun. *Helpers* support with logistics on the day (arrivals, racket rentals, coordination).

These sessions are how we grow our community from the ground up — giving complete beginners their first taste of padel and helping Keep Calm thrive for years to come. By getting involved, you could be changing someone's life. For many people, padel becomes a sport for life, and it all started with a session just like this. A great way to give back!

Both roles are voluntary — your time and energy is what makes this possible. If you're an intermediate or advanced player, we'd especially love for you to get involved if you have the capacity! In return, it's a brilliant chance to meet new people, strengthen your connection to the community, and be part of something that genuinely matters. No advanced padel skills needed, just enthusiasm!

📍 *Aurial Pàdel Cornellà* — 13:00 to 14:00, with the usual Sunday session to follow straight after!
