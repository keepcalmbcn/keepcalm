# End of Session Speech – Key Points

## 1. Thank Everyone for Coming
- “Thank you all so much for coming today!”
- “We had over 100 people interested – what an incredible turnout!”
- “I hope you had fun and learned something new, met new friends!”

## 2. Thank the Volunteers
- “Huge thank you to our volunteers court leaders and helpers – please give them a round of applause!”
- “They’ve given up their time to help you learn – none of this happens without them.”
- “If your leader was great please do tell them and keep in touch!”

## 3. About *Keep Calm and Carry On*
- *Keep Calm and Carry On* is a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona.
- We’re all about sports and social connection — not competition.
- We run: Padel / Volleyball / Ping pong / Hiking / Tennis / Badminton / Social events
- **Our values:**
  - Inclusive (all levels welcome)
  - Collaborative
  - Non-commercial (people, not profit)

## 4. What’s Next for Beginners
- “Don’t be afraid to join our regular sessions — we have beginner-friendly courts. Saturday nad Sunday”
- “Connect with the people you played with today — follow each other on Playtomic.”
- “Practice together join keep calm events together!”

## 6. Stay for Drinks
- “Please stay for drinks at the terrace!”
- “This is a social community — the best part is meeting people.”

## 7. Close
- “Thanks again for being part of this — we’re so glad you came!”
- “See you at the next session!”
