🙋 *HELPER ROLE GUIDE*

Thanks for volunteering! Helpers support the event without needing padel expertise. You don't need to know how to play - just be welcoming and helpful!

⏰ *BEFORE (12:30-13:00)*
• Attend the briefing
• Learn the venue layout (courts, toilets, bar, reception)

🚪 *DURING ARRIVAL (12:30-13:00)*
• **Station yourself at reception** to greet players as they arrive
• **Greet everyone and ask if they need a racket:**
  - No racket needed → take them directly to their leader at the outside bar area
  - Needs a racket → direct them to reception first (€2 + ID), then take them to their leader
• Help with racket rentals at reception (€2 + ID required)
• Point out toilets, water fountains, where to store bags
• Answer questions about Keep Calm
• Introduce anyone standing alone to others

🎾 *DURING SESSION (13:00-14:00)*
• **Collect balls from courts at the start of the session** and distribute the right number per court
• Float around - check if leaders need anything (balls/help)
• Help anyone who looks confused or turns up late
• Assist with equipment issues
• **Collect all balls from courts at the end of the session**

📸 *AFTER SESSION (14:00+)*
• **Go court to court rallying players towards the outside bar area** for the group photo and speech
• Help gather everyone together and make sure no court is left behind
• Encourage people to stay for drinks/social
• Tell people about Keep Calm's Sunday Padel
• Help players exchange contacts
• Remind people to join WhatsApp
• Thank people for coming!

💡 *TIPS*
• Wear something identifiable
• A BIG smile goes a long way! 😊
