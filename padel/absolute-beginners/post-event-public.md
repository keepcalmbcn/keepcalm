🌟 *ABOUT KEEP CALM AND CARRY ON*
Keep Calm is a *not-for-profit community* for English speakers in Barcelona who want to stay active, meet new people, and have fun through sports.

Our values:
• *Inclusive* - Everyone welcome, no matter your skill level or background
• *Collaborative* - We work with other communities in Barcelona
• *Non-commercial* - This is about people, not profit
