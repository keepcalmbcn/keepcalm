## Day of Event

### Arrival & Setup
- **Leaders arrive 10-15 minutes before players** to be ready to receive them
- Agree on a meeting location at the start (outside bar area)
- Players must go to reception and pay for their place
- **Players must note their court number and leader name at reception**
- Players can rent a racket for 2 euros at reception (bring ID as they may ask for it)
- **After check-in, players go to the outside bar area to meet their leader and other players**

### During the Session
- Leaders should ensure player experience is good and minimize players standing around
- Make sure games are set to non-competitive and scores are not uploaded

### After the Session
- **At the end of the hour, leaders bring all players back to the outside bar area for group photo** (will be used for social media and promoting the next beginner session)
- Meet at agreed location after games
- Thank everyone for coming and thank volunteers for volunteering their time
- Tell everyone about Keep Calm and the wider community
- **Consider asking venue to pre-set up a group of tables at the bar to encourage socializing**
- Encourage players to stay for drinks and meet other community members
- **Encourage beginners to exchange contact information and follow each other on Playtomic** - this helps them arrange future games together as they progress

## Post-Event Follow-Up
- Thank all volunteers and players
- Setup and post feedback form
- Schedule next absolute beginner session (e.g., for January) and point old form to new form


## Teaching Guide for Volunteer Leaders

### Key Responsibilities
- **Arrive 10-15 minutes before session start time** to meet players as they arrive
- Understand player experience is the priority
- Minimize players standing around
- Ensure rotation and engagement
- **At the end of the hour, bring players back to the outside bar area for the group photo**
- **Tell players about the main Keep Calm padel community and how we organize regular games through the Google Form system**
- **Encourage players to make friends and swap contact details with other beginners** - recommend they follow each other on Playtomic so they can arrange games together in future

### Topics to Cover with Beginners
1. **How to hold the racket** - Proper grip technique
2. **How to serve** - Basic serving technique and rules
3. **The basic rules** - Scoring, faults, let serves
4. **Court zones** - Where you can/cannot play from
5. **Playing off the glass/metal** - How the walls work in padel
6. **Point system** - How scoring works in padel
7. **When to move/switch sides** - Court positioning and rotation
8. **About Keep Calm Padel** - What is our community, how to join and play
