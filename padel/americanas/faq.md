# Americana FAQ

Answers to common questions about Keep Calm Americana events.

---

**What is an Americana?**
An Americana is a friendly mini tournament where you play short 15-minute matches and rotate partners and courts between each round. It's not a serious competition — it's a great way to play with a lot of different people and find your level naturally.

**How does the rotation work?**
After each 15-minute match, the winning pair splits up and the losing pair splits up. Winners move up one court, losers move down one court. You get a new partner every single round. After a few rounds the courts self-regulate so you're roughly always playing at your own level.

**What level do I need to be?**
Our americanas are open to *Improver through to Advanced* level players, and beginners are also welcome. The rotation system means you'll naturally end up on a court suited to your level within a few rounds.

**How many people play?**
We have 32 spots across 8 courts (4 players per court).

**Is it competitive?**
No — it's friendly and social. There are no prizes for winning, and the focus is on having fun and meeting other members of the community. That said, playing your best makes the experience better for everyone!

**What do I need to bring?**
- Padel racket (rental may be available at the venue if needed)
- Sportswear and trainers
- Water bottle
- ~€10 for court costs
- Good vibes!

**How much does it cost?**
Around €10, which covers your share of the court and includes a ball to take home.

**What happens after?**
We head to a nearby bar for celebration drinks — highly encouraged! It's a great chance to chat and get to know people outside of the court.

**Can I sign up with a friend?**
Yes — but starting court assignments are based on level, so you may not start on the same court. You'll likely end up playing together at some point during the session as courts mix.

**I signed up but now can't make it — what should I do?**
Please cancel your spot on Playtomic as early as possible so someone on the waitlist can take your place. Last-minute cancellations and no-shows affect the whole group and may result in removal from the community.

**How do I sign up?**
Via the Playtomic link posted in the WhatsApp group. Spots are limited to 32 and it's first come first served — sign up quickly!
