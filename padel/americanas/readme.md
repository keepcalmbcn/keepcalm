# Keep Calm Americana Padel

Part of the **Keep Calm** not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona.

---

## What is an Americana?

An Americana is a friendly mini tournament where you play short matches, rotate partners, and move up or down courts based on results. It's not a serious competition - the aim is to play with lots of different people and find your level naturally.

- **Format**: 15-minute matches across 8 courts (32 players, 4 per court)
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Level**: Improver to Advanced (beginners welcome)
- **Cost**: ~€10 per person (includes a ball to take home)
- **Sign-ups**: Via Playtomic, first come first served
- **After**: Celebration drinks at a nearby bar

---

## How the Rotation Works

1. Play a 15-minute match on your court.
2. At the end, the **winning pair splits up** - one moves up a court, one stays.
3. The **losing pair also splits up** - one moves down a court, one stays.
4. Everyone gets a new partner for the next round.

After a few rounds, courts self-regulate so you're always playing at roughly your own level. Don't worry if the first round feels uneven - it sorts itself out quickly.

---

## How the Golden Point Works

When a game reaches deuce (40-40), instead of playing out traditional advantage points, a single **golden point** decides the game:

- The **receiving pair chooses which side** they receive on (left or right).
- Whoever wins that one point wins the game - no second chances.

This keeps the pace fast and avoids drawn-out deuce battles during 15-minute matches.
