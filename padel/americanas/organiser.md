# Americana Organiser Guide

## Overview
- **Format**: Americana (round-robin mini tournament) — 15-min matches, rotating partners and courts
- **Players**: 32 (8 courts of 4)
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Sign-ups**: Playtomic tournament link, first come first served
- **Level**: Improver to Advanced (beginners welcome)
- **After**: Celebration drinks at a nearby bar

---

## Pre-Event

### 1. Book Courts
- Reserve 8 courts for 2 hours at the venue via Playtomic
- Confirm availability and court numbers with venue in advance

### 2. Set Up Playtomic Tournament
- Create a tournament on Playtomic and set capacity to 32
- Note the tournament sign-up link for the announcement post

### 3. Post the Announcement
- Fill in the `americana_post.md` template with: date, time, venue, price, sign-up link
- Post in the padel WhatsApp group
- Emphasise limited spaces (32) and first come first served
- Post early in the week to allow time to fill up

### 4. Manage Sign-Ups
- Monitor sign-ups on Playtomic
- Once full, post a message in the group confirming it is full and directing latecomers to the waitlist
- Chase for payment if required (payment is handled through Playtomic)

### 5. Plan Starting Court Assignments
- Review sign-up levels and assign players to starting courts (8 courts, 4 players each)
- Try to balance levels so the first round is fair — courts self-regulate from there
- Prepare a simple list or diagram to share with players the day before

---

## Day Before

- Send the day-before reminder (see template below)
- Confirm court bookings and numbers with the venue
- Share starting court assignments with players

---

## Day Of

### Before Players Arrive (arrive 15–20 mins early)
- Check courts are set up and ready
- Prepare timing (phone timer or watch for 15-min rounds)
- Brief any helpers if present

### Player Arrival
- Welcome players as they arrive and direct them to their starting court
- Encourage people to arrive 15 mins early — start on time regardless
- Give a brief explanation of the format (see FAQ or use the speech below)

### Running the Session
- Call time at the end of every 15-minute match
- Winners move up one court, losers move down one court
- Partners rotate each round (players split after each match)
- Keep it fun and social — this is a community event, not a serious tournament
- Be visible and available to resolve any disputes or confusion

### End of Session
- Announce final results/top court winners (optional but fun)
- Thank everyone and remind them about the drinks afterwards
- Take a group photo for social media

---

## Post-Event

- Post the post-event thank you message in the group (see `post-event.md`)
- Share photos on Instagram and community channels
- Note any feedback or issues for the next event

### Format Explanation (Verbal, for Day Of)

*"Welcome everyone! Quick overview for those who haven't played an americana before: we play 15-minute matches. At the end of each match, the winning pair splits up — one goes up a court, one stays. The losing pair does the same — one goes down, one stays. You'll always be with a new partner and playing against different people. After a few rounds the courts self-regulate by level, so don't worry if the first round feels uneven. Any questions before we start?"*
