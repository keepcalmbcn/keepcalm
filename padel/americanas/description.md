# Keep Calm Saturday Padel - Group Description

The following is used as the WhatsApp group description.

---

Keep Calm Saturday Padel - part of the Keep Calm and Carry On community for English speakers in Barcelona.

🗓️ Saturday padel in Americana (round-robin tournament) format. Play 15-minute matches, rotate partners, and move up or down courts based on results — levels self-regulate after a few rounds.

Open to all levels. Use the pinned poll to register your interest and level for initial court placement.

Cost: Your share of the court cost, paid at reception on the day.

Venue: Aurial Pàdel Cornellà.
