# Keep Calm Americana Padel - Group Description

The following is used as the WhatsApp group description.

---

Keep Calm Americana Padel - part of the Keep Calm and Carry On community for English speakers in Barcelona.

🎾 Americana (round-robin tournament) format. Play 15-minute matches, rotate partners, and move up or down courts based on results — levels self-regulate after a few rounds.

Open to Improver through to Advanced players. Beginners welcome. Sign up via the Playtomic link pinned in the group — limited spaces, first come first served.

Cost: ~€10 per person (includes a ball to take home!), paid via Playtomic.
