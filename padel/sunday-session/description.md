# Keep Calm Sunday Padel - Group Description

The following is used as the WhatsApp group description.

---

Keep Calm Sunday Padel - part of the Keep Calm and Carry On community for English speakers in Barcelona.

🗓️ Weekly padel every Sunday for all levels. Everyone who votes in our poll gets a guaranteed spot to play.

Form opens Thursday at 18:00 and closes Sunday at 22:00 (or at 80 responses) — vote for the time slots you can play that Sunday, and we'll organise your game during the week.

Levels are based on Playtomic scores:
- Beginner: First time playing
- Improver: 0–1
- Intermediate: 1–2
- Advanced: 2+

Cost: Around €5–8 per person. Racket rental available (€4).

Venues: Padel Delfos, Up Pádel & Tennis Cornellà, Aurial Pàdel Cornellà.

Post-game socials most Sundays at Caña + Tapa from 19:00 🍻
