# Keep Calm Languages - Group Description

The following is used as the WhatsApp group description.

---

Keep Calm Languages - part of the Keep Calm and Carry On community for English speakers in Barcelona.

A relaxed language exchange (intercambio) for people who want to practise Spanish, Catalan, English, and other languages over a drink and good company. Living in Barcelona is the perfect excuse to finally improve your Spanish, and the best way to do it is by actually talking.

How it works: we meet at a bar or cafe, pair up or form small groups, and swap languages. Spend a while in one language, then switch. No teachers, no pressure, no grades, just conversation and laughs while everyone helps each other along.

All levels welcome, from complete beginners to near-fluent. You do not need to be an English native, and you do not need to "be good" at anything to join.

Watch this group for meetup announcements including the venue, day, and time.

Open to adults only (18+).
