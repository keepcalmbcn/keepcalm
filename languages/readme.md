# Keep Calm Languages 🗣️

Welcome to Keep Calm Languages! A relaxed language exchange (intercambio) for English speakers in Barcelona who want to practise Spanish, Catalan, and more.

Part of the larger **Keep Calm** not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona, we're built on values of inclusivity, reliability, and collaboration. Everyone is welcome, and the community belongs to all of us.

## How It Works

- Meetups are posted in the WhatsApp group with the venue, day, and time
- We gather at a bar or cafe and pair up or form small groups
- Spend a set time in one language, then switch to another
- Everyone gets to practise the language they are learning and help others with theirs
- No teachers, no lessons, no pressure, just real conversation

## What to Expect

- A friendly, low-pressure atmosphere
- A mix of languages, usually Spanish, Catalan, and English, plus whatever else people bring
- All levels together, from complete beginners to near-fluent speakers
- Patience and encouragement - everyone is here to learn
- A drink and a chat, not a classroom

## Who It's For

Everyone. This is one of the easiest groups to join because there is no skill bar at all:

- **Learning Spanish or Catalan?** Perfect, this is exactly what it is for
- **Native or fluent English speaker?** Great, you have something to offer in exchange
- **Speak another language?** Even better, bring it along
- You do not need to be confident, "good," or a native of anything

A reminder on our wider community: Keep Calm runs in English so that everyone can take part, but this group is the one place we actively celebrate practising other languages too.

## Cost

- **Free to join.** You just pay for your own drinks or food at the venue
- No membership fee, no charge for the exchange itself

## Tips for a Great Exchange

- **Just start talking** - the whole point is to make mistakes and learn
- **Be patient** - give others time to find their words, as you will want the same
- **Split the time fairly** - make sure both languages get a fair share
- **Correct gently** - offer the right word kindly, do not turn it into a lesson
- **Mix it up** - move around and talk to different people through the evening
- **Bring a notebook or phone** if you like jotting down new words
