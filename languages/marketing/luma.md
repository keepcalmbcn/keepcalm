# Luma Event Description - Keep Calm Languages

---

**Relaxed language exchange (intercambio) in Barcelona - Spanish, Catalan, and more**

A low-pressure language exchange over a drink at a bar or cafe. Pair up, swap languages, switch after a set time. No teachers, no lessons, no grades.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Meetups posted in the WhatsApp group
- 📍 A bar or cafe in Barcelona
- 💶 Free, just pay for your own drinks or food
- 👥 All levels, complete beginners to near-fluent, 18+

**How to join**

Join our Languages WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/luma to get notified when the next meetup is announced.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
