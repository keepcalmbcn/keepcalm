Living in Barcelona is the perfect excuse to finally improve your Spanish, and the best way to do it is by actually talking. Keep Calm Languages is a relaxed language exchange (intercambio) where you can practise Spanish, Catalan, and more over a drink.

Here's how it works: we meet at a bar or cafe, pair up, swap languages, and switch after a set time. No teachers, no pressure, no grades, just real conversation. All levels welcome, from complete beginner to near-fluent.

Part of Keep Calm and Carry On - a not-for-profit English-speaking community in Barcelona, run entirely by volunteers.

Link in bio to join the Languages group and see the next meetup. 🗣️
