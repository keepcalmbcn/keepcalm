# WhatsApp Post - Keep Calm Languages

---

🗣️ *Keep Calm Languages - come and practise with us!*

A relaxed language exchange (intercambio) for English speakers in Barcelona who want to practise Spanish, Catalan, and more. Living here is the perfect excuse to finally improve your Spanish, and the best way to do it is by actually talking.

*What to expect:*
- Meet at a bar or cafe, pair up, swap languages, switch after a set time
- No teachers, no pressure, no grades, just real conversation
- All levels welcome, from complete beginner to near-fluent
- Cost: free, you just buy your own drinks 🍹

Part of Keep Calm and Carry On, a not-for-profit English-speaking community in Barcelona.

👉 Join the Languages WhatsApp group at *keepcalm.fit* to see the next meetup announcement!

18+ only.
