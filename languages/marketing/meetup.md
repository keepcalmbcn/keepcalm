# Meetup.com Event Description - Keep Calm Languages

---

**Relaxed language exchange (intercambio) in Barcelona - Spanish, Catalan, and more**

Join Keep Calm Languages for a low-pressure language exchange over a drink. We meet at a bar or cafe, pair up or form small groups, and swap languages. Spend a set time speaking one language, then switch to another. Everyone practises what they're learning and helps others with theirs.

**What to expect**

- Format: pair up, swap languages, switch after a set time
- Languages: usually Spanish, Catalan, and English, plus whatever else people bring
- Levels: all welcome together, from complete beginners to near-fluent speakers
- Atmosphere: friendly and patient, no teachers, no lessons, no grades
- A drink and a chat, not a classroom

**Cost**

Free - you just pay for your own drinks or food at the venue.

**What to bring**

Yourself and a willingness to make mistakes. A notebook or phone is handy if you like jotting down new words.

**How to join**

Meetups are posted in our Languages WhatsApp group with the venue, day, and time. Turn up at the venue and look for the group. Join the WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/meetup to get notified when the next meetup is announced.

**A note on languages**

The wider Keep Calm community runs in English so everyone can take part, but this group is the one place we actively celebrate practising other languages too.

**About Keep Calm**

Keep Calm and Carry On is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona. Adults only (18+).
