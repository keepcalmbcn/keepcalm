# Facebook Post - Keep Calm Languages

---

If you've been living in Barcelona and meaning to actually improve your Spanish (or Catalan), this might be the nudge you need.

Keep Calm Languages is a relaxed language exchange run by Keep Calm, a not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona. We meet at a bar or cafe, pair up or form small groups, and swap languages. Spend a while speaking one language, then switch to another. Everyone gets to practise what they're learning and help others with theirs.

The whole thing is built around being low-pressure. There are no teachers, no lessons, and no grades. It's just conversation and good company, with people being patient and encouraging while everyone finds their words.

All levels are welcome, from complete beginners who can barely string a sentence together to near-fluent speakers polishing the last few rough edges. You don't need to be a native English speaker, and you don't need to "be good" at anything to come along. If you speak another language entirely, even better, bring it with you.

Cost is nothing. The exchange itself is free, you just pay for your own drinks or food at the venue.

A quick note: the wider Keep Calm community runs in English so everyone can take part, but this group is the one place where practising other languages is actively celebrated.

We're a volunteer-run community with no commercial interest, just people who want to learn and meet others in good company.

Head to keepcalm.fit to find the Languages WhatsApp group and see what's coming up next. Open to adults (18+).
