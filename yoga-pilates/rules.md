# Keep Calm Yoga & Pilates - Group Rules

Welcome to Keep Calm Yoga & Pilates! To keep things enjoyable for everyone, please follow these guidelines.

> **These rules add to — but don't replace — our community-wide policies. Please read these first if you haven't already:**
> - [WhatsApp Rules](https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/whatsapp-rules.md) — group communication, unsolicited DMs, referral links, spam
> - [Community Conduct](https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/community-conduct.md) — respect, harassment, advertising, conflict resolution
> - [Event Rules](https://github.com/keepcalmbcn/keepcalm/blob/main/keep-calm/rules/event-rules.md) — commitment, cancellations, the strike system

## 1. Attendance

- If you sign up for a session, make the effort to show up
- If there's a cost involved, your spot could mean someone else misses out
- If your plans change, let the group know so someone else can take your place

## 2. During Sessions

- Arrive on time - turning up late disrupts the class for everyone
- Follow the instructor's guidance
- Keep noise to a minimum during the session
- Be mindful of the space around you

## 3. Equipment

- Bring your own mat if you have one
- If mats are provided, look after them and clean up after yourself
- Bring water and a towel

## 4. All Levels Welcome

- Don't worry if you've never done yoga or pilates before
- Listen to your body and don't push beyond what's comfortable
- Be supportive of others regardless of their experience

## 5. Be Sound

- Keep the atmosphere calm and positive
