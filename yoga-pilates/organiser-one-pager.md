# Yoga & Pilates & Meditation Organiser - One-Pager

**The role in one sentence:** you plan sessions and find the venues (or instructors) - nothing here is automated, so the announcements are yours too.

*You're a volunteer, same as everyone else who helps Keep Calm run - this page is guardrails so we're roughly on the same page, not a checklist you're marked against. We're all doing this to grow the community together and meet great people along the way.*

## Regular rhythm

Sessions happen whenever a venue, instructor, or the weather lines up - there's no fixed weekly slot:

- **A few days before**: settle the format (yoga, pilates, or meditation), the venue, and whether it's outdoors or indoors for the season
- **Post the announcement**: time, location, cost (if any), and what to bring
- **Session day**: be there a few minutes early, welcome people, and either lead the session yourself or introduce whoever is

## Handled automatically (you do nothing)

- Nothing session-specific is automated - announcements, polls, and reminders are yours

## Yours to do

- **Plan the session**: format, venue (indoors or out, depending on the season), and who's leading
- **Post the announcement** with time, location, cost, and what to bring (mat, water, towel)
- **Manage sign-ups**: a poll or a simple headcount, whichever suits the venue's capacity
- **Answer questions** in the group: level, what to expect, logistics
- **Welcome newcomers**: reassure complete beginners that all levels practise together
- **Day-of calls**: weather (for outdoor sessions), venue issues, timing

## You decide without asking anyone

- Format, venue, and timing for a given session
- Whether a session runs outdoors or indoors
- Go/no-go weather calls for outdoor sessions

## Decide together with other organisers

- A recurring paid venue or instructor arrangement
- Changing the general cadence (e.g. moving to a fixed weekly slot)
- Partnerships with studios or instructors

## Escalate to the Community Lead only for

- Money beyond the above
- Bans and appeals
- Anything technical: forms, automation, the website

## Handover

Stepping down? Follow the Stepping Down checklist in the [Organiser Guide](../keep-calm/governance/organiser-guide.md#stepping-down), and hand over any venue or instructor contacts.
