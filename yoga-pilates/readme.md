## What We're Planning

- Regular yoga and pilates sessions
- Various styles and levels
- Outdoor sessions (beaches, parks) and/or indoor studios
- Welcoming atmosphere for all experience levels

## Getting Started

1. Join the Yoga & Pilates WhatsApp group (links at https://keepcalm.fit/)
2. Watch for session announcements
3. Show up and enjoy!
