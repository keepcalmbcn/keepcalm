# Keep Calm Yoga & Pilates & Meditation 🧘

Welcome to Keep Calm Yoga & Pilates & Meditation! Relaxed yoga, pilates, and meditation sessions for English speakers in Barcelona, for every level and every body.

Part of the larger **Keep Calm** not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona, we're built on values of inclusivity, reliability, and collaboration. Everyone is welcome, and the community belongs to all of us.

## How It Works

- Sessions are posted in the WhatsApp group with the time, location, and cost
- If you want to come, respond to the poll or message to confirm
- Spaces can be limited, especially indoors, so sign up early and only if you can commit
- Turn up a few minutes early, roll out your mat, and settle in

## What to Expect

- A welcoming, judgement-free space for all levels and all body types
- A mix of yoga and pilates styles, depending on the session and who is leading
- Sessions that focus on wellbeing, movement, and community rather than performance
- Outdoor sessions in the warmer months and indoor sessions when the weather turns
- Often a coffee or a chat together afterwards

## Where We Practise

Sessions rotate depending on the season and what is available:

- **Outdoors** - on the beach, in parks, or other open spots around the city when the weather is good
- **Indoors** - in a studio or hired space during cooler or wetter months

The exact location is posted with each session announcement.

## Styles and Levels

All experience levels practise together, and you never need to keep up with anyone else:

- **Yoga** - from gentle, slow flows to more active sessions, depending on who is leading
- **Pilates** - core, strength, and mobility work
- **Meditation** - guided beach meditation sessions, often with stretching, breathing, journaling, and time to just watch the sunset together
- **Complete beginner?** Perfect. Just let the group or the instructor know and they will look after you
- Always listen to your body and rest whenever you need to

## Cost

- Outdoor community sessions are often free or just a few euros
- Studio or instructor-led sessions may have a small cost to cover the space or teacher
- The exact cost is always posted with each session
- No Keep Calm membership fee - everything is volunteer-run and not-for-profit

## What to Bring

- **A mat** if you have one (let the group know in advance if you need to borrow one)
- **Water** to stay hydrated
- **Comfortable clothing** you can move and stretch in
- **A towel** and a layer for the end of the session (it can get chilly when you cool down, especially outdoors)
- For beach or park sessions, maybe sunscreen and a hat

## Getting Started

1. Join the Yoga & Pilates & Meditation WhatsApp group (links at https://keepcalm.fit/)
2. Watch for session announcements
3. Confirm your spot via the poll or message
4. Bring a mat, water, and comfy clothes
5. Turn up, breathe, and enjoy
