# Media Ideas for Yoga & Pilates Marketing

Photos and visuals that work well for promoting this activity.

## Best Visual Angles

**Outdoor Barcelona settings**
- Yoga on the beach (Barceloneta, Bogatell) at sunrise or golden hour
- Pilates or stretching in a park (Ciutadella, Montjuic) with greenery in the background
- Poses with the Barcelona skyline or sea in the background
- Groups practicing outdoors with natural light

**Community feel**
- Small groups of people of mixed ages and abilities
- People laughing or chatting before or after a session
- Inclusive shots - not just advanced poses, show beginners too
- Flat-lay of mats, water bottles, and towels on the beach or grass

**Studio / indoor**
- Cosy, well-lit studio spaces
- Candles or soft lighting for ambience
- Close-ups of hands, feet, and posture details

## Style Notes

- Natural, unposed photography works better than glossy stock imagery
- Show real people from the community, not models
- Bright, warm tones perform well on Instagram
- Avoid anything that looks intimidating or elite - this is for all levels
- Sunrise and golden hour shots of Barcelona are always eye-catching

## Formats Needed

- Square (1:1) and portrait (4:5) crops for Instagram
- Landscape for Facebook headers and Meetup banners
- Short video clips of flows or group warmups for Stories / Reels
