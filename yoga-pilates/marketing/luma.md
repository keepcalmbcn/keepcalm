# Luma Event Description - Yoga & Pilates & Meditation

---

**English Speaking Yoga, Pilates and Meditation - All Levels**

Relaxed sessions with an English-speaking group, at a mix of outdoor locations (beaches and parks) and indoor studios depending on the season. Yoga flows, pilates fundamentals, or guided meditation, no pressure, no performance.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Sessions announced in the WhatsApp group
- 📍 Mix of outdoor (beach/park) and indoor studio locations
- 💶 Varies per session, confirmed in each announcement
- 👥 All levels, complete beginners welcome, 18+

**How to join**

RSVP here, then join our WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/luma/yoga - session time, location, format, and cost are posted there.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
