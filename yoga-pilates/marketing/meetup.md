# Meetup.com Event Description - Yoga & Pilates

---

**Keep Calm Yoga & Pilates - All Levels Welcome**

Keep Calm and Carry On is a not-for-profit sports community for English speakers in Barcelona, and we're bringing yoga and pilates into the mix.

Sessions will be held at a mix of outdoor locations (beaches and parks around Barcelona) and indoor studios. Each session announcement will include the specific location, time, format, and any cost involved.

**What to expect**

A relaxed, friendly session with an English-speaking group. No pressure, no performance - just movement and a good atmosphere. Sessions may include yoga flows, pilates fundamentals, or a combination depending on the session.

**Level**

Complete beginners are very welcome. You do not need any prior experience.

**Cost**

Varies per session and will be confirmed in each event listing. Keep Calm is not-for-profit, so any cost goes directly towards the instructor or venue.

**What to bring**

Bring your own mat if you have one. Water and a small towel are recommended. We'll share any additional requirements with each session.

**How to join**

RSVP on Meetup, or join the Keep Calm Yoga & Pilates WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit for session updates and community chat.
