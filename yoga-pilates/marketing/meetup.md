# Meetup.com Event Description - Yoga & Pilates & Meditation

---

**English Speaking Yoga, Pilates and Meditation - All Levels**

https://keepcalm.fit/meetup/yoga

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit sports community for English speakers in Barcelona, and yoga, pilates, and meditation are part of the mix.

Sessions are held at a mix of outdoor locations (beaches and parks around Barcelona) and indoor studios, depending on the season and who is leading.

**What to expect**

A relaxed, friendly session with an English-speaking group. No pressure, no performance - just movement, breath, and a good atmosphere. Sessions may include yoga flows, pilates fundamentals, guided beach meditation, or a combination, depending on the session.

**Level**

Complete beginners are very welcome. You do not need any prior experience.

**Cost**

Varies per session and will be confirmed in each announcement. Keep Calm is not-for-profit, so any cost goes directly towards the instructor or venue.

**What to bring**

Bring your own mat or towel if you have one. Water is recommended, plus a journal if you fancy it for meditation sessions. We'll share any additional requirements with each session.

**How to join**

RSVP here on Meetup, then join our WhatsApp group at https://keepcalm.fit/meetup/yoga - this is where session announcements are posted with the time, location, format, and cost, and you must be in the group before you can come along.

**About Keep Calm**

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona. Adults only (18+).
