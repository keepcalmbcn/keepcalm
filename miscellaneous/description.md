# Keep Calm General Chat - Group Description

The following is used as the WhatsApp group description.

---

Keep Calm General Chat - part of the Keep Calm and Carry On community for English speakers in Barcelona.

The main space for our community to connect, share updates, and have open conversations. Think of it as the community's living room — where everyone is welcome to join in, stay up to date, and feel part of the group.

Also the home for activities that don't yet have their own dedicated group — things like golf, climbing, boxing, padel surf/board, cycling, and much more. Once there's enough interest, we'll spin off a separate group.

Friendly discussions, quick questions, casual chats, and anything that doesn't quite fit into the more specific topic groups — it all belongs here.
