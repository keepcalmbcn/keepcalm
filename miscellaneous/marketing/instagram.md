Not everything fits in a box - and that's exactly why we love it. 🎯

Keep Calm Barcelona runs one-off activities alongside our regular sports: golf, climbing, cycling, boxing, padel surf, and whatever else the community wants to try next. No fixed schedule, just good people up for something a bit different.

Part of Keep Calm and Carry On - a not-for-profit sports and social community for English speakers in Barcelona. All levels welcome, everything volunteer-run.

Got an idea for something we should do? We're listening. Details and upcoming events in the link in bio 👆
