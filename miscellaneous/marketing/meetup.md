# Meetup.com Event Description - Keep Calm Miscellaneous Activities

---

**Keep Calm Barcelona - One-Off Activity**

Join us for one of our ad hoc events as part of Keep Calm and Carry On, the not-for-profit sports and social community for English speakers in Barcelona.

**What to expect**

This event is part of our miscellaneous activities programme - one-off sessions for activities that the community wants to try. The specific activity will be listed in the event title and details. These events are a great way to try something new with a friendly, mixed group of people.

**Who it's for**

All levels welcome. Keep Calm events are designed for people who want to enjoy an activity in good company, not compete. Whether it's your first time trying something or you've done it for years, you'll fit right in.

**Cost**

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit community run entirely by volunteers. You pay only for the activity itself - any venue, hire, or entry cost will be listed in the event details. There's no membership fee or additional charge.

**How to join**

Sign up here on Meetup, and join the Keep Calm WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit to stay updated on future events and suggest activities you'd like to see. You'll need to be 18 or over to take part.

See you there! 🙌
