# Luma Event Description - Keep Calm Miscellaneous Activities

---

**Keep Calm Barcelona - One-Off Activity**

A one-off session as part of Keep Calm's miscellaneous activities programme, activities the community wants to try. The specific activity is listed in the event title. All levels, come to enjoy it in good company, not to compete.

**The essentials**

- 📅 One-off, per event
- 📍 Venue listed in the event details
- 💶 Pay only for the activity itself, no membership fee
- 👥 All levels welcome, 18+

**How to join**

RSVP here, then join the Keep Calm WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/luma to stay updated on future events and suggest activities you'd like to see.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
