# WhatsApp Post - Keep Calm Miscellaneous Activities

---

🎯 *Keep Calm Barcelona - there's always something going on!*

Alongside our regular sports (padel, volleyball, hiking, and more), we also organise one-off activities based on what the community fancies - golf, climbing, cycling, boxing, padel surf, and whatever else people suggest.

No fixed schedule, no commitment - just keep an eye out and join when something catches your eye 👀

*What it costs:* just the activity itself. Keep Calm is not-for-profit and volunteer-run.

Part of *Keep Calm and Carry On* - the English-speaking sports and social community in Barcelona. All levels welcome. 18+.

👉 Find us at *keepcalm.fit* or search *Keep Calm Barcelona* on Instagram and Facebook.
