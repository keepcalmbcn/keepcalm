# Keep Calm Miscellaneous Activities

Welcome to the Keep Calm miscellaneous activities page — a home for activities that don't yet have their own dedicated group, but may in the future.

Part of the larger **Keep Calm and Carry On** not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona, we're built on values of inclusivity, reliability, and collaboration. Everyone is welcome, and the community belongs to all of us.

## What This Is

These are activities that community members have expressed interest in, but that don't yet have a regular session or dedicated WhatsApp group. They may grow into full Keep Calm activities in the future.

## Interested in Starting Something?

If you'd like to help organise a new activity through Keep Calm:

1. Post in the main **Keep Calm WhatsApp group**
2. Express your interest and describe the activity
3. Share ideas for how it could work (venue, format, frequency, cost)
4. Help us find others who are interested

We're always open to growing the community in new directions — we just need people willing to help make it happen!
