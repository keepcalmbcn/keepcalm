# Friday Badminton Organisation

The Friday session runs in partnership with **BCN Badminton**. They book the courts at La Mar Bella, run the games, provide an on-site host, and handle RSVPs and payment through Meetup. The Keep Calm organiser's job is to keep the community informed and to make sure our members fill the spots.

---

## How the Partnership Works

- **BCN Badminton** books the courts, sets the price, runs the games on the day, and manages the Meetup RSVPs and waitlist.
- **Keep Calm** promotes the session to the community, shares the Meetup link in the WhatsApp group, and keeps an eye on numbers.
- Players **RSVP and pay through BCN Badminton** - Keep Calm does not handle bookings or money for the badminton session.

This is the same model we use for Friday Football with Vaya Sports. The partner owns the logistics and we drive attendance.

---

## Weekly Timeline

### Early in the week - Confirm the session
- Check that BCN Badminton has posted the Friday session on Meetup (time, courts, price).
- Grab the Meetup event link.

### Mid-week - Post in the group
- Post the Meetup event link in the **Keep Calm Badminton WhatsApp group**.
- Include the key details: Friday 16:00 to 18:30, La Mar Bella, doubles, lower-intermediate, 5€, and a reminder to bring a racket, indoor shoes, and exact cash.
- Pin the message so latecomers can find it.

### Through the week - Keep it filled
- Keep an eye on the RSVP count and waitlist on Meetup.
- If spots are open, give it a nudge in the group.
- If someone needs to drop out, remind them to **change their RSVP on Meetup** early so a waitlisted player moves up (within 24 hours counts as a no-show).

### Friday - Match day
- Send a reminder in the group on the morning of the session.
- Players arrive at La Mar Bella before the 16:00 start with racket, indoor shoes, and exact cash.
- The **BCN Badminton host** (Mel) organises the courts and games and collects payment.
- After the session: post a recap or group photo in the WhatsApp group, and suggest a drink nearby on the Poblenou seafront if people are up for it. Invite people to share their own photos/videos and tag @keepcalmbarcelona (#keepcalmbarcelona) on Instagram so we can repost.

## Notes for the Keep Calm Organiser

- The BCN Badminton host is the **point of contact for anything on the day** (courts, games, payment). Direct player questions about the session itself to them.
- Keep Calm's [community rules](../rules.md) still apply on top of BCN Badminton's RSVP and conduct rules - conduct, the strike system for repeated no-shows, and the 18+ requirement.
- If the partnership details change (price, courts, time, level, host), update [Venue](friday-badminton-venues.md) and the [FAQ](friday-badminton-faq.md) so the docs stay accurate.
- The Meetup waitlist is automatic, so the main thing to watch is members cancelling late (within 24 hours), which leaves spots unfilled. Encourage early RSVP changes.
