🏸 *Fancy a game of badminton?* 🏸

We're a friendly, sociable group that plays badminton every Friday, and we'd love more people to join us! 🙌

🕓 *When:* Fridays, 16:00-18:30
📍 *Where:* La Mar Bella sports complex, Poblenou (indoor, so we play rain or shine, easy to reach on the tram)
💶 *Cost:* Just €5 per person (covers courts and shuttlecocks)
🏸 *Format:* Friendly doubles across 4 courts, players rotate so everyone gets plenty of court time

✅ *All levels welcome* - currently a relaxed lower-intermediate level, social and friendly, not a competition
✅ Bring your own racket, indoor court shoes and water
✅ Mixed group, ladies and gents, all ages
