# Luma Event Description - Keep Calm Badminton

---

**English Speaking Badminton - All Levels**

Social badminton for English speakers in Barcelona, run in partnership with BCN Badminton. Friendly doubles, rotating across 4 courts so everyone gets plenty of court time. Social, not competitive.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Weekly session, day and time confirmed in the WhatsApp group
- 📍 Barcelona badminton court (with BCN Badminton)
- 💶 5€ per person (court + shuttlecocks), cash, Bizum, or transfer
- 👥 Lower-intermediate mixed group, all ages, 18+

**How to join**

RSVP here, then join our badminton WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/luma/badminton - that is where day, time, and venue are shared. Limited to 24 players with an automatic waitlist, so only RSVP if you can make it.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
