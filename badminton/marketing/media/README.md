# Media Guide - Keep Calm Badminton

Photos and visuals that work well for promoting the badminton sessions.

## Best Shots

- **Action on court** - players mid-rally, a smash or a dive, the shuttle in flight
- **Group shots after the session** - smiling, relaxed, rackets in hand. Authenticity over polish
- **Doubles in play** - shows the social, partner-based format
- **Mixed ability and mixed group** - ladies and gents, different levels playing together
- **The venue** - the sports hall, and the Poblenou / Mar Bella beachfront nearby for atmosphere
- **Close-ups of rackets, shuttles, and shoes** - good filler content between event posts

## What to Avoid

- Overly posed or staged photos
- Shots where faces are unclear (ask permission before posting anyone clearly identifiable)
- Empty courts with no people
- Blurry indoor shots - sports halls can be dim, so steady the camera or use a faster shutter

## Format Notes

- Landscape works well for Facebook and Meetup
- Square or portrait for Instagram
- Short video clips (15-30 seconds) of rallies and smashes perform well on Instagram Reels
- Bright, natural light preferred where possible - avoid heavy filters
