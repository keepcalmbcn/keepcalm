# Meetup.com Event Description - Keep Calm Badminton

---

Social badminton for English speakers in Barcelona. Part of the Keep Calm and Carry On community - a not-for-profit, volunteer-run sports and social network for the city's English-speaking community, run in partnership with BCN Badminton.

**What to expect**

Friendly doubles practice games at La Mar Bella sports complex in Poblenou, every Friday afternoon. Players rotate across 4 courts so everyone gets plenty of court time. This is social badminton, not a competition - good-natured games with good people.

**Skill level**

Currently a lower-intermediate level. Mixed group, ladies and gents, all ages welcome. Be honest about your level when you RSVP so the games stay enjoyable for everyone.

**When and where**

Friday, 16:00 to 18:30 (2.5 hours) at La Mar Bella Municipal Sports Complex, Av. del Litoral, 86-96, Barcelona. Courts in the main building.

**Cost**

5€ per person, which covers the court hire and shuttlecocks, paid to the host on the day. Bring the exact amount in cash (no change available), or pay by Bizum or transfer.

**What to bring**

Your own racket, indoor court shoes with non-marking soles, and water.

**Spots and waitlist**

Limited to 24 players. There's an automatic waitlist - if it's full, RSVP anyway and you'll be moved up if someone drops out. Please only RSVP yes if you can make it, and cancel as early as possible if your plans change. Changing your RSVP within 24 hours of the event counts as a no-show unless someone takes your spot.

**Safety**

Participation is voluntary and at your own risk. The host facilitates the event only and is not responsible for participants' wellbeing or belongings. Please warm up before and stretch afterwards.

Adults only (18+).
