🏸 Social badminton for English speakers in Barcelona - mixed, friendly, all welcome.

Keep Calm Badminton is part of our not-for-profit sports community. We've teamed up with BCN Badminton to run a weekly Friday session at La Mar Bella in Poblenou, right by the beach. Doubles practice games, players rotating, currently a lower-intermediate level. No pressure, just good games and good people.

Indoor courts so we play rain or shine. Just 5€ per person, RSVP through Meetup. Bring your own racket and indoor court shoes.

Adults (18+). Find the link in bio to join the group and see what else we run.
