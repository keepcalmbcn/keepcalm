# Keep Calm Ping Pong <�

Welcome to Thursday Ping Pong! Our weekly table tennis sessions at the outdoor tables in Sants are a fun, free way to stay active and meet people.

Part of the larger **Keep Calm and Carry On** not-for-profit community for English speakers in Barcelona, we're built on values of inclusivity, reliability, and collaboration. Everyone is welcome, and the community belongs to all of us.

## Weekly Schedule

**Every Thursday**
- **Time**: 18:00 - 21:00 (approximately 2-3 hours)
- **Location**: Sants outdoor tables - [Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Y7rTeKDC61sYLTvh9)
- **Capacity**: 20 players (5 tables, 4 players per table)
- **Weather dependent**: Cancelled if raining

## How It Works

### Before Thursday
- **Poll posted**: Usually Tuesday/Wednesday
- **Vote for your arrival time**: 18:00, 18:30, or 19:00
  - This helps avoid people waiting around
  - Helps organizers know how many to expect

### On Thursday
- **Arrive at your selected time**
- **Find the group** at the outdoor tables
- **Mix and play**: We rotate players and mix up games
- **Stay as long as you like**: People arrive and leave throughout the session

## What to Bring

- **Water** - Stay hydrated
- **Racket** - If you have one (we have spares!)
- **Good vibes** - Most important!

**Don't have equipment?** No problem!
- We have spare rackets available
- Balls are provided
- Just bring yourself!

## Skill Levels

**All levels welcome!**

From complete beginners to experienced players - we mix up games to accommodate everyone.

- Never played before? Perfect time to learn!
- Recreational player? Great practice!
- More experienced? Help teach others!

## Organizer

The organiser coordinates the Thursday sessions and keeps things running smoothly. Check the WhatsApp group for who to contact.

## Getting Started

### If You're New
1. Join the Ping Pong WhatsApp group (links at https://keepcalm.fit/)
2. Watch for the weekly poll (usually posted Tuesday/Wednesday)
3. Vote for your arrival time
4. Show up on Thursday and look for the group
5. Introduce yourself - everyone is friendly!

### What to Expect
- **Casual atmosphere** - not competitive, just fun
- **Rotating games** - you'll play with different people
- **All abilities mixed** - helps everyone improve
- **Social vibes** - lots of chat between games

## Tips for Thursday Ping Pong

- **Vote accurately** - arrive at the time you vote for so people aren't waiting
- **Bring water** - can get warm playing outdoors
- **Comfortable clothing** - sportswear or casual active wear
- **Check weather** - if it looks rainy, check WhatsApp for updates
