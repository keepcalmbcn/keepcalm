# Ping Pong Tables in Barcelona

Known public table tennis spots around the city, compiled from player recommendations.

---

## Primary Venue

### Sants (Weekly Thursday meetup)
Our regular home. Three main tables with space to use nearby overflow tables when we have big numbers.
- **Location**: https://maps.app.goo.gl/Y7rTeKDC61sYLTvh9
- **Tables**: 3 main + nearby overflow
- **Notes**: Outdoor. Well-lit for evening play. Near Sants station.

---

## Alternative Outdoor Venues

### Glòries Park
Popular central spot. Often busy with regulars but worth trying, especially on weekday evenings.
- **Location**: https://maps.app.goo.gl/EYqc9AEKjKfn8Pd79
- **Tables**: Several
- **Notes**: Can be busy with advanced players. Nearby smaller parks tend to be quieter.

### Parc Central del Poblenou
Newer tables in the Poblenou area, good for players on that side of the city.
- **Location**: https://maps.app.goo.gl/ctTR89F1QpBrxk4G8
- **Tables**: 2
- **Notes**: Reported to be less busy than Glòries.

### Parc de la Ciutadella
Central park with tables. Popular and can be busy.
- **Tables**: Multiple
- **Notes**: Worth trying earlier in the day or on weekday afternoons.

### Turó Park / near Diagonal
Used by some regulars for informal sessions.
- **Notes**: Quieter, good for smaller groups.

### Verdaguer
Two tables, mentioned as a convenient central option.
- **Tables**: 2

### Barceloneta Beach Square
Tables near the beach — good for a casual session with a post-game walk.

### Estació del Nord area
Tables in the park next to the station, reported to often be free.
- **Location**: https://maps.app.goo.gl/6nADqsay1MhzU3PM9

## Indoor Venues

### Estació del Nord (Estacio Nord)
Indoor tables inside the building.
- **Tables**: ~8
- **Notes**: Unclear booking process — may need to enquire at the office inside.

### CNAB Club
Sports club with indoor ping pong tables.
- **Cost**: ~€16 entry (day pass, includes full facilities minus spa)
- **Notes**: Non-members can pay the entry fee. Good quality tables.

### BCN Ping Pong
Dedicated ping pong venue, reported as nice and affordable. Booking via WhatsApp.
- **Website**: https://www.bcnpingpong.com/
- **Notes**: A bit outside the centre but worth it for a proper session.

### Casals / Civic Centres
Several civic centres across the city rent out tables by the hour.
- **Cost**: ~€3.50/hour
- **Known locations**: Glòries (in the train station), Sants, Poblenou
- **Notes**: Most people don't know about this option — worth exploring further.

### Bar near Plaza España
A bar close to Plaza España with a ping pong table — good backup for windy or rainy evenings.
- **Notes**: Useful when outdoor play isn't possible.
