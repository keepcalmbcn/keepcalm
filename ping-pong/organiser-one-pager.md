# Ping Pong Organiser - One-Pager

**The role in one sentence:** you are the human at the tables on Thursday - the posts and the weekly poll run themselves, you show up, set the tone, and make the rain call.

## Weekly rhythm

Sessions run Thursday evenings at the Sants outdoor tables:

- **Thursday from 18:00**: be at the tables, greet arrivals, keep games rotating
- **Thursday (weather watch)**: if rain threatens, make the call and post it - no update means it's on
- **Through the week**: answer questions in the group as they come up

## Handled automatically (you do nothing)

- Info/reminder post on Tuesday, day-of post on Thursday
- Post-session thank-you on Thursday evening
- Next week's start-time poll on Friday

## Yours to do

- **Run the session**: rotate players so nobody hogs a table, mix abilities per the [group rules](rules.md)
- **Bring the kit**: spare rackets and balls (or coordinate who does)
- **Make the rain call**: outdoor tables are unplayable wet - post a clear cancellation when needed
- **Answer questions** in the group: location, timing, equipment
- **Welcome newcomers**: hand them a spare racket, get them into a game straight away
- **Apply strikes** only for real patterns (it's a drop-in session, so be generous)

## You decide without asking anyone

- Session format, table allocation, and rotations on the day
- Timing tweaks within the usual evening window
- The rain call
- Strikes 1 and 2 for genuine repeat offenders

## Decide together with other organisers

- An indoor venue for winter or rainy days, or anything with a recurring fee
- Moving or adding a weekly session
- Partnerships that touch ping pong

## Escalate to the Community Lead only for

- Money beyond the above
- Bans and appeals
- Anything technical: polls, automation, the website

## Handover

Stepping down? Follow the Stepping Down checklist in the [Organiser Guide](../keep-calm/governance/organiser-guide.md#stepping-down), and pass on the spare rackets.
