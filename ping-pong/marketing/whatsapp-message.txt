🏓 *Fancy a game of table tennis?* 🏓

We're a friendly, sociable group that plays ping pong every Thursday evening at the outdoor tables in Sants, and we'd love more people to join us! 🙌

🕕 *When:* Thursdays, from 18:00 (drop in any time, stay as long as you like)
📍 *Where:* Sants outdoor tables
💶 *Cost:* Free! Public outdoor tables

✅ *All levels welcome* - from complete beginners to seasoned players, we rotate and mix up the games so everyone gets a play
✅ Got a bat? Bring it! No bat? No problem, we've got spares and balls
✅ Just bring water, comfy clothes and good vibes

It's a super casual, social session and a great way to stay active and meet people.

If you'd like to come along, or just have a question, *send me a message* and I'll get you involved! 🏓
