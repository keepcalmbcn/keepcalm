# Luma Event Description - Ping Pong

---

**English Speaking Table Tennis and Ping Pong - All Levels**

Casual, social table tennis at outdoor public tables. Not a training session or tournament - players rotate throughout the evening so you'll meet everyone.

**The essentials**

- 📅 Weekly, arrival time voted via a poll in the group
- 📍 Outdoor public tables in Barcelona
- 💶 Free
- 👥 All levels, spare rackets available, 18+

**How to join**

RSVP here, then join our WhatsApp group at keepcalm.fit/luma/ping-pong - day, time, and location are shared there, plus the poll to vote for your arrival time.

Keep Calm is a not-for-profit, volunteer-run community for English speakers in Barcelona.
