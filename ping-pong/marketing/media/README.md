# Media Notes - Ping Pong

Photos and visuals that work well for promoting Thursday Ping Pong.

## Best Shot Types

- **Action at the tables** - mid-rally shots, paddle in motion, ball in the air. The outdoor setting in Sants is distinctive and worth showing.
- **Groups between games** - people chatting, laughing, standing around the table. Shows the social side more than the sport itself.
- **Mixed ability moments** - someone teaching a new player, a beginner getting a high five. Communicates the "all levels welcome" message without needing words.
- **The venue itself** - a wide shot of the outdoor tables in the evening light, ideally with people around them. Helps newcomers recognise the spot.
- **Candid smiles** - not posed group shots. Natural reactions mid-game work far better.
- **Evening atmosphere** - the tables are well-lit, so evening shots have a nice quality. Shows this is an after-work activity.

## Things to Avoid

- Overly competitive or intense expressions - this isn't a tournament.
- Empty tables with no people.
- Blurry or dark shots that don't show faces or action clearly.

## Notes

- Always get consent before posting photos of people.
- Landscape format works for Facebook and Meetup; square or portrait for Instagram.
- Short video clips (10-20 seconds) of rallies perform well on Instagram Reels.
