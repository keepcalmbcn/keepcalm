🏓 *Thursday Ping Pong in Barcelona - all welcome!*

Every Thursday from 6pm at the outdoor tables in Sants. It's free, casual, and a brilliant way to meet people.

No experience needed - we have spare rackets and people are really friendly with beginners. Whether you haven't picked up a bat in 20 years or you play regularly, you'll fit right in.

Part of *Keep Calm and Carry On* - a not-for-profit English-speaking community in Barcelona.

📍 Sants outdoor tables
🕕 Every Thursday from 18:00
💰 Free
🎉 All levels welcome

Join the group and look out for the weekly poll to pick your arrival time 👉 *keepcalm.fit*

See you Thursday! 🏓
