# Meetup.com Event Description - Ping Pong

---

**English Speaking Table Tennis and Ping Pong - All Levels**

https://keepcalm.fit/meetup/ping-pong

Join us for a casual, social game of table tennis at outdoor public tables. This is a relaxed, friendly session - not a training session or a tournament. We rotate players throughout the evening so you'll get to meet everyone, and games are kept light and fun.

**What to expect**

- Arrive at your voted time and find the group at the tables
- Play rotating games with a mix of abilities
- Stay for as long as you like - people come and go throughout the evening
- Lots of chat, a good atmosphere, and usually a drink somewhere nearby after

**Skill level**

All levels welcome. Complete beginners play alongside regulars and it works just fine. Nobody is here to win a trophy.

**Equipment**

Spare rackets are available if you don't have your own. Balls provided.

**Cost**

Free. Public outdoor tables.

**How to join**

RSVP here on Meetup, then join our WhatsApp group at https://keepcalm.fit/meetup/ping-pong - this is where all the organisation happens, and you must be in the group before you can come to the event. Day, time, and location are shared there, and a poll is posted so you can vote for your arrival time, so read the pinned info or ask the group if you're not sure.

Part of Keep Calm, a not-for-profit English-speaking sports community in Barcelona.

Adults only (18+). Follow us on Instagram at instagram.com/keepcalmbarcelona and like us on Facebook at facebook.com/keepcalmbarcelona to stay up to date with everything we do.
