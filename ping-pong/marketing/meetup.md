# Meetup.com Event Description - Ping Pong

---

**Thursday Ping Pong - Keep Calm and Carry On**

Join us every Thursday evening for a casual, social game of table tennis at the outdoor public tables in Sants. This is a relaxed, friendly session - not a training session or a tournament. We rotate players throughout the evening so you'll get to meet everyone, and games are kept light and fun.

**What to expect**

- Arrive at your voted time (18:00, 18:30 or 19:00) and find the group at the tables
- Play rotating games with a mix of abilities
- Stay for as long as you like - people come and go throughout the evening
- Lots of chat, a good atmosphere, and usually a drink somewhere nearby after

**Skill level**

All levels welcome. Complete beginners play alongside regulars and it works just fine. Nobody is here to win a trophy.

**Equipment**

Spare rackets are available if you don't have your own. Balls provided.

**Cost**

Free. Public outdoor tables.

**How to join**

Head to keepcalm.fit/meetup to find the WhatsApp group. A poll is posted midweek - vote for your arrival time so the organiser has a rough headcount.

Part of Keep Calm and Carry On, a not-for-profit English-speaking sports community in Barcelona.
